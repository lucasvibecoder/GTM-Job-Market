# Role Archetypes

Living document. Updated by `/analyze` as patterns emerge.

## outbound-builder
**Outbound Infrastructure Builder**

Owns the signal-to-meeting pipeline. Detects buying signals, enriches contacts, generates personalized outreach, routes qualified leads. Lives in Clay + email tools. Success = meetings booked, pipeline generated.

**Typical tools**: Clay, Smartlead/Instantly/Lemlist, Apollo, Common Room, LinkedIn Sales Navigator, HubSpot
**Typical titles**: GTM Engineer, Senior GTM Engineer, Growth Marketing Lead, Lead GTM Engineer
**Typical YOE**: 1-3
**Market share**: 18/61 JDs (30%)
**Salary median**: $145,000
**Key differentiator from growth-engineer**: Doesn't write code. Builds with no-code/low-code GTM tools. Campaigns over experiments.

## growth-engineer
**Growth / Technical GTM Engineer**

Writes code to build internal tools, run experiments, and automate GTM workflows. Full-stack capable. Thinks in hypotheses and metrics. Builds the infrastructure that GTM runs on.

**Typical tools**: Python, SQL, JavaScript, n8n/Make/Zapier, APIs/webhooks, Salesforce, HubSpot, Clay
**Typical titles**: GTM Engineer, Growth Engineer, Founding GTM Engineer, Data Engineer - GTM
**Typical YOE**: 2-5
**Market share**: 11/61 JDs (18%)
**Salary median**: $167,500
**Key differentiator from outbound-builder**: Writes production code. Experiment-driven. Measures everything.

## revops-ops
**RevOps / Marketing Operations**

Owns CRM and marketing automation platform. Builds workflows, lead routing, scoring, attribution models, pipeline reporting. Keeps the system of record clean and useful.

**Typical tools**: Salesforce/HubSpot, Marketo, Segment, Looker/Tableau, Sigma, Clay, Gong, Workato
**Typical titles**: Revenue Operations GTM Engineer, Marketing Operations Manager, Sr. Lifecycle Marketing Manager, Business Development Operations Senior Manager, GTM Engineer (GTM Ops)
**Typical YOE**: 3-6
**Market share**: 7/61 JDs (11%)
**Salary median**: $133,750
**Key differentiator from outbound-builder**: CRM-centric, not outreach-centric. Reporting and routing over campaigns.

## gtm-systems-architect
**GTM Systems Architect**

Designs and owns the full GTM tech stack. Builds AI agents, automation workflows, and data pipelines that span the entire revenue org. Strategic + hands-on. Often a leadership/senior IC role.

**Typical tools**: Salesforce, Clay, HubSpot, n8n, Gong, Zapier, AI/LLM APIs, internal tools, data platforms
**Typical titles**: GTM Engineer, GTM Engineering Lead, Senior Manager GTM Engineer, Platform Engineer (GTM), Senior Go-To-Market Engineer
**Typical YOE**: 3-7+
**Market share**: 20/61 JDs (33%) — largest cluster
**Salary median**: $162,700
**Key differentiator from growth-engineer**: Scope is the whole stack, not individual experiments. Architect + operator. Coding required in most roles (SQL/Python/JS).

## ai-strategist
**AI / Automation Strategist**

Consulting-flavored role. Runs strategy audits, builds automation for clients, manages change. Bridges business strategy and technical execution. External-facing.

**Typical tools**: Salesforce, Claude/OpenAI API/Gemini, Clay, Gong, Zapier, Make, n8n, low-code platforms
**Typical titles**: AI Strategist, GTM Consultant, GTM Engineer, Senior GTM Engineer / AI GTM Architect
**Typical YOE**: 3-7+
**Market share**: 5/61 JDs (8%)
**Salary median**: Insufficient data (1 JD with salary)
**Key differentiator from gtm-systems-architect**: Client-facing or advisory, not purely internal. Strategy + change management alongside building. Heaviest tool stacks — requires broad tech literacy across AI/LLM tools.
