# Tags

## Topic Tags
Auto-assign 1-3 per JD based on relevance:

- `outbound-infrastructure` — Signal detection, enrichment, personalized outreach pipelines
- `growth-engineering` — A/B testing, conversion optimization, experiment velocity
- `revops` — CRM workflows, lead routing, scoring, attribution, pipeline reporting
- `marketing-ops` — Campaign ops, email automation, lifecycle marketing
- `gtm-systems` — Full stack architecture, AI agents, tech stack ownership
- `ai-strategy` — AI consulting, strategy audits, change management
- `data-engineering` — SQL, warehouses, ETL, dashboards
- `sales-enablement` — Playbooks, tooling for sales teams, rep productivity
- `agentic-engineering` — Role explicitly mentions building, deploying, or owning AI agents/LLM workflows in Responsibilities or Must-Have skills. One bullet is enough. Not assigned when AI is merely a "plus" or "nice-to-have."
