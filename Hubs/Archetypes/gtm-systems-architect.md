# GTM Systems Architect

Designs and owns the full GTM tech stack. Builds AI agents, automation workflows, and data pipelines that span the entire revenue org. Strategic + hands-on. Often a leadership/senior IC role.

## At a Glance
- **Typical tools**: HubSpot/Salesforce, Clay, Gong, AI/LLM APIs, internal tools, data platforms
- **Typical titles**: GTM Engineer, GTM Engineering Lead, Senior Manager GTM Engineer, Platform Engineer
- **YOE**: 3-7+
- **Salary range**: $109,000 - $250,000 base (median $162,700)
- **Key differentiator**: Scope is the whole stack, not individual experiments. Architect + operator. Coding required.
