# Outbound Infrastructure Builder

Owns the signal-to-meeting pipeline. Detects buying signals, enriches contacts, generates personalized outreach, routes qualified leads. Lives in Clay + email tools. Success = meetings booked, pipeline generated.

## At a Glance
- **Typical tools**: Clay, Smartlead/Instantly/Lemlist, Apollo, Common Room, LinkedIn Sales Navigator
- **Typical titles**: GTM Engineer, Senior GTM Engineer, Growth Marketing Lead
- **YOE**: 1-3
- **Salary range**: $100,000 - $200,000 base (median $145,000)
- **Key differentiator**: Doesn't write code. Builds with no-code/low-code GTM tools. Campaigns over experiments.
