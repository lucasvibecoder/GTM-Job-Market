# RevOps / Marketing Operations

Owns CRM and marketing automation platform. Builds workflows, lead routing, scoring, attribution models, pipeline reporting. Keeps the system of record clean and useful.

## At a Glance
- **Typical tools**: Salesforce/HubSpot, Marketo, Segment, Looker/Tableau, Sigma
- **Typical titles**: Revenue Operations GTM Engineer, Marketing Operations Manager, Sr. Lifecycle Marketing Manager, Business Development Operations Senior Manager
- **YOE**: 3-6
- **Salary range**: $115,000 - $205,000 base (median $133,750)
- **Key differentiator**: CRM-centric, not outreach-centric. Reporting and routing over campaigns.
