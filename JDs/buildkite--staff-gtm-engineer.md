## Summary
Buildkite is a CI/CD platform used by high-growth tech companies (Airbnb, Shopify, Canva, PagerDuty, Lyft, Pinterest) to ship software faster. The Staff GTM Engineer builds the orchestration engine powering go-to-market — data foundations, agentic tooling, and workflows that supercharge Sales and Brand teams end-to-end. This is a senior IC role combining engineering depth with commercial fluency, designing signal taxonomies, stage plays, and compounding operational loops.

## Responsibilities
- Map Buildkite's GTM and brand experience end-to-end
- Build a signal taxonomy across the full customer journey with actionable triggers at each stage
- Establish and protect a clean, enforceable data foundation
- Develop a coherent operating system without tool sprawl
- Design agentic workflows that accelerate human work without removing human judgment
- Implement guardrails and quality controls so agentic output stays trustworthy
- Devise and ship stage plays across all stages of the customer journey
- Make plays measurable and iterative — learn fast, harden what works
- Diagnose funnel friction and ship fixes side-by-side with Sales and Brand
- Operate compounding loops: foundation, signal, play, quality, and adoption
- Document decisions, risks, and tradeoffs like an engineering team
- Create standards and reusable components for scalable GTM execution

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- Engineering background — shipped and operated production systems
- GTM engineering experience — built systems that power revenue teams
- Experience sequencing and scripting AI-led tooling (Metaflow, Clay, Customer.io, reo.dev or adjacent)
- Sales fluency — practical knowledge of how sales works, process gap identification
- Marketing fluency — brand consistency, tailored enrichment, demand capture
- Agentic workflow maturity — built and scaled AI-led workflows
- Systems thinking under constraints
- Commercial judgment — lateral thinking on funnel issues, ROI focus
- Clear written communication
- Comfort working async in a high-trust environment

### Nice-to-Have
- SQL and lightweight scripting (TypeScript/Python) for integrations and workflow glue
- CRM/data architecture experience at scale
- Familiarity with intent/ABM systems, deliverability and suppression discipline
- Developer tools / technical GTM motions experience

## Key Concepts
- **Signal taxonomy**: A structured classification of buyer signals across the customer journey, defining which signals are actionable at each stage. The foundational data model this role builds.
- **Stage plays**: Predefined, measurable GTM motions designed for specific funnel stages — prototyped, shipped, measured, and iterated.
- **Agentic workflows**: AI-powered automation that accelerates human work while maintaining human oversight and judgment. A core technical competency for this role.
