## Summary
ListLab is a funded, remote-first startup in private alpha led by a serial technical founder with a 20-person team. The Lead GTM Engineer builds and runs multi-channel outbound infrastructure — email, LinkedIn, Facebook/social DMs — owning the full pipeline from first touch to qualified handoff. Flexible engagement (full-time, fractional, or side-gig). Salary ceiling of $350K total comp (cash + equity) in Tier 1 cities. Company product not described in the JD — company_type classified as sales-tech based on company name inference; may need verification.

## Responsibilities
- Build and run the outreach machine — systems that move leads from first touch to qualified handoff
- Orchestrate multi-channel sequences across email, LinkedIn, and Facebook/social DMs at scale
- Connect the stack — integrate CRM, outreach tools, enrichment, automation, and analytics
- Own email infrastructure — domain warmup, deliverability, list hygiene, and sequence iteration
- Run LinkedIn and Facebook automation — configure tools, avoid platform violations, vary copy with AI
- Track and report — build dashboards, measure by channel, identify bottlenecks, iterate fast
- Document and systematize — create playbooks so the process scales beyond one person
- Optionally coordinate vendors or specialists as the motion matures

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- Hands-on tactical execution — building and running systems, in the tools daily
- Multi-channel outbound experience — email, LinkedIn, or social DMs at scale
- AI exposure for personalization, automation, or copy variation
- Willingness to learn, test, and try — fast iteration in days not weeks
- Technical proficiency with the GTM stack (Apollo, Clay, Instantly, Lemlist, etc.)
- Systems thinking — seeing the whole outreach pipeline and connecting the pieces

### Nice-to-Have
- Experience leading or coordinating GTM operators, SDRs, or vendors
- Venture-backed startup experience

## Key Concepts
- **Multi-channel outbound infrastructure**: Coordinated outreach across email, LinkedIn, and social DMs, unified under one owner with consistent reporting and deliverability management.
- **Domain warmup**: The process of gradually building sender reputation on new email domains to ensure deliverability at scale. Critical infrastructure this role owns.
