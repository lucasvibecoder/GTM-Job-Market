## Summary
Plain is a B2B customer support platform used by companies like Cursor, Ashby, and Sanity to unify customer interactions and supercharge workflows with AI. The GTM Engineer is one of the first hires on a new GTM team being built from scratch — owns pipeline generation across signal-based outbound, LLM-powered content (SEO/GEO), paid, and copywriting. Small team, real budget, high autonomy. Hybrid role (3 days/week in SF office).

## Responsibilities
- Build and scale repeatable signal-based outbound to generate net-new pipeline
- Create LLM-powered content that ranks highly in SEO and AI search (Generative Engine Optimization / GEO)
- Report on successes and improvements with constant iteration
- Write copy that converts — landing pages, ads, and emails
- Instrument everything to know what's working and feed that loop back into faster shipping

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- Strong sales acumen and copywriting skills (can write converting copy without AI)
- Active testing and skill development with AI and workflow automation tools (n8n, Unify, Claude Code, Clay, Instantly)
- Builder mentality — prioritize shipping this week over a perfect Q4 plan
- Comfortable with APIs, light coding, and connecting systems to unlock new GTM motions
- Obsessive experimenter — happiest when testing, iterating, and letting results guide decisions

### Nice-to-Have
- None explicitly listed

## Key Concepts
- **Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)**: Creating content optimized not just for traditional SEO but for AI search engines and LLMs. Plain's term for ensuring their content ranks in both Google and AI-powered search tools.
- **Signal-based outbound**: Outreach triggered by buying signals rather than static lists — the core pipeline generation motion this role builds and scales.
