## Summary

Clientbook is a clienteling and sales platform for retail — primarily jewelry stores and luxury brands. This GTM Engineer role owns the entire top-of-funnel revenue engine: AI-powered outbound, automated prospecting workflows, CRM architecture (Salesforce), and all integrations connecting them. Reports to Director of AI Strategy; in-office in Lehi, UT.

## Responsibilities

- Design, build, and optimize AI SDR workflows that generate qualified pipeline
- Build and manage automated outbound sequences across email and other channels
- Use Clay for data enrichment, lead scoring, and account research automation
- Stand up prospecting workflows pulling from ZoomInfo, Leadfeeder, and other data sources, routing signals into Salesforce and outbound sequences
- Own top-of-funnel metrics: pipeline generated, conversion rates, sequence performance, cost per lead
- Manage Salesforce org as system of record: user management, permissions, page layouts, fields, validation rules, Flows, and Apex
- Maintain Opportunity lifecycle, lead conversion, Closed Won handoff to CS, and custom Customer object architecture
- Build and maintain reports and dashboards for SDRs, AEs, CS, and leadership
- Own data imports, exports, cleanup, and sales data quality
- Troubleshoot integration issues between Salesforce and connected tools (ZoomInfo, Leadfeeder, PandaDoc, Calendly, AI SDR platforms, demo recording tools)
- Evaluate and implement new GTM tools (current projects: replacing sequencing software, migrating from Granola to Fathom, optimizing AI SDR stack)
- Build integrations using Zapier, n8n, APIs, and Python scripts
- Use Claude, ChatGPT, and other AI tools daily to ship faster
- Document every system and integration built

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- Hands-on experience building automated outbound/prospecting workflows (AI SDR, Clay table, or automated enrichment pipeline)
- Working knowledge of Salesforce administration
- Proficiency with automation platforms (Zapier, n8n, Make)
- Basic Python scripting
- Comfort with APIs, webhooks, and data formats (JSON, CSV)
- Experience with Clay or ability to learn complex data/automation platforms quickly
- Familiarity with AI-powered tools as actual work tools

### Nice-to-Have
- Apex experience
- B2B SaaS experience
- Salesforce Administrator certification
- AI SDR or automated outbound workflow experience

## Key Concepts

- **Clienteling**: A retail strategy where sales associates build long-term relationships with customers through personalized follow-up, outreach, and service — Clientbook's core product enables this.
- **AI SDR**: An AI-powered system that automates parts of the Sales Development Representative workflow — prospecting, outreach, and lead qualification — reducing manual effort in top-of-funnel pipeline generation.
- **Variable Compensation (Pipeline-Tied)**: Bonus structure tied to pipeline generation and system performance metrics rather than closed-won revenue — aligns incentives with the GTM Engineer's actual scope of impact.
