## Summary
Slang AI is a voice AI platform transforming customer engagement in the restaurant industry — making every phone interaction seamless and efficient. The GTM Engineer - Revenue Operations builds and optimizes systems powering sales and marketing — automating prospecting workflows, enriching lead data, building internal tools, and creating leverage for AEs and BDRs. Code-heavy role (Python, SQL, APIs) with a clear career path into Head of Growth, RevOps, or GTM leadership.

## Responsibilities
- Build and maintain automated outbound prospecting systems using Clay, Apollo, Instantly, and custom scripts
- Develop AI-powered workflows for personalized outreach at scale
- Own technical aspects of new channel launches (partnerships, paid, events)
- Enrich and score leads using APIs, web scraping, and third-party data providers
- Integrate and optimize CRM (HubSpot) with marketing automation and data sources
- Analyze funnel performance and identify bottlenecks across the GTM motion
- Create internal tools and dashboards to increase sales team productivity
- Collaborate with Sales, Marketing, and Product to ship experiments fast
- Own the data integrity and operational health of the lead-to-revenue pipeline

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 2+ years in GTM engineering, revenue ops, growth engineering, or similar technical GTM role
- Proficient in Python, SQL, and API integrations
- Hands-on experience with Clay, Apollo, Outreach, or similar sales automation tools
- Strong understanding of B2B SaaS sales funnels and lead lifecycle
- Comfortable with ambiguity; can build v1 from scratch
- Metrics-obsessed and comfortable running A/B tests on outreach campaigns
- Bias toward simplification, clarity, and leverage

### Nice-to-Have
- Restaurant tech, hospitality, or SMB sales experience
- Familiarity with AI/LLM-based personalization (GPT, Claude)
- Startup experience (sub-50 person company)
- Data engineering or analytics background

## Key Concepts
- **Lead-to-revenue pipeline**: The full data and process chain from initial lead capture through enrichment, scoring, qualification, handoff to sales, and ultimately closed revenue. Owning its "operational health" means ensuring data integrity and process reliability at every stage.
- **GTM leverage**: Using technical tools (code, automation, AI) to make a small team perform like a much larger one. The core philosophy of this role — build infrastructure that multiplies human effort rather than adding headcount.
