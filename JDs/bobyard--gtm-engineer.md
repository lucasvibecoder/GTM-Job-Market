Bobyard is a construction tech startup building AI for takeoff estimation. The GTM Engineer owns the infrastructure tying together HubSpot, Equals, Clay, and Lemlist — building systems and analyzing data that flows through them to fuel growth. Blend of technical execution, analytical rigor, and creative problem-solving.

- Design, build, and scale systems behind the go-to-market motion
