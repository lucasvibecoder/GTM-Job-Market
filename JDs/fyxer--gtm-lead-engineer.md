## Summary
Fyxer is an AI productivity product with a working PLG motion converting prosumer users into enterprise contracts — on track for $100M ARR by end of 2026. The GTM Lead Engineer will design, build, and run the entire revenue engine from scratch. Positioned as the "CTO of go-to-market" — equal parts strategist, systems builder, and operator.

## Responsibilities
- Build the full outbound stack from scratch: email domains, deliverability, sending tools, CRM workflows, enrichment pipelines
- Engineer AI-powered prospecting workflows: Clay-powered enrichment waterfalls, intent signals, lead scoring, personalized outreach at scale
- Define the ICP, source and segment target account lists
- Eliminate manual work across the full sales and CS motion — automate prep, follow-up, and note-taking
- Surface churn risk, expansion triggers, and pipeline health automatically
- Build briefings and alerts so Sales and CS always know where to focus
- Own the strategic view of the GTM tech stack — cut what's redundant, add what's missing
- In the early days, run the motion yourself; as the company scales, own the architecture

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- Technical GTM operator with deep knowledge of the modern stack — Clay, Instantly/Smartlead, Apollo, Gong, CRM automation
- Builder, not just configurer — has done this the hard way and now uses AI to do it 10x faster
- Commercial and technical in equal measure — connects data signals to revenue outcomes
- Systems thinker — when you see a manual process, your instinct is to automate it
- Able to code or script automations and work with APIs without engineering support
- 5-10 years in GTM engineering, RevOps, or outbound-focused growth at B2B SaaS

### Nice-to-Have
- None explicitly listed

## Key Concepts
- **Signal layer**: An automated system that surfaces churn risk, expansion triggers, and pipeline health without anyone requesting a report. The intelligence layer on top of raw CRM data.
- **PLG-to-enterprise conversion**: Product-led growth motion where free/prosumer users convert into paid enterprise contracts. Fyxer's existing motion that this role will systematize and accelerate.
