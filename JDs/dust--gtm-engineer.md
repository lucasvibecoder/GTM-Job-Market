## Summary
Dust is a Sequoia-backed AI operating system for companies (customers include Cursor, Clay, Whatnot). The GTM Engineer scales automated outbound prospecting systems for the US market, using Dust's own platform alongside Clay, Common Room, and Lemlist. Unique opportunity to shape an emerging discipline at an AI-first company.

## Responsibilities
- Build automated prospecting systems for US markets using GTM stack (Dust, Common Room, Clay, Lemlist, HubSpot)
- Design end-to-end workflows: identify high-intent prospects, enrich data, generate personalized outreach, route qualified leads to BDRs
- Enable Sales teams by automating repetitive tasks, developing playbooks, training on automation
- Drive measurable pipeline generation with clear targets, tracking key metrics
- Run rapid A/B experiments for messaging and targeting, scale successful ones into playbooks
- Define what world-class GTM engineering looks like

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 1-3 years in Operations or Engineering role
- Technical fluency: comfortable with SaaS tools, APIs, webhooks, automation platforms, basic scripting
- AI-forward: excited about AI transforming GTM
- Business oriented: understands sales processes, lead qualification, growth mindset
- Data-driven: measures everything, optimizes based on numbers
- Scrappy and resourceful

### Nice-to-Have
- Familiarity with Dust, Clay, Common Room, Lemlist, Default, HubSpot
- Early-stage startup or high-growth experience
- Python or JavaScript

## Key Concepts
- **AI-native GTM**: Using Dust (the company's own AI OS) to build Dust's GTM engine — dogfooding at its best.
- **Experiment-to-playbook pipeline**: Rapid A/B testing → measure → scale winners into repeatable playbooks. The core growth loop.
