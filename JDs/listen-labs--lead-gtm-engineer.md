## Summary

Listen Labs (Sequoia-backed) is an AI-powered research platform that helps teams uncover insights from customer interviews in hours instead of months. Grew from $0 to $14M ARR in under a year with enterprise wins at Google, Microsoft, Nestle, and P&G. This Lead GTM Engineer works directly with the Head of Growth to build the entire outbound and demand-gen engine — owning infrastructure, SDR enablement, tech stack, and performance analytics.

## Responsibilities

- Design and build scalable systems for prospecting, enrichment, and outreach — own the technical backbone of outbound
- Equip SDRs with world-class tooling, playbooks, and data insights to maximize efficiency and conversion
- Build ICP models, scoring frameworks, and segmentation logic for high-intent, high-fit account targeting
- Evaluate, implement, and optimize GTM tools (CRM, sequencing, enrichment, intent data) for a high-performance outbound engine
- Track, analyze, and report on outbound metrics — reply rates to pipeline contribution — and drive continuous improvement
- Work with Heads of Growth, Sales, Product, and Engineering to integrate outbound systems with broader GTM strategy

## Requirements

### Must-Have

- 5+ years in GTM operations, sales engineering, or RevOps
- Tooling expert — opinionated about best-in-class GTM tech stacks, experienced evaluating and implementing tools at scale
- Hands-on and resourceful — shift between high-level strategy and execution, from designing data models to writing SQL queries
- Highly analytical and creative — blend technical problem-solving with strategic GTM thinking
- Care deeply about craft and impact, not just output
- Curious and collaborative — energized by talking to SDRs and sales reps to understand workflows

### Nice-to-Have

- Experience building outbound motions and SDR systems
- Prior experience in technical sales, data engineering, or growth/marketing operations

## Key Concepts

- **SDR Enablement**: Building the systems, tooling, playbooks, and data insights that make Sales Development Reps more effective — not just hiring more reps, but making each rep produce more pipeline through better infrastructure.
- **ICP Scoring Framework**: A systematic model that scores and prioritizes target accounts based on firmographic, technographic, intent, and behavioral signals — ensuring outbound efforts focus on accounts most likely to convert.
