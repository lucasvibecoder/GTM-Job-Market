## Summary

Flagler Health is a fast-growing healthtech company transforming healthcare delivery through AI-powered workflow automation, remote patient engagement, and chronic care programs — serving 1.5M+ patients with a freemium model targeting the $4.5T U.S. healthcare market. This GTM Engineer role is a full-stack growth engineering position: building internal tools with AI/LLMs, running 0→1 growth experiments (SEO/AEO hacking, web scraping, AI content automation), and developing integrations across Flagler's product and business systems.

## Responsibilities

- Build internal tools and systems to scale sales and marketing through AI and automations
- Partner with stakeholders to run high-impact growth experiments (SEO/AEO hacking with LLMs, web scraping, AI-powered content automation)
- Take projects from 0→1 across a variety of surface areas and tech stacks
- Develop integrations between Flagler's products, business systems, and third-party platforms

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 2-3+ years of software engineering experience with full-stack product development
- AI/LLM experience in production environments
- Comfort working across a range of technologies and problem domains
- Modeling data flows for maximum system efficiency

### Nice-to-Have
- Experience in early-stage or growth-focused technical roles
- Familiarity with the modern data stack (React, Python, Next.js, Sanity CMS, dbt)
- Understanding of sales/marketing automation tools and B2B sales processes

## Key Concepts

- **AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)**: Optimizing content to appear in AI-generated answers (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews) rather than traditional search results — a newer growth lever alongside SEO that this role would experiment with using LLMs.
- **0→1 Projects**: Building something from nothing — no existing codebase, no template, no established process. Signals the role requires high autonomy and comfort with ambiguity over incremental feature work.
