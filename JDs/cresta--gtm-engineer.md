## Summary
Cresta is an AI platform for contact centers (Stanford AI lab origins, backed by Greylock/Sequoia/a16z, customers include Intuit, Hilton, Carmax). The GTM Engineer will design and deploy an "AI-native operating system" for the revenue engine — working directly with the CEO, CRO, and CMO to eliminate manual work, automate deal execution, and help teams prioritize the right accounts. Listed as "GTM Automation Lead" on LinkedIn; Cresta's own listing uses "GTM Engineer."

## Responsibilities
- Use AI and data to help SDR, marketing, and sales focus on highest-value accounts
- Surface hard-to-find intelligence (contact center scale, BPO footprint, tech stack)
- Continuously enrich and reprioritize accounts as signals change
- Replace manual research, opportunity prep, and CRM entry with automation and AI
- Build systems that tell sellers what to focus on each day and what actions move deals forward
- Auto-generate account plans, stakeholder maps, and discovery prep inside seller workflows
- Automate quoting, approvals, and document generation using AI agents
- Streamline order forms, security reviews, and deal documents
- Create real-time deal signals and next steps for every opportunity
- Write custom code and use AI coding tools to connect and extend the GTM stack
- Integrate and maximize Salesforce, Gong, Clay, Centralize, Slack, Glean, Coda
- Rethink segmentation, territories, and coverage using data and AI

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- Engineering ability with real GTM understanding
- Entrepreneur mindset with strong ownership and bias to build
- Strong understanding of enterprise GTM and sales workflows
- Comfortable writing code, using APIs, automation, data systems, and AI tools
- Deep familiarity with modern sales tech stacks
- High ownership, speed, and focus on real revenue outcomes

### Nice-to-Have
- None explicitly listed

## Key Concepts
- **AI-native operating system for revenue**: A connected layer of AI agents, automations, and data enrichment replacing manual research, CRM entry, and deal prep — letting sellers focus on relationships and decision-making.
- **Account intelligence surfacing**: Using AI and data to uncover hard-to-find signals (contact center scale, BPO footprint, tech stack) that help sellers prioritize and personalize their approach to high-value accounts.
