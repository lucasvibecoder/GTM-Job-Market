## Summary

Axial is a profitable, founder-led M&A platform connecting buyers and sellers of $5M-$100M private companies (4,500+ acquirers, 3,000 advisors, $1B+ annual transaction volume, 60 employees, NYC). This GTM Engineer sits at the intersection of BizOps, GTM, and Product — designing and operating AI-powered systems across the member lifecycle. Initial focus: intelligent workflows for member qualification, automated support resolution, agentic SDR systems, and AI-driven chat. Hybrid in Manhattan (Tue/Wed/Thu).

## Responsibilities

- Design, build, and operate AI-driven agentic systems powering core GTM and member workflows — deploy production-grade tooling and own reliability and performance
- Build and maintain scalable workflow orchestration using n8n, Clay, Zapier, Google Apps Script, and custom code
- Design and build custom internal tools using Superblocks that integrate with core systems and increase operational leverage
- Design and maintain data foundations required for AI-driven and automated workflows — accurate, structured, accessible GTM data
- Configure workflows, manage integrations, troubleshoot issues, and maintain stability within Axial's GTM tech stack
- Lead exploratory projects on new tools, architectures, and automation opportunities — prototype quickly, convert to production
- Partner with BizOps, GTM, Product, and Engineering to translate real-world workflows into technical solutions

## Requirements

### Must-Have

- 3-5 years in BizOps, Engineering, RevOps, SalesOps, GTM Engineering at a high-growth tech company or startup
- Hands-on workflow automation experience with n8n, Clay, Zapier, or Google Apps Script
- Building internal tools and applications for operational and GTM workflows
- Strong Salesforce knowledge and related GTM systems (HubSpot, Outreach, Gong) beyond basic administration
- Code writing in JavaScript, SQL, Python, or Apps Script for automation and systems integration
- Comfortable with APIs, webhooks, and structured data
- Familiarity with AI-assisted development tools
- Operational data comfort for reporting and decision-making

### Nice-to-Have

- None listed

## Key Concepts

- **Agentic SDR Systems**: AI-powered systems that proactively identify and engage the right prospects without human initiation — moving beyond simple sequences into autonomous outreach that qualifies, prioritizes, and personalizes based on real-time signals and member data.
- **Member Lifecycle AI**: Deploying intelligent workflows across the full member journey — from real-time qualification and verification of new members, through automated support resolution, to proactive engagement — creating operational leverage where AI handles routine decisions and humans focus on high-judgment interactions.
