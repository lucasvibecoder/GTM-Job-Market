## Summary
TalentPluto is recruiting for an undisclosed venture-backed fintech startup building a consumer trading platform in NYC. The Founding Growth role defines and executes growth strategy during a critical scaling phase — designing experiments across social, community, and referral channels to drive user acquisition. Highly hands-on and experimental, no specific tooling prescribed. 6 days/week in office.

## Responsibilities
- Design, test, and execute growth experiments across multiple channels (social platforms, community-driven channels, referral programs)
- Identify new user acquisition opportunities and evaluate performance using data and experimentation
- Collaborate closely with product, engineering, and design partners to launch and iterate on growth initiatives
- Develop clear hypotheses, run tests, and scale successful programs
- Contribute to the development of scalable growth systems and processes
- Communicate learnings, results, and recommendations clearly to the broader team

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- Demonstrated ability to execute independently and take ownership from idea to completion
- Strong written communication — ability to persuade or engage audiences succinctly
- Experience with growth, marketing, or user acquisition in a fast-paced environment
- Comfort working in an onsite, highly collaborative startup setting
- Analytical mindset with willingness to experiment, learn, and iterate
- High professional standards and strong interpersonal skills

### Nice-to-Have
- Experience with social or community-driven growth channels
- Familiarity with performance marketing, experimentation frameworks, or automation tools
- Background in consumer, fintech, or high-velocity digital products

## Key Concepts
- **Founding Growth role**: First dedicated growth hire at a scaling startup — owns the strategy and execution of user acquisition channels from scratch. More ambiguity and ownership than joining an established growth team.
- **Community-driven growth**: Acquiring users through organic community channels (social platforms, referral programs, user communities) rather than paid acquisition or outbound sales. The primary growth lever for this consumer fintech product.
