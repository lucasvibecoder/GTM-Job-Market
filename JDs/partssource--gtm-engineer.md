## Summary
PartsSource is a healthcare equipment procurement platform trusted by 5,000+ US hospitals and 15,000 clinical sites (PE-backed by Bain Capital since 2021, 700+ employees). The GTM Engineer owns the full commercial tech stack across Marketing, Sales, and CS — architecting systems, enrichment/signal workflows, and AI-enabled operations to drive measurable revenue impact.

## Responsibilities
- Own end-to-end GTM stack design across CRM, marketing automation, analytics, and CS systems
- Evaluate tools for adoption, ROI, cost efficiency, and risk reduction
- Identify redundancies, gaps, and technical debt; lead consolidation or replacement initiatives
- Establish documentation, standards, and scalable system architectures
- Design and implement enrichment and signal workflows across firmographic, technographic, and intent data
- Build CRM-ready objects and actionable segments using orchestration tools
- Define data quality standards, governance models, and ROI measurement frameworks
- Identify and deploy AI use cases across attribution, lead scoring, forecasting, and next-best-action workflows
- Implement LLM-powered automation and agentic workflows to improve GTM productivity
- Establish AI governance guardrails for data access, privacy, and compliance
- Lead GTM initiatives from requirements through rollout, training, and adoption measurement
- Build dashboards and reporting for funnel performance, lifecycle metrics, and tool effectiveness

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 4+ years in GTM systems, revenue operations, sales/marketing operations, or business systems
- Hands-on experience integrating CRM, marketing automation, analytics, and CS platforms
- Experience with enrichment, orchestration, and intent tooling (Clay, ZoomInfo, 6sense, Demandbase, Bombora)
- Experience designing LLM orchestration, automation workflows, and AI-enabled use cases
- Strong understanding of data flows, APIs/iPaaS, attribution models, and funnel metrics

### Nice-to-Have
- Healthcare, B2B SaaS, or PE-backed growth environment experience

## Key Concepts
- **Intent data orchestration**: Using firmographic, technographic, and behavioral signals to identify in-market accounts and route them into automated workflows — the foundation of signal-driven GTM.
- **GTM technical debt**: Accumulated redundancies, unused integrations, and fragile automations across the commercial tech stack that slow execution and obscure reporting.
