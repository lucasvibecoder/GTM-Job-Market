## Summary
Traversal is an AI SRE platform for the enterprise — trusted by large companies to troubleshoot, remediate, and prevent production incidents. Team includes researchers from MIT, Harvard, Berkeley and engineers from Citadel, Datadog, Perplexity, and more. The GTM Engineer builds and scales an AI-native revenue engine — shipping AI-powered workflows across outbound, inbound, and lifecycle channels, and developing a centralized marketing AI agent. Sits within Marketing, partners with Sales, RevOps, and Engineering.

## Responsibilities
- Build and ship AI-powered GTM workflows that generate qualified sales pipeline across outbound, inbound, and lifecycle channels
- Design and maintain a centralized marketing AI agent trained on company content, competitive intel, messaging, and performance data
- Own key GTM automation systems end-to-end: prospecting workflows, enrichment, lead routing, scoring, and reporting
- Partner with Marketing, Sales, RevOps, and Engineering to identify friction in the customer journey and implement scalable solutions
- Design, test, and iterate on growth experiments with measurable feedback loops to improve conversion and pipeline efficiency
- Establish documentation, governance, and quality standards for AI and automation across the GTM organization

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 5+ years in GTM systems, growth engineering, revenue operations, marketing automation, or related technical role in B2B SaaS
- Strong hands-on experience building automation using APIs, webhooks, LLMs, and modern GTM tools (Salesforce, HubSpot, Clay, Outreach, data enrichment platforms)
- Comfort with technical systems including JSON payloads, APIs, and basic SQL
- Proven ability to translate ambiguous GTM challenges into shipped, measurable improvements
- Experience building AI-powered workflows, agents, or automation systems
- Strong systems thinking and ability to design scalable, repeatable infrastructure
- Excellent cross-functional collaboration, especially with engineering teams

### Nice-to-Have
- Experience building or deploying AI agents in production environments
- Familiarity with observability, developer tools, or technical B2B audiences
- Experience training LLM workflows on internal knowledge bases or structured datasets
- Experience supporting enterprise sales motions
- Prior experience as Sales Engineer, Forward Deployed Engineer, or Account Executive
- High-growth, early-stage startup experience

## Key Concepts
- **Centralized marketing AI agent**: An internal AI system trained on company content, messaging, competitive intel, and performance data to accelerate content creation and campaign execution. A unique differentiator of this role — not just using AI tools but building a custom AI agent for the marketing org.
- **AI-native revenue engine**: A GTM system where AI is foundational rather than bolted on — AI-powered workflows drive pipeline generation, content creation, and campaign execution end-to-end. Reflects the company's identity as an AI agent lab extending into its own GTM.
