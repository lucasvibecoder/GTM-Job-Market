## Summary
Luxury Presence is a leading digital platform for real estate agents ($52.6M raised, Bessemer, 15K+ businesses, Inc. 5000 three years running). The Sr. Lifecycle Marketing Manager owns email channel across cold outreach, newsletters, onboarding, and product updates. High-impact IC role at the intersection of growth and lifecycle — not a traditional GTM Engineer title but heavily GTME-adjacent.

## Responsibilities
- Plan, build, and send email campaigns across cold outreach, newsletters, onboarding, product updates
- Intake, prioritize, and execute requests from multiple teams with speed and precision
- Develop workflows, campaign calendars, and internal documentation for repeatable execution
- Define internal standards for prospect and client email engagement
- Use HubSpot as primary execution platform, integrate Clay, Apollo, Instantly where appropriate
- Run A/B tests on subject lines, copy, send times, visuals
- Craft campaigns balancing conversion performance with brand standards
- Maintain clean contact database, own segmentation and targeting

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- HubSpot mastery: email execution, automation, segmentation, reporting
- AI and modern email/outreach tools (Clay, Apollo, Instantly)
- A/B testing, deliverability, open rate optimization, performance metrics
- Strong copywriting instincts and visual taste
- 4+ years in email marketing or growth marketing (high-growth tech/SaaS)
- Cold outreach workflow design
- Large database management and CRM hygiene
- Cross-functional collaboration with sales, marketing, success, and design

### Nice-to-Have
- None explicitly listed

## Key Concepts
- **Lifecycle marketing + cold outreach hybrid**: Unusual combination — owns both nurture/retention AND cold outbound email. Most roles split these into separate positions.
- **Email as growth channel**: This role treats email as the primary growth lever, not just a communication channel. Performance metrics, A/B testing, and conversion optimization are core.
