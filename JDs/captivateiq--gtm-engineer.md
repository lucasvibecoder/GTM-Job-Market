## Summary
CaptivateIQ builds sales performance management software (incentive compensation, sales planning) used by Netflix, Figma, and Stripe. Backed by Sequoia, ICONIQ, Accel, and Sapphire Ventures. The GTM Engineer operates as a builder at the center of the GTM stack — creating AI-powered automations, copilots, and agents while designing data pipelines that unify data across the full tool ecosystem. Hybrid in Austin, TX; remote from Raleigh, Nashville, or Toronto.

## Responsibilities
- Map GTM bottlenecks: partner with RevOps, Sales, Marketing, and CS to identify where AI can reduce manual effort, increase speed, or improve targeting
- Create AI-powered automations, copilots, and agents to support GTM teams
- Design and maintain scalable data pipelines that unify data across HubSpot, Salesforce, Outreach, Gong, ChurnZero, etc.
- Implement systems that measure campaign, channel, and program effectiveness
- Identify and design automated workflows to support internal operations
- Innovate and experiment — pilot new tools, integrations, and AI workflows

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 5+ years as a Marketing Analyst or technical role in Marketing Operations/RevOps
- Comfortable writing code (Python/SQL) and working with APIs
- Deep knowledge of Salesforce Architecture
- Fluency in GTM tools (Clay, HubSpot/Marketo, Outreach/Apollo, Gong, Nooks/Orum, 6sense, UserGems) and automation platforms (Zapier, Workato)
- Scrappy builder who thrives in ambiguity, enjoys automating manual work, and owns projects end-to-end
- Translate business problems into elegant, maintainable system solutions
- Strong attention to detail and high standards for data quality and process consistency
- Proven ability to design and scale processes across global marketing teams

### Nice-to-Have
- Background in data engineering or ETL tools

## Key Concepts
- **AI copilot**: An AI-powered assistant embedded in GTM workflows that helps reps act on signals, automate repetitive tasks, and make faster decisions.
- **Data pipeline unification**: Connecting data across multiple GTM tools (CRM, sequencing, conversation intelligence, customer success) into a single, consistent layer for attribution and lifecycle reporting.
