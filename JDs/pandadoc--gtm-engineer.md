## Summary

PandaDoc is a document workflow automation platform (proposals, contracts, quotes, payments, eSignatures) used by 60,000+ organizations. The RevTech team owns Salesforce as the central nervous system for the entire revenue engine. This GTM Engineer role is **Salesforce-first** — you own and evolve Salesforce as the backbone of GTM operations, build AI-powered workflows for routing/scoring/enrichment, and integrate across the broader GTM stack (Clay, ZoomInfo, LeanData, Chili Piper, SalesLoft, Outreach). Blends hands-on Salesforce admin/dev with systems design and experimentation.

## Responsibilities

- Own Salesforce as the primary GTM platform: data architecture, automation, permissions, workflows
- Design and maintain complex Salesforce automations using Flows, validation rules, and programmatic solutions
- Build AI-powered workflows for lead and account routing, scoring, enrichment, personalization, and prioritization — anchored in Salesforce
- Integrate Salesforce with PandaDoc and the broader GTM stack (Clay, ZoomInfo, LeanData, Chili Piper, Actively AI, SalesLoft, Outreach)
- Turn ambiguous business problems into clear Salesforce system designs, experiments, and implementation plans

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 5+ years Salesforce-first GTM systems experience — owned Salesforce orgs or major functional areas
- Strong Salesforce administration and development skills (data models, Flows, permissions, reports/dashboards)
- GTM systems and toolchain fluency — understands how Salesforce orchestrates LeanData, Chili Piper, Actively AI, SalesLoft, Outreach, enrichment providers, and marketing platforms
- AI-aware systems thinking — how AI/automation improves routing, scoring, forecasting, personalization, and GTM efficiency
- Clear communication and cross-functional collaboration with Sales, Marketing, CS, Finance, and Engineering

### Nice-to-Have
- Apex, Lightning components, or advanced Salesforce customization
- SQL fluency and REST API experience
- Salesforce certifications (Administrator, Advanced Administrator, Platform App Builder, Developer)
- Complex routing and attribution logic at scale
- Experience supporting high-growth B2B SaaS GTM teams

## Key Concepts

- **RevTech Team**: PandaDoc's internal team that owns the entire GTM tech stack with Salesforce as the orchestration layer — a signal that companies are building dedicated "revenue technology" functions rather than splitting GTM systems across Sales Ops and Marketing Ops.
- **Salesforce-First GTM Architecture**: Using Salesforce as the source of truth and central orchestration layer rather than a parallel tool like Clay or n8n — all routing, scoring, enrichment, and automation flows through Salesforce natively.
- **LeanData**: A Salesforce-native lead-to-account matching and routing platform — automates which rep gets which lead based on territory, segment, and custom logic.
