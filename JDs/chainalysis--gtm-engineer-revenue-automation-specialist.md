## Summary

Chainalysis provides blockchain intelligence and analytics used by businesses and governments for compliance, investigations, and safe blockchain navigation. This GTM Engineer - Revenue Automation Specialist sits within the Demand Generation team, owning the technical build-out of growth workflows — AI-powered outbound campaigns via Clay and nReach, self-service PLG experiences, and integrated engagement paths that reduce friction and accelerate pipeline creation.

## Responsibilities

- Design and maintain product tours, gated demo environments, and PLG workflows to enhance conversion and accelerate buyer education
- Implement AI-powered outbound programs using Clay and nReach to automate account research, data enrichment, and personalized outreach sequences
- Partner with Marketing Operations and Revenue Systems to connect CRM, marketing automation, and enrichment tools for seamless campaign workflows from first touch to opportunity creation
- Analyze user journeys across ads, landing pages, chatbots, and demos to identify drop-offs and optimize conversion rates
- Collaborate with sales, product, product marketing, and brand/content marketing to translate campaign strategies into scalable workflows
- Prototype new processes, measure results, and refine approaches to boost speed-to-lead, lead quality, and pipeline efficiency

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 3–5 years in growth engineering, technical marketing, marketing ops, or revenue operations with measurable results
- Hands-on experience with Clay, Zapier or n8n, GPT/LLM APIs, and CRM platforms (Salesforce or HubSpot)
- Ability to blend no-code, low-code, and light scripting to automate and scale marketing workflows
- Track record of turning marketing strategies into scalable systems

### Nice-to-Have
- PLG or B2B SaaS environment experience
- Familiarity with CDPs (Segment, Hightouch) and analytics tools (Looker, Tableau)
- Marketing attribution, lead scoring, or conversion funnel design experience
- API or light scripting proficiency (Python, JavaScript)

## Key Concepts

- **Product-Led Growth (PLG)**: A go-to-market strategy where the product itself drives user acquisition, conversion, and expansion — users experience value before talking to sales.
- **Speed-to-Lead**: The elapsed time between a prospect showing interest and receiving a response — faster speeds correlate with higher conversion rates.
- **Data Enrichment**: Augmenting raw lead data with additional attributes (firmographic, technographic, intent signals) to improve targeting and personalization.
