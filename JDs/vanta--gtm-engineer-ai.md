## Summary

Vanta builds a Trust Management Platform that helps companies automate security compliance (SOC 2, HIPAA, ISO 27001). This GTM Engineer, AI role owns strategy and execution of AI-driven automations across the full GTM stack — building workflows and agents with Salesforce, HubSpot, Gong, Clay, Workato, n8n, and dbt/Snowflake. Direct impact on pipeline generation, win rates, renewal velocity, and rep productivity.

## Responsibilities

- Identify high-leverage opportunities for AI and automation across GTM workflows (prospecting, pipeline generation, account research, renewal prep)
- Design, build, and maintain AI-driven workflows and agents using Salesforce Flows, Workato, Python, and SQL
- Design, develop, and maintain internal-facing web applications using Claude Code and Netlify
- Partner with Sales and Post Sales leadership to build custom AI-based solutions aligned with strategic goals
- Develop technical documentation, monitoring, and testing frameworks for GTM systems and AI workflows
- Evaluate and implement new GTM and AI technologies, APIs, and tools with a build-vs-buy perspective

## Requirements

### Must-Have

- 5+ years in business systems, GTM engineering, or data/analytics engineering in high-growth B2B SaaS
- Strong knowledge of AI and automation in GTM systems (workflow automation, summarization, routing, scoring)
- Prior software engineering experience
- Salesforce proficiency (data model, Flows, integrations)
- Experience integrating GTM systems (Gong, Heap, marketing automation tools)
- Strong SQL skills
- Excellent cross-functional collaboration and communication skills
- Openness to using AI to amplify skills

### Nice-to-Have

- Experience with modern data stack tools (Snowflake, dbt)

## Key Concepts

- **Trust Management Platform**: Software that automates security compliance monitoring and reporting, enabling companies to continuously prove their security posture against standards like SOC 2, HIPAA, and ISO 27001.
- **AI-Driven GTM Workflows**: Automated processes powered by AI that handle GTM tasks like lead scoring, account research summarization, pipeline routing, and outreach personalization — replacing manual work with intelligent systems.
- **Build vs. Buy**: A decision framework for evaluating whether to develop a custom internal solution or purchase an existing vendor tool, weighing factors like cost, maintenance burden, and fit.
