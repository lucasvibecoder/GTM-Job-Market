## Summary

**Rutter** builds a Unified API powering integrations across 75+ accounting, commerce, and payments platforms for companies like PayPal, Capital One, Mercury, and Airwallex. Backed by a16z and YC. This **Lead GTM/Growth Engineer** owns the growth roadmap and builds the future Growth team. You inherit a system that generated 80% of Rutter's pipeline and scale it with AI and automation to operate like a 20+ person org. Includes team leadership (growth associate + contractors). In-person, Flatiron NYC.

## Responsibilities

- Own the growth roadmap — define and prioritize initiatives across automation, demand generation, and GTM experimentation
- Lead a small team — manage and mentor a growth associate and contractors
- Systematize and automate the GTM motion — build scalable, AI-driven workflows for prospecting, lead routing, and pipeline generation
- Run growth experiments — design, test, and iterate on acquisition and activation strategies
- Leverage modern GTM + AI stack — expand and optimize workflows using Clay, Unify, Outreach, Orum, and Salesforce
- Build data-driven feedback loops — own reporting, analytics, and insights on GTM efficiency, conversion, and ROI
- Collaborate across marketing, sales, and product to align initiatives and maximize pipeline impact

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 5+ years in growth, marketing operations, or revenue systems (ideally B2B SaaS, fintech, or developer tools)
- Built systems and teams from scratch — thrives on scaling what works
- Systems thinker who breaks complex GTM processes into structured, repeatable systems
- Data-driven — measures everything and translates metrics into meaningful action
- Hands-on with automation — design and execute automations that replace repetitive work
- Strong cross-functional collaborator with GTM, product, and leadership teams

### Nice-to-Have
- None listed

## Key Concepts

- **Unified API**: A single API layer that abstracts away the differences between dozens of third-party platforms (accounting, commerce, payments), letting developers build one integration that works across all of them instead of maintaining separate connections to each.
- **GTM Leverage Through Automation**: The philosophy of using AI and automation to make a small team (2-3 people) operate with the output of a 20+ person org — replacing headcount-dependent scaling with systems-dependent scaling.
