## Summary
Acres is a land intelligence platform analyzing 150M+ U.S. parcels for real estate, agriculture, and finance customers. The GTM Engineer owns the technical infrastructure powering the entire go-to-market motion — connecting CRM, enrichment tools, AI platforms, and outreach channels into a unified revenue engine. Builder role, not traditional sales or RevOps.

## Responsibilities
- Connect and configure CRM systems (HubSpot/Salesforce) with enrichment tools (ZoomInfo, Apollo) and AI platforms for lead enrichment and prospecting
- Build and maintain integrations across the GTM tech stack using Clay, Zapier, Make, and custom scripts
- Eliminate manual, repetitive tasks so the revenue team can focus on selling
- Create workflows to move, clean, and analyze data across the GTM tech stack
- Build lead scoring models and account prioritization logic
- Ensure data integrity and hygiene across all systems
- Deploy AI tools to generate personalized outreach at scale
- Use AI for account research, signal detection, and buying intent analysis
- Own reporting and analytics infrastructure — build dashboards and attribution models
- Serve as the technical resource for the BDR team: train reps on new tools, workflows, and prospecting best practices
- Run A/B tests on outreach strategies, sequences, and messaging
- Identify bottlenecks in the sales funnel and build technical solutions
- Create repeatable processes, templates, and playbooks for the sales org

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- Builder mentality — see broken processes and ship solutions
- Technical fluency across CRMs (HubSpot/Salesforce), automation platforms (Clay, Zapier, Make), enrichment tools (ZoomInfo, Apollo), and AI tools
- Sales acumen — understand the sales process well enough to build systems that help reps close
- Data-driven — set up experiments, measure results, explain outcomes
- Curiosity about customer businesses and new technology
- Communication — translate technical concepts to non-technical teammates

### Nice-to-Have
- Prior experience in Sales, RevOps, or Sales Engineering
- Startup and/or B2B SaaS experience
- Experience with Claude, Clay, HubSpot, Salesforce, ZoomInfo, Apollo, Gong, Titan, Zapier, Make
- Familiarity with APIs, webhooks, and basic scripting (Python, JavaScript)
- Bachelor's degree or equivalent (Business, Sales, or Tech focus)

## Key Concepts
- **Revenue engine architecture**: Connecting CRM, data enrichment, AI tools, and outreach channels into a single automated system. The core infrastructure this role builds and maintains.
- **Signal detection and buying intent**: Using AI and data tools to identify when prospects are showing purchase intent, then routing that signal into personalized outreach — the difference between relevant messaging and spam.
