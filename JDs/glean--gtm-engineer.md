## Summary

Glean (1,000+ employees, series-c+, Forbes AI 50, $4.6B+ valuation) is the Work AI platform powering enterprise search, AI assistants, and scalable AI agents. The GTM Engineer is the "technical backbone" of the go-to-market engine, sitting at the intersection of RevOps, marketing, sales, customer success, and data. Owns AI-first workflows, data pipelines, and automations across the full customer journey — from first touch through renewal. Reports to VP of Revenue Strategy & Operations. Dogfoods Glean's own product ("Leverage Glean at Glean").

## Responsibilities

- Build AI-first automation across the full customer journey to eliminate manual work in prospecting, deal execution, onboarding, adoption, and renewals.
- Systematize and automate GTM workflows — enrichment, scoring, routing, and outreach across the customer journey.
- Accelerate pipeline generation through differentiated and innovative scaled outreach.
- Set up and run data-driven GTM experiments, track impact, and scale what works.
- Leverage Glean at Glean — drive new ways for GTM org to be more AI-first across all processes.
- Identify areas of opportunity for AI tooling.
- Partner across Sales, Marketing, Post-Sales, and Product to identify leverage, run experiments, and scale successful initiatives.

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 5+ years in GTM or Revenue Operations with strong technical orientation.
- Proven track record designing and shipping GTM workflows (scoring, routing, lifecycle, PLG/usage-based signals) that moved the needle on pipeline, conversion, or retention.
- Experience with AI-native and automation tools, particularly Clay.
- Deep understanding of B2B SaaS funnels and GTM motions.
- Systems thinker with structured, process-driven approach and bias for simplification and automation.
- Comfortable working autonomously in a fast-paced environment where priorities shift.

### Nice-to-Have
- Computer Science degree.

## Key Concepts

- **Enterprise Graph / Personal Knowledge Graph**: Glean's proprietary data model that maps relationships between people, content, and activity across an organization — powers the context-aware AI responses their platform delivers.
- **AI-First GTM Operations**: An approach where AI agents and automation are the default execution layer for GTM workflows (not bolted on after the fact) — this role is explicitly tasked with making every GTM process AI-native.
- **PLG/Usage-Based Signals**: Product-Led Growth signals derived from how users interact with a product (feature adoption, usage patterns, expansion triggers) used to score and route leads alongside traditional intent data.
