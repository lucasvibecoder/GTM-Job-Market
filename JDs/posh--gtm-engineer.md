## Summary

Posh is an IRL community platform enabling anyone to build and monetize real-life communities — 7M+ users, $70M in funding, $300M+ in transactions. This GTM Engineer owns the revenue systems powering Sales, Success, and Partnerships. Combines systems administration (HubSpot + Planhat) with GTM engineering — building AI agents and automations that remove manual work from the go-to-market motion. Reports to Director of Revenue Operations. In-office NYC (SoHo).

## Responsibilities

- Serve as primary admin and technical owner for HubSpot and Planhat
- Evaluate new AI and GTM tools, make recommendations, and implement
- Manage pipelines, lifecycle logic, routing rules, fields, and data models
- Maintain clean data flows and resolve identity and sync issues
- Document systems and train GTM teams for adoption and trust
- Design, build, and own AI-driven agents that automate recurring GTM workflows
- Deliver automations that drive Sales metrics (inbound lead counts, reply rates, meeting-to-close-win rate)
- Build end-to-end automations across HubSpot, Planhat, Clay, Zapier, and APIs
- Embed intelligence into routing, enrichment, follow-ups, alerts, and handoffs
- Measure automation impact in hours saved, speed, and GTM throughput

## Requirements

### Must-Have

- 2-4 years in GTM Engineering, RevOps, SalesOps, Business Systems, or similar
- Hands-on experience managing a modern CRM and workflow tools (HubSpot, Salesforce, Zapier)
- Strong understanding of core sales funnel metrics and how to move them with automations and AI
- SQL fluency and experience connecting tools to centralized reporting infrastructure
- Strong communication to collaborate with non-technical users
- Comfort with APIs and light scripting in Python or JavaScript

### Nice-to-Have

- Experience with Clay, Gong, Default, Unity, Artisan, or similar enrichment platforms
- Experience in marketplaces or B2B2C business models
- Experience owning CRM automations and improvements
- Comfort navigating early-stage ambiguity and fast execution cycles

## Key Concepts

- **AI-Driven GTM Agents**: Autonomous workflows that handle recurring go-to-market tasks — lead routing, enrichment, follow-ups, alerts — without human intervention. The aspirational goal here is fully automated deal sourcing and closing.
- **Customer Success Platform (Planhat)**: Software that tracks customer health, engagement, and lifecycle post-sale. Paired with HubSpot (pre-sale CRM) to give full revenue visibility from first touch through retention.
