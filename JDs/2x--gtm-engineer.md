## Summary
2X (2x.marketing) is a GTM consulting agency that embeds engineers into client engagements. The GTM Engineer owns end-to-end client delivery for up to 3 accounts simultaneously — translating strategic goals into executed campaigns, managing CRM workflows, and running outbound operations across sales, marketing, and RevOps.

## Responsibilities
- Own delivery for up to 3 client engagements simultaneously
- Act as primary client contact, driving timelines, execution, and results
- Translate client strategy into actionable projects and campaigns
- Scope, manage, and track projects using Monday.com, Slack, and Clay
- Validate data accuracy, CRM updates, automation workflows, and outbound campaigns
- Maintain campaign readiness, deliverability, and system reliability
- Proactively identify opportunities, propose new initiatives, and improve workflows
- Lead issue resolution and crisis management with urgency and ownership

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- Strong client-facing communication and project leadership skills
- GTM knowledge across sales, marketing, and RevOps
- Hands-on experience with CRMs (Salesforce, HubSpot), automation tools, sequencing platforms, and Clay
- High attention to detail, organization, and bias toward action

### Nice-to-Have
- 3+ years in GTM, RevOps, consulting, or client-facing technical roles
- Experience executing outbound campaigns and GTM workflows
- Startup, agency, or fast-growth environment experience

## Key Concepts
- **GTM-as-a-service**: An agency model where GTM Engineers serve as embedded operators for client companies, running outbound campaigns, CRM workflows, and RevOps initiatives across multiple accounts in parallel.
- **Client delivery ownership**: End-to-end accountability for translating a client's strategic goals into executed GTM campaigns, managing scope, timelines, and quality across parallel engagements.
