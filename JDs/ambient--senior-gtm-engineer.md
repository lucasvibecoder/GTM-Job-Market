## Summary
Ambient.ai is a Series B AI-powered physical security platform (a16z, Y Combinator, serves 7 of top 10 US tech companies plus Fortune 500). The Senior Founding GTM Engineer is the technical architect of their AI-driven growth engine — not managing tools but building the "AI brain" for the entire go-to-market motion. Sits at the nexus of Sales, Marketing, and Data Engineering.

## Responsibilities
- Architect AI-driven GTM workflows using Clay, AirOps, and n8n for account prioritization, persona identification, personalized messaging
- Operationalize intent signals: synthesize first- and third-party signals for hyper-targeted ABM campaigns
- Write glue-code (Python/JavaScript) to ensure data flows between Salesforce, HubSpot, Snowflake, and AI toolchain
- Build prompt engineering and orchestration workflows with structured prompts and model context protocols
- Define KPIs and monitor system performance (engagement rates, prompt drift, latency)
- Document system behavior, contribute to shared prompt libraries, evangelize the AI engine to Sales and Marketing

### 90-Day Plan
- 30 Days: Map workflows, identify data silos, deliver systems audit with quick-win automations
- 60 Days: Build and test agent-level workflows in production, define messaging context boundaries
- 90 Days: Fully own AI-driven GTM infrastructure, implement observability loops, productionize agent governance

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 6+ years in Technical GTM, Growth Engineering, or RevOps Engineering (B2B SaaS)
- Scripting proficiency (Python or JavaScript) for data pipelines and system triggers
- AI-workflow automation (n8n, AirOps, Clay)
- CRM/MAP technical familiarity (Salesforce/HubSpot)
- Model context protocols, multi-agent orchestration, prompt sandboxing tools (Cursor, Lovable)
- Complex dataset synthesis → elegant technical implementations
- Builder mindset with cross-functional communication

### Nice-to-Have
- None explicitly listed

## Key Concepts
- **AI brain for GTM**: A centralized AI system that handles account prioritization, persona identification, and personalized messaging — replacing manual research and template-based outreach.
- **Prompt drift monitoring**: Tracking how AI-generated outputs change over time and degrading in quality — an emerging operational concern as AI becomes embedded in GTM workflows.
