## Summary
Decagon is a leading conversational AI platform (backed by a16z, Accel, Bain Capital, Coatue, Index Ventures) enabling enterprises like Cash App, Chime, and Oura Health to deploy AI agents across voice, chat, email, and SMS. The GTM Engineer reimagines, automates, and optimizes the full GTM motion — building custom solutions, AI agents, and data flows rather than managing SaaS configurations. Developer-minded operator role with end-to-end ownership. In-office 5 days/week (SF or NYC).

## Responsibilities
- Optimize the stack: lead technical integration and optimization of external tools (Salesforce, Clay, Gong, enrichment APIs) into a cohesive, automated engine
- Build AI agents: design and deploy agents that optimize account research, reduce manual data entry, and enhance outbound personalization
- Engineer data flows: build webhooks and middleware that sync data between product, CRM, and internal systems with 100% integrity
- Programmatic GTM: partner with leadership to translate sales strategy into automated workflows, custom internal tools, and programmatic outbound campaigns

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 3+ years in GTM Engineering or technical RevOps, comfortable writing code (Python, JS, or SQL) and working with APIs
- Builder DNA — skilled at AI tools like Clay, Claude Code, Parallel, Unify, Gumloop/n8n/Zapier, and web scraping/indexing
- GTM expertise — understanding of sales funnel and processes, business logic behind data movement
- Integration mastery — track record stitching together a GTM stack and managing complex data waterfalls
- Bias toward action — build v1 today, thrive in ambiguity of fast-paced AI startup
- End-to-end ownership of GTM infrastructure and the outcomes it produces

### Nice-to-Have
- None explicitly listed

## Key Concepts
- **Programmatic GTM**: Translating sales strategy into automated workflows and programmatic outbound campaigns rather than manual execution. The shift from human-driven to systems-driven pipeline generation.
- **Data waterfalls**: Complex multi-step data flows where information cascades through enrichment, scoring, routing, and sync layers across the GTM stack. The integration architecture this role manages end-to-end.
