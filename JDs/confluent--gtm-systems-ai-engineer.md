## Summary

Confluent is a public data streaming platform (real-time data infrastructure, built on Kafka). This GTM Systems & AI Engineer drives the shift to an AI-first GTM operating model across Sales and Customer Success — taking real workflow pain from the field and translating it into practical improvements using AI agents, automation, and better data flow. Designs and ships solutions using Relevance AI, Momentum, Clay, and LLM tooling. Acts as the internal GTM AI SME, owning end-to-end from intake through delivery and iteration.

## Responsibilities

- Own AI intake and delivery across Sales and CSG — run intake, clarify business problems, define requirements and success metrics, prioritize backlog, drive 0-to-1 through release and iteration
- Build and ship AI workflows end-to-end — map current-state processes, identify friction, design future-state workflows, implement agents and automations
- Partner cross-functionally with Sales, CS, Marketing Ops, Business Technology, Data Science, Finance, and Legal on requirements, data usage, governance, and outcomes
- Build with guardrails — embed permissioning, auditability, and compliance from day one
- Launch with lightweight enablement (training assets, office hours, comms), gather feedback, continuously improve
- Own quality and operational rigor — test plans, QA, prompt and workflow versioning, documentation, runbooks, release notes
- Track adoption and performance, validate results with Ops and Analytics, iterate based on usage and outcomes

## Requirements

### Must-Have

- 3-7+ years in Revenue Operations, GTM Systems, Sales Ops, CS Ops, Business Systems, or similar roles
- Pattern of taking ambiguous GTM problems, getting to root cause fast, and shipping improvements teams actually adopt
- Strong judgment and follow-through — surface tradeoffs early, close open loops, drive idea to rollout to iteration
- Comfort with APIs/webhooks, JSON payloads, and SQL for validation/analysis (BigQuery preferred)
- Salesforce fluency across pipeline, lifecycle, and post-sales workflows

### Nice-to-Have

- Hands-on experience with Relevance AI, Momentum, Clay, Gemini, or Claude
- Familiarity with SalesLoft, ZoomInfo, Clari, D&B, DemandTools and common Sales/CSG workflows
- Experience building AI workflows with governance patterns (access controls, auditability, feedback loops)

## Key Concepts

- **AI-First GTM Operating Model**: A strategic shift where AI agents and LLM-powered workflows become the default operating layer for Sales and Customer Success — not bolted-on tools, but the primary way teams execute pipeline generation, deal management, onboarding, and renewal.
- **Prompt and Workflow Versioning**: Treating AI prompts and automation workflows with the same rigor as software releases — version control, test plans, QA, and release notes to ensure reproducibility and safe iteration at scale.
