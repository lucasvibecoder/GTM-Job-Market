## Summary

HackerRank is a developer skills assessment platform trusted by 2,500+ companies (NVIDIA, Amazon, Microsoft) for hiring and upskilling technical talent. This Demand-focused GTM Engineer owns end-to-end execution of multi-channel demand generation programs — paid social, ABM plays, email nurture, and direct mail — turning marketing strategy into pipeline. The role sits on the Growth Marketing team and directly impacts revenue through campaign execution, budget management, and performance optimization.

## Responsibilities

- Execute and optimize full-funnel demand generation programs across paid media and owned channels.
- Lead day-to-day management of paid media, including audience targeting, creative rotation, pacing, and bid strategies.
- Drive ABM campaigns (1:Many, 1:Few, and 1:1), collaborating with SDRs, design, and content to engage key accounts.
- Run diagnostics across GTM funnel systems, report on campaign performance, and implement A/B and multivariate tests.
- Streamline campaign operations with documented playbooks and automation to reduce manual effort and increase speed.

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 5+ years in B2B demand generation or growth marketing (agency or SaaS).
- Hands-on with LinkedIn Ads and at least one additional paid platform (Meta, Reddit, etc.).
- Experience running 1:Many, 1:Few, and 1:1 ABM campaigns with measurable results.
- Fluent with CRM and marketing automation (e.g., Salesforce, HubSpot).
- Strong in reporting, attribution modeling, and funnel diagnostics (pivot tables, UTM tracking).
- Skilled at conversion tracking and syncing audience segments (Tag Manager, Analytics events).
- Familiar with low-code and automation tools such as Clay, Make, n8n, or Zapier.

### Nice-to-Have
- Experience with APIs, webhooks, dashboarding, or ETL.
- Light creative skills with tools like Figma or Canva.
- Copywriting chops to iterate messaging and improve campaign performance.

## Key Concepts

- **ABM (Account-Based Marketing)**: Targeted marketing strategy that focuses resources on specific high-value accounts using tiered approaches — 1:Many (broad), 1:Few (cluster), and 1:1 (personalized).
- **Attribution Modeling**: Framework for assigning credit to marketing touchpoints along the buyer journey, used to understand which campaigns and channels drive pipeline.
- **Multivariate Testing**: Experimentation method that tests multiple variables simultaneously (vs. A/B which tests one), used to optimize campaign elements like creative, audience, and messaging.
