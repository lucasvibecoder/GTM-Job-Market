## Summary
Syncari is an agentic master data management (MDM) platform helping enterprises build trusted, AI-ready data foundations. The GTM Systems Engineer owns the data and systems lifecycle for Syncari's internal revenue operations — integration, governance, and activation across the full GTM stack. Reports to Chief of Staff & Operations.

## Responsibilities
- Own unified data management across the GTM stack — data models, quality rules, and governance frameworks
- Build and maintain integrations using APIs and webhooks via Syncari to connect HubSpot, Gong, Warmly, Zendesk, Jira, and Amplemarket
- Design ETL/ELT pipelines and data transformations to centralize, enrich, and activate data in real-time
- Create automated workflows for lead routing, territory assignment, opportunity management, and customer lifecycle processes
- Develop dashboards and reports surfacing critical KPIs for revenue operations, pipeline health, and data quality metrics
- Monitor and optimize system performance, data integrity, and integration reliability through audits, testing, and continuous improvement
- Collaborate cross-functionally to translate business requirements into technical solutions

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 4+ years in business systems, GTM automations, or systems integration at a B2B SaaS company
- Hands-on experience administering HubSpot, Salesforce, and marketing automation tools
- Strong ETL/ELT skills for CRM and marketing data pipelines with focus on data quality and integrity
- Solid grasp of data modeling, MDM, and data governance in revenue systems
- Proficient in APIs, webhooks, REST, OAuth, and middleware tools (Syncari, Workato, Tray.io, Zapier)
- Skilled in workflow automation and scalable process design
- SQL and scripting (Python/JavaScript) for data transformation and integrations
- Experience with BI tools (Looker, Tableau, Power BI) to support GTM analytics
- Clear communicator with ability to align technical solutions to business needs

### Nice-to-Have
- None explicitly listed

## Key Concepts
- **Master Data Management (MDM)**: A discipline for ensuring data consistency, accuracy, and governance across all systems — the "single source of truth" that prevents the chaos of fragmented, contradictory data across CRM, marketing, and support tools.
- **Data governance framework**: Policies, rules, and processes that define how data is created, stored, maintained, and used. In a GTM context, this means enforcing standards for how contact, company, and deal records are structured and updated across tools.
