## Summary
Huzzle is a talent placement firm hiring a GTM Engineer on behalf of an unnamed AI-first B2B growth agency ($0 → $2M ARR in under a year). The role builds scalable RevOps systems, outbound infrastructure, and Clay-powered enrichment workflows for high-growth B2B SaaS clients. Agency-side: you work across multiple client accounts, designing ICP models, TAM maps, and multi-channel outbound campaigns.

## Responsibilities
- Recommend and implement outbound GTM plays tailored to each client's ICP and growth stage
- Advise clients on sales tech stack selection and automation tools
- Build lead allocation systems to distribute prospects across sales teams
- Build ICP models using firmographic, technographic, and behavioral signals
- Map Total Addressable Markets (TAM) and prioritize high-value accounts
- Implement signal tracking systems using first-party and third-party data sources
- Identify decision-makers and buying committee members within target accounts
- Build advanced Clay workflows for data enrichment and account research
- Implement AI-driven enrichment pipelines to automate manual research
- Create automation workflows using Clay, APIs, and workflow automation platforms
- Develop account scoring systems to prioritize high-intent prospects
- Build personalized multi-channel outbound campaigns across email and LinkedIn
- Create scalable outbound sequences while maintaining personalization
- Analyze campaign performance and iterate based on results and client feedback
- Participate in weekly client calls to discuss strategy and performance
- Contribute to internal GTM playbooks and process documentation
- Deliver performance reports and growth recommendations

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 2-4+ years in Sales, RevOps, Sales Operations, BizOps, or GTM Engineering
- Experience in B2B SaaS, startups, or agencies
- Strong working knowledge of Clay
- Curiosity about AI tools, automation, and growth experimentation
- Strong problem-solving mindset and ability to learn quickly
- Fluent English communication

### Nice-to-Have
- HubSpot or Salesforce CRM experience
- Workflow automation tools such as n8n
- Sales automation or outbound tooling experience

## Key Concepts
- **TAM mapping**: Building Total Addressable Market models using firmographic, technographic, and behavioral signals to identify and prioritize the full universe of potential accounts for a client.
- **Account scoring**: Ranking prospects by intent signals and fit criteria to focus outbound effort on accounts most likely to convert — a core Clay workflow output.
