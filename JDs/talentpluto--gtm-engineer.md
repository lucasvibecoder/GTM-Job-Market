## Summary

**TalentPluto** (recruiting firm) posting on behalf of an unnamed venture-backed B2B SaaS company building infrastructure software for engineering teams. Despite the "GTM Engineer" title, this is a **full-cycle AE role** — own prospecting through close for mid-market and enterprise. - Own the full sales cycle from prospecting through close for mid-market and enterprise customers
- Proactively source and develop pipeline in addition to working inbound opportunities
- Conduct technical discovery conversations with engineers, technical leaders, and executive stakeholders
- Collaborate with deployment engineers and product teams during evaluations and onboarding
- Help refine messaging, positioning, and early-stage sales processes
- Operate effectively in a startup environment with evolving structure and responsibilities

- 1-3 years of Account Executive experience in B2B SaaS (or equivalent senior SDR transition)

- Strong foundational understanding of modern software stacks, cloud infrastructure, or developer tools
- Ability to communicate technical concepts clearly and credibly
- Comfort operating in early-stage startup environment and wearing multiple hats

### Nice-to-Have
- None listed

## Key Concepts

- **Founder-Led Sales to Scalable Motion**: The transition point where a startup moves from founders closing deals personally to building a repeatable, process-driven sales org with dedicated AEs and defined playbooks.
- **Multi-Threaded Enterprise Sales**: Engaging multiple stakeholders across an organization simultaneously during a deal — rather than relying on a single champion — to reduce risk and accelerate consensus.
