## Summary

Lighthouse builds a commercial strategy platform for the hospitality industry — pricing tools, business intelligence, and actionable insights ($370M Series C, $100M+ ARR, 850+ employees). This GTM Engineer is the founding member of the GTM Engineering practice, owning automation across the full funnel (Lead → Opportunity → Customer → Expansion) with a core focus on Clay and Salesforce. Reports to Senior Manager of Commercial Systems with high autonomy.

## Responsibilities

- Partner with Marketing, Sales, Data, and Operations teams to identify opportunities for AI-driven solutions and technology adoption
- Implement CRM data enrichment solutions to enhance lead quality and empower Sales and Marketing with deeper prospect insights
- Design, build, and deploy AI agents and scalable automated workflows across end-to-end marketing processes
- Identify strategic opportunities to better leverage the existing sales engagement platform and implement improvements
- Create and maintain technical documentation and training materials for operational best practices
- Improve reliability, observability, and performance of GTM automations to ensure systems scale

## Requirements

### Must-Have

- 3-5 years in a GTM Engineer, Growth Engineer, or technical RevOps role at a high-growth B2B SaaS company
- Hands-on experience using Clay to optimize and automate GTM processes
- Salesforce administration and configuration proficiency
- Highly analytical and process-driven, focused on efficiency and scalability
- Proven track record applying AI or advanced automation to complex business problems
- Exceptional communication and executive presence

### Nice-to-Have

- HubSpot as a marketing automation platform
- API integrations and workflow tools (Zapier, n8n)
- Clay certification (Automated Inbound, CRM Enrichment)
- Sales engagement platforms (Groove, Clari Copilot)
- Data modeling, forecasting, or analytics

## Key Concepts

- **Quote-to-Cash Flow**: The end-to-end business process from initial quote/proposal through contract signing, delivery, and payment — spanning Sales, Finance, and Operations systems.
- **CRM Data Enrichment**: Automatically augmenting CRM records with external data (firmographics, technographics, contact info) to improve lead quality, scoring accuracy, and Sales team context.
