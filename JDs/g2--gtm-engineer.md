## Summary

G2 is the world's largest software marketplace (now merged with Capterra, SoftwareAdvice, and GetApp — 200M+ combined annual visitors, 6M verified reviews). This is G2's **first GTM AI Engineer** role — a hands-on strategist and builder who prototypes AI-powered workflows, runs pilots with SDRs/AEs/CSMs, measures ROI, and operationalizes what works. Sits at the intersection of Revenue, Marketing, and CS. **Note:** LinkedIn and Clay list this as "GTM Engineer" while the JD body uses "GTM AI Engineer."

## Responsibilities

- Map GTM bottlenecks by partnering with RevOps, Sales, Marketing, and CS to identify where AI reduces manual effort, increases speed, or improves targeting
- Prototype fast using GPT-4, Claude, Zapier, Clay, Hex, Notion, and similar tools
- Run pilots by deploying in-field with SDRs, AEs, and CSMs — shadow, iterate, and learn
- Measure ROI by quantifying hours saved, pipeline influenced, and conversion lifts
- Operationalize workflows by documenting and transitioning working flows to GTM Systems, Enablement, or Marketing Ops for scale
- Translate GTM strategy into execution by operationalizing Piper artifacts into real-time workflows
- Deploy self-serve AI tools via Slack-triggered workflows that reduce prep/admin time by 25–40%
- Automate connected workflows to improve hand-offs across Salesforce, SalesLoft, Gong, and G2 Intent
- Partner across functions to increase adoption of GTM tools by 60–80%
- Benchmark against best-in-class GTM platforms like Qualified

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 3–5+ years in RevOps, GTM strategy, or growth — preferably in B2B SaaS
- Demonstrated success deploying AI into GTM workflows (lead scoring, pipeline insights, AI summaries, outbound triggers)
- Hands-on experience with GenAI and automation tools (GPT-4, Claude, Zapier, Clay, Airtable, Notion)
- Strong data literacy across Salesforce, Snowflake, Looker, and/or equivalent platforms
- Cross-functional operator who collaborates with Sales, CS, and Marketing
- Bias for speed: quick iteration, hypothesis testing, and continuous learning

### Nice-to-Have
- B2B SaaS experience

## Key Concepts

- **Piper**: GTM planning tool used at G2 to define strategy artifacts that this role translates into executable real-time workflows bridging planning with execution in Salesforce and SalesLoft.
- **G2 Intent**: G2's proprietary buyer intent data product showing which companies are actively researching software categories — used here as a key signal source combined with engagement and web activity data.
