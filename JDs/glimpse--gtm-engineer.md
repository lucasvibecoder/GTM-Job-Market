## Summary

Glimpse builds AI agents that automate finance workflows (revenue recovery, accounting reconciliation, trade spend management) for 200+ consumer brands in food, beverage, health, and beauty. This GTM Engineer owns the full technical execution of Glimpse's GTM motion — programmatic outbound engine, revenue system optimization, data pipelines, and growth experiments — as the company scales to eight figures.

## Responsibilities

- Manage and optimize the GTM tech stack (HubSpot, Apollo, Clay) and build data pipelines between CRM, sales tools, and analytics platforms for real-time reporting and multi-attribution
- Design lead workflows that improve conversion rates across the funnel and ensure data quality, hygiene, and observability across all GTM systems
- Design and implement AI-powered outbound sequences that personalize at scale
- Own email deliverability infrastructure including domain health, warming strategies, and sender reputation monitoring
- Build automated research and trigger workflows using Clay to improve targeting and personalization
- Ship high-velocity experiments (website widgets, landing page variants, email sequences) and build internal tools for marketing and sales to launch tests without engineering support
- Instrument conversion funnels with tracking and analytics to measure performance
- Build LLM-powered workflows for prospect research, personalization, content generation, and lead qualification
- Automate manual processes across marketing, sales, and operations to create scalable compounding systems

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 2+ years in GTM, RevOps, or Growth engineering at B2B SaaS companies
- Hands-on experience with HubSpot and Apollo (or similar outbound automation platforms)
- APIs, webhooks, light scripting (Python/JavaScript), and SQL
- Comfortable vibe coding and prototyping quickly
- Track record of rapid experimentation and shipping growth initiatives with measurable results
- High agency and bias toward action

### Nice-to-Have
- Experience building automated outbound systems and email deliverability best practices
- Familiarity with HubSpot, Clay, Apollo, or other modern growth tools
- Background in CPG, Retail, or vertical SaaS

## Key Concepts

- **Programmatic Outbound**: Automated, data-driven outbound at scale — using enrichment, AI personalization, and trigger workflows to generate pipeline without manual prospecting.
- **Email Deliverability Infrastructure**: The systems and practices (domain health, warming strategies, sender reputation monitoring) that ensure outbound emails reach inboxes rather than spam folders.
- **Vibe Coding**: Rapid prototyping approach where speed and iteration trump polish — shipping functional automation quickly and refining based on results.
