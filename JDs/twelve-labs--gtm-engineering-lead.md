## Summary
Twelve Labs is a multimodal AI company ($107M raised from NVentures/NVIDIA, NEA, Radical Ventures, Index Ventures) building video understanding models. The GTM Engineering Lead designs, builds, and ships infrastructure powering GTM operations — a foundational role creating systems and internal products that drive the next stage of growth. Hands-on, builder-first, highly technical.

## Responsibilities
- Design, build, and ship HubSpot-based GTM systems: configuration, data structure, workflow automation
- Build and own Clay-native automations: enrich, score, route, orchestrate GTM data in real time
- Design automation replacing manual GTM work: routing, lifecycle management, ownership logic, enrichment, partner attribution
- Build reliable multi-system workflows across HubSpot, Clay, SalesLoft, Avoma, Crossbeam
- Create systems where accurate reporting is a byproduct of clean workflows
- Prototype quickly, ship v1 systems, iterate based on GTM performance
- Evaluate and implement new GTM tools and integrations
- Collaborate with Sales, Partnerships, and Growth leadership
- Build guardrails, QA processes, and self-healing workflows for data quality at scale

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 3+ years building GTM systems, internal automations, or revenue infrastructure (high-growth B2B)
- Strong HubSpot technical expertise (workflows, data models, integrations)
- Deep Clay experience beyond basic enrichment (scoring, routing, orchestration, signal detection)
- Sales engagement and marketing automation tools (HubSpot Marketing Hub, SalesLoft, Outreach, 6sense)
- Data analysis and visualization (Google Sheets, Looker, Metabase, SQL)
- Builder mindset: ships systems, automates manual work, iterates quickly
- Complex GTM requirements → practical, scalable technical solutions
- Cross-functional and autonomous operation in fast-paced environment

### Nice-to-Have
- None explicitly listed

## Key Concepts
- **Clay as orchestration layer**: Using Clay not just for enrichment but as the central nervous system — scoring, routing, and orchestrating data flows across the entire GTM stack.
- **Self-healing workflows**: Automated systems with built-in guardrails and QA that detect and correct data quality issues without manual intervention.
