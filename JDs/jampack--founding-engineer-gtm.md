## Summary

**Jampack** is an AI-native platform automating the wholesale order-to-cash lifecycle for CPG brands (Fishwife, Immi, Ghia, Sanzo) — processing $500M+ in annualized volume. Raised $3.2M seed from Maveron. This **Founding Engineer - GTM** architects the internal systems powering outbound strategy: automated pipelines that identify, enrich, score, and engage high-value CPG prospects. The pitch: "If you've ever used Clay, Instantly, Apollo and thought 'I could build something better and more tailored,' this role is for you."

## Responsibilities

- Build the data foundation — automated pipelines for lead enrichment, CRM hygiene, and account research with clean, deduplicated records
- Architect outbound systems — programmatic prospecting workflows that identify high-fit CPG brands, enrich with signals, and route into personalized outbound sequences
- Automate sales enablement — auto-generate prospect research, score inbound leads, draft personalized outreach, sync engagement data to CRM
- Experiment and iterate — test new channels, messaging strategies, and targeting criteria; measure, kill, and compound
- Own the GTM tech stack — evaluate, integrate, and orchestrate tools across enrichment, sequencing, CRM, and analytics; build custom connectors where off-the-shelf falls short

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 2+ years in GTM engineering, growth engineering, RevOps, or sales engineering — ideally at a B2B startup
- Hands-on experience with Clay, Apollo, Zapier, or similar GTM/automation platforms
- Technical fluency — comfortable with SQL, Python, APIs, and no-code/low-code orchestration
- Commercial mindset — pipeline, conversion rates, and deal impact over system uptime
- Deep curiosity about the CPG/wholesale space
- Scrappy and experimental — ship 80% today, iterate over perfect spec

### Nice-to-Have
- None listed

## Key Concepts

- **Order-to-Cash Lifecycle**: The full business process from receiving a purchase order through fulfillment, invoicing, and payment collection — traditionally manual in wholesale, now being automated by platforms like Jampack.
- **Programmatic Prospecting**: Using code and automation to systematically identify, enrich, and score target accounts at scale — replacing manual research with data pipelines that continuously surface high-fit prospects.
