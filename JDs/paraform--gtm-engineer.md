## Summary
Paraform is a recruiting platform for elite recruiters (mid 8-figures ARR, backed by Felicis, BOND, DST Global, works with Cursor, Palantir, Windsurf). The GTM Engineer treats GTM as an engineering and experimentation problem — discovering, testing, and scaling the highest-quality leads, channels, and motions. Decides where sales and marketing spend resources.

## Responsibilities
- Continuously identify and validate highest-quality lead sources, segments, and channels
- Design systems that surface who to contact, when, and why
- Build lead-scoring and prioritization frameworks
- Translate insights into executable inputs for sales (lists, alerts, routing logic, playbooks)
- Build robust reporting infrastructure for experiment and channel performance visibility
- Build lightweight tools, scripts, and automations to test GTM ideas quickly
- Use Clay, LinkedIn, scraping utilities, APIs, and no-code tools to enrich, prioritize, and personalize at scale
- Design and launch experimental website features for marketing acquisition
- Uplevel internal GTM tooling to production-grade applications

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- Hands-on experience with low-code AI workflow tools (Clay, n8n, Zapier)
- 3+ years in Investment Banking, GTM Engineering, Growth Ops, or Data Science
- Advanced SQL, Python, and Excel
- Ability to operate independently with minimal direction
- Clear communication to senior leadership
- In-office 5 days/week in San Francisco

### Nice-to-Have
- Bachelor's in CS, Engineering, Data Science, or related STEM field

## Key Concepts
- **GTM as engineering problem**: Treating go-to-market as a discipline requiring hypothesis generation, experimentation, and scaling — not just "doing outbound."
- **Lead intelligence systems**: Autonomous systems that surface, score, and route leads without manual intervention. The end state this role builds toward.
