## Summary
Perk (formerly TravelPerk) is a travel and spend management platform used by 10,000+ companies including Wise and Fabletics. The GTM Engineer (US) sits within the Revenue Systems team, providing local systems support and operational execution for NAM Sales. Hands-on CRM and workflow maintenance role focused on keeping outbound infrastructure running reliably during US business hours.

## Responsibilities
- Support, configure, and maintain GTM systems and workflows including Salesforce, Outreach, Gong, and Clay
- Ensure reliable system performance during NAM business hours for high-volume outbound activity
- Maintain accurate account and contact data across Salesforce and connected GTM tools
- Support TAM sourcing, enrichment, and hygiene workflows
- Partner with NAM Sales teams to achieve productivity targets including high-volume dialing and automated outbound campaigns
- Troubleshoot system or workflow issues to prevent lost selling time
- Provide on-the-ground systems support without waiting for Europe-based teams
- Enable faster iteration of GTM processes while maintaining alignment with global standards
- Serve as primary NAM contact for Revenue Systems and GTM Engineering support
- Execute against the Revenue Systems and GTM Engineering roadmap

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 5+ years in Revenue Operations, Sales Operations, or related technical roles in B2B SaaS
- Hands-on experience supporting GTM workflows, CRM tools, and revenue systems (Salesforce preferred)
- Experience with sales engagement platforms (Outreach, SalesLoft) and other revenue tools (Gong, Clay, or similar)
- Proven ability to troubleshoot systems, automate workflows, and maintain high data quality
- Strong collaboration and communication skills
- Comfortable managing competing priorities and supporting ambitious productivity targets

### Nice-to-Have
- None explicitly listed

## Key Concepts
- **Revenue Systems team**: Internal function at Perk combining data, systems, and process enablement to support revenue team performance. This role is their NAM-local execution arm.
- **NAM GTM Engineer**: North America-focused GTM Engineering role designed to provide timezone-aligned systems support, reducing dependency on European headquarters for troubleshooting and iteration.
