## Summary

TalentPluto (recruiting firm) is hiring on behalf of an early-stage AI startup building a reinforcement learning platform that helps organizations create production-grade RL environments and commercialize RL capabilities. This founding GTM Engineer owns the full outbound and demand-gen engine — from targeting and enrichment through sequencing and meeting booking. High ownership, systems-building role with room to expand into lifecycle and growth motions.

## Responsibilities

- Build and operate an end-to-end outbound engine: targeting → list building → enrichment → sequencing → booking meetings
- Own CRM hygiene and workflow design to ensure leads move cleanly from sourcing through qualification
- Implement and iterate automation systems (workflow tools, enrichment, sequencing, routing, scoring)
- Define and improve qualification standards for meetings/leads in partnership with leadership
- Run structured experimentation on messaging, audiences, and channels to improve conversion rates
- Build durable documentation and internal processes so the motion scales beyond a single person
- Partner with engineering and product teams to stay aligned on positioning, ICP refinement, and feedback loops

## Requirements

### Must-Have

- Strong analytical and operational skills with a demonstrated ability to build systems (not just execute tasks)
- High ownership mindset: comfortable operating with ambiguity and setting your own plan/metrics
- Excellent organization and follow-through — reliably manage pipelines, tasks, and priorities
- Strong written communication for outbound messaging and clear internal documentation
- Comfort learning new tools quickly; experience with CRMs and automation tooling
- Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced startup environment with high standards and rapid iteration
- Authorized to work in the U.S. (visa sponsorship may be available)

### Nice-to-Have

- Experience building outbound or growth systems (GTM ops, growth, sales ops, rev ops, automation-heavy SDR/BDR work)
- Familiarity with AI/developer tooling ecosystems and technical buyer personas
- Experience using automation tools (e.g., n8n) or modern prospecting stacks

## Key Concepts

- **Reinforcement Learning (RL)**: A type of machine learning where models learn through trial-and-error interaction with an environment, optimizing for cumulative rewards. The partner company builds tooling for creating production-grade RL environments.
- **Outbound Engine**: A systematic pipeline for generating sales meetings — from identifying target accounts, enriching contact data, writing personalized messaging, to booking calls. The core system this role builds and owns.
