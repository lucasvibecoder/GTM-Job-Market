## Summary

PitchBook (Morningstar company) is hiring for its subsidiary **Lumonic**, a private credit management platform that automates reporting and compliance for private credit lenders. This Growth Marketing Lead owns pipeline creation end-to-end — designing automated, multi-channel programs across outbound, paid, ABM, content, events, data, and automation. Uses Clay, Apollo, Smartlead. Works closely with Lumonic founders and PitchBook senior leaders. In-office 5 days, Seattle.

> **Title note:** Listed as "Growth Marketing Lead" on LinkedIn. Clay defaulted to "GTM Engineer." The role is clearly GTME-relevant — owns outbound automation with Clay/Apollo/Smartlead, builds enrichment workflows and lead scoring. Marked `gtm_adjacent`.

## Responsibilities

- Design and implement automated workflows (Clay, Apollo, Smartlead) that generate qualified pipeline at scale — outbound sequences, inbound routing, signal-based campaigns, website conversion
- Design and execute integrated campaigns across outbound, paid channels, and content to generate qualified demos
- Tap into PitchBook's resources (SEO, social media, PR, events, cross-sell) to maximize conversion efficiency
- Own critical KPIs and establish comprehensive tracking systems
- Launch rapid-fire experiments across channels with disciplined analysis — validate fast, kill underperformers, scale winners
- Create data enrichment workflows, predictive lead scoring, and intelligent CRM automation
- Develop content and positioning for private credit and private equity decision-makers
- Address strategic growth problems including cross-sell optimization and seller enablement

## Key Concepts

- **Private Credit**: Non-bank lending to businesses — a rapidly growing segment of finance where compliance and reporting are complex and manual. Lumonic automates this, and this role builds the GTM engine to acquire private credit lenders as customers.
- **Signal-Based Campaigns**: Outbound campaigns triggered by specific buying signals (funding events, hiring patterns, tech stack changes) rather than static lists. Uses enrichment and intent data to time outreach to when prospects are most likely to engage.
