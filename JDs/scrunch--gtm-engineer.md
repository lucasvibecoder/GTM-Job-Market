## Summary
Scrunch is an AI-first marketing platform ($26M raised, Mayfield/Decibel, 500+ paying brands) helping companies get discovered by LLMs like ChatGPT and Claude. The GTM Engineer owns pipeline generation by building and automating outbound systems using Clay, Common Room, and Smartlead. High-leverage role at the intersection of growth, sales, and RevOps.

## Responsibilities
- Design and implement scalable systems for outbound pipeline generation
- Build micro-campaigns that activate on buying signals
- Connect and orchestrate tools across the GTM stack (Clay, Common Room, Smartlead)
- Partner with sales and marketing to translate signals into converting plays
- Develop durable processes that scale beyond one-off experiments
- Apply revenue operations skills to ensure data integrity, routing, and reporting

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- Systems thinker who sees growth problems as engineering challenges
- Creative and analytical — comfortable ideating campaigns and measuring results
- Fluent in modern GTM tools, enjoys stitching together workflows
- Approaches pipeline generation with curiosity, experimentation, and bias for action
- Treats AI and automation as force multipliers

### Nice-to-Have
- None explicitly listed

## Key Concepts
- **Signal-activated micro-campaigns**: Small, targeted outbound plays triggered by buying signals rather than static lists.
- **GTM stack orchestration**: Connecting multiple tools (Clay for enrichment, Common Room for signals, Smartlead for delivery) into a unified pipeline.
