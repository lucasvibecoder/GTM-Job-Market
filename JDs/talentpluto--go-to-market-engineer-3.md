## Summary
TalentPluto is recruiting for an undisclosed Series A healthcare technology company building an AI-native platform for large-scale care providers. The GTM Engineer owns the outbound and demand-generation engine from scratch — targeting, list building, enrichment, sequencing, and meeting generation for enterprise pipeline. Remote-first (NYC preferred), high ownership, with runway to expand into lifecycle motions and broader growth.

## Responsibilities
- Build and operate an end-to-end outbound engine: targeting → list building → enrichment → sequencing → meeting generation
- Own CRM structure, hygiene, and workflow design for clean lead movement from sourcing through qualification
- Implement and iterate automation systems (enrichment workflows, sequencing, routing, scoring, experimentation)
- Define and refine qualification standards for meetings and pipeline in partnership with leadership
- Run structured experiments on messaging, ICP targeting, and channels to improve conversion rates
- Build durable documentation and scalable processes so the motion extends beyond a single operator
- Partner cross-functionally with engineering and product to refine ICP, positioning, and feedback loops
- Identify bottlenecks in pipeline generation and proactively design systems to resolve them

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- Demonstrated ability to build systems and processes (not just execute tasks)
- Strong analytical and operational skills — comfortable designing metrics and iterating quickly
- High ownership mindset with comfort operating in ambiguity
- Excellent written communication for outbound messaging and internal documentation
- Experience with CRM systems and modern automation tooling (workflow automation, enrichment, sequencing)
- Ability to thrive in a fast-paced startup environment with high standards and rapid iteration

### Nice-to-Have
- Experience building outbound or growth systems (GTM ops, growth, sales ops, rev ops, automation-heavy SDR/BDR work)
- Familiarity with AI or technical buyer personas
- Experience using workflow automation tools (n8n or similar)
- Experience supporting enterprise sales motions

## Key Concepts
- **Automation-driven pipeline system**: An outbound engine where enrichment, sequencing, routing, and scoring are automated rather than manual. The core infrastructure this role builds — systems that generate meetings consistently without human bottlenecks.
- **Enterprise pipeline generation**: Building outbound motions specifically for enterprise sales cycles — longer, more complex, and requiring higher-quality targeting and messaging than SMB outbound. The strategic context for this healthcare AI company's GTM.
