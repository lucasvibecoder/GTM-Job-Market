## Summary
Radar is the global leader in geolocation ($85.5M raised from Accel, Insight Partners, processing 1B+ API calls/day). The GTM Engineer designs and maintains automation-rich workflows that reduce friction, ensure data accuracy, and give GTM teams more time to sell. Hands-on, systems-minded role reporting to Senior RevOps Manager.

## Responsibilities
- Architect intelligent, AI-native systems for GTM efficiency: lead enrichment/routing, automated outbound, opportunity management, renewal forecasting, billing workflows, CS automation
- Integrate and evolve GTM tools (Salesforce, HubSpot, Clay, ChatGPT, Outreach, Zendesk, ZoomInfo, Gong, Webflow) into cohesive stack
- Create feedback loops from prospect/customer calls and signals for Sales
- Partner with stakeholders to identify pain points and implement streamlined workflows
- Evaluate, select, and implement new technologies
- Collaborate with RevOps on CRM hygiene, data governance, process adherence
- Build reports and dashboards for KPIs (meeting velocity, pipeline, response rates, conversion, churn, forecasting)

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 2-4 years in GTM, Systems Architecture, RevOps, Marketing Ops, or technical GTM roles
- Salesforce and Clay experience
- Marketing automation (HubSpot, Marketo) and sales engagement tools (Outreach, SalesLoft, Gong)
- Systems designer: test hypotheses, iterate fast, monitor performance, design for scale
- Proactive GTM tool learning
- Business problem → system solution translation
- High data quality and process consistency standards

### Nice-to-Have
- None explicitly listed

## Key Concepts
- **AI-native GTM systems**: Using ChatGPT and AI alongside traditional tools (Salesforce, HubSpot) to build intelligent, automated workflows. Not AI as an add-on — AI as the architecture.
- **Full-lifecycle automation**: This role spans the entire customer journey — from lead enrichment to renewal forecasting to CS automation. Broader scope than pure outbound.
