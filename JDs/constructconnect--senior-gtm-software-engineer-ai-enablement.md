## Summary
ConstructConnect builds software solutions for the construction industry (TOP Server, OPC Router, OmniServer). This Senior GTM Software Engineer role centrally owns the GTM AI initiative portfolio — serving as the technical bridge between GTM, Product/Development, and IT. Owns a single GTM AI roadmap, runs structured intake/prioritization, and guides initiatives from discovery through POC, pilot, and scale. Hybrid in Atlanta or Cincinnati/NKY area.

## Responsibilities
- Own the GTM AI initiative roadmap across all segments and functions
- Run structured intake and prioritization process for AI ideas from GTM leaders and frontline teams
- Build business cases (impact, effort, dependencies) and facilitate trade-off decisions with Revenue, Product, and Development leaders
- Work with GTM leaders and AI Center of Excellence to define problems, workflows, and success criteria
- Translate GTM needs into clear technical requirements for Development, Product, IT, and GTM Systems
- Define and enforce standard initiative lifecycle: discovery, POC, pilot, scale
- Coordinate cross-functional project teams; track risks, issues, and dependencies
- Help define and apply AI standards and patterns for GTM (platforms, integration patterns, guardrails)
- Align with central AI governance on responsible use, data, and security
- Partner with Analytics and GTM Systems to define adoption, productivity, and revenue impact metrics
- Provide regular executive-level reporting on roadmap status and impact
- Coordinate with Sales Enablement, Academy, and GTM Programs on training and rollout plans
- Capture and share success stories and playbooks

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 10+ years in relevant technical role with GTM experience, product management, sales operations, revenue operations, or cross-functional role
- Demonstrated experience owning roadmaps or program portfolios across multiple teams
- Strong product mindset and ability to translate between GTM and technical stakeholders
- Experience designing, developing, and deploying AI and automated workflows using Python, AI, and APIs
- Knowledge of Microsoft products (Excel, Power Automate, SharePoint, Teams)

### Nice-to-Have
- None explicitly listed

## Key Concepts
- **GTM AI initiative roadmap**: A unified, prioritized backlog of AI projects across all go-to-market functions, managed through a structured lifecycle from discovery to scale.
- **AI governance**: Standards, guardrails, and responsible-use policies that ensure AI tools deployed in GTM are secure, ethical, and aligned with company-wide data practices.
