## Summary
Apollo.io is a $1.6B GTM platform (500K+ companies, Sequoia/Bain Capital-backed). The GTM Engineer is the primary operational partner to AE and Account Management teams, working with sales leadership to orchestrate outbound campaigns. Primary goal: increase rep productivity by 5%+ through outbound pipeline generation. Uses Apollo's own platform including new AI features.

## Responsibilities
- Design and launch outbound campaigns using Apollo's own tool
- Be at the forefront of Apollo's AI innovation — implement and test AI features into campaigns
- Maintain campaign documentation, roadmaps, and calendars
- Set goals, accelerate where exceeding, troubleshoot where falling behind
- Inspect and ensure accountability to playbooks and activity adherence
- Manage data accuracy through reviews and audits
- Liaise with Sales leaders to build outbound strategies
- Provide data support for whitespacing, champion mapping, leadership outreach, account penetration
- Configure new users with domains, manage large-scale outbound campaigns
- Collaborate with Corporate Engineering and Marketing on deliverability best practices

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 5+ years in Sales Ops, Revenue Ops, or related role
- Experience supporting AEs and Account Management teams
- Strong outbound campaign design, reporting, and process adherence
- Fast-paced, product-led, high-growth SaaS experience
- Cross-functional collaboration, data analysis, operational problem solving
- AI-focused sales outreach tools (Apollo, Clay, Regie.ai, Seamless.ai)

### Nice-to-Have
- GTM Engineer experience

## Key Concepts
- **Dogfooding GTM tools**: Using Apollo's own platform to run Apollo's outbound — unique credibility and product feedback loop.
- **Rep productivity engineering**: Measuring GTM Engineer success by a specific metric: 5%+ rep productivity increase through outbound pipeline generation.
