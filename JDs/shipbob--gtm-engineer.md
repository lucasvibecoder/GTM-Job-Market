## Summary

ShipBob is the nation's leading e-commerce fulfillment and logistics platform, helping merchants scale with distributed warehousing and shipping infrastructure. This GTM Engineer role owns end-to-end GTM Engineering initiatives — a hybrid of project manager and hands-on builder. Heavy Clay usage as the primary engine, with deep work across Salesforce, Gong, Vector, and 6sense to build signal engines, scoring models, and workflow automation. Reports to Director of Revenue Strategy & Operations.

## Responsibilities

- Own GTM Engineering initiatives from conception through delivery and iteration
- Identify, scope, and prioritize GTM Engineering opportunities with Sales, Marketing, and RevOps leadership
- Translate business needs into clear technical requirements and delivery plans
- Run GTM Engineering like product releases: design, build, QA, launch, measure, iterate
- Build and maintain Clay workflows for enrichment, routing, segmentation, scoring, and trigger-based actions
- Automate manual tasks that slow down revenue teams (research, list building, enrichment, lead management, CRM hygiene)
- Implement workflow orchestration across systems (Salesforce + intent tools + conversation intelligence + engagement tools)
- Combine multiple signal sources into composite scoring models and actionable triggers (fit + intent + engagement + timing)
- Design automation logic that avoids "single-signal spam" and prioritizes high-quality, high-confidence actions
- Improve speed-to-lead and account prioritization through signal-based orchestration
- Coordinate stakeholders across Sales, Marketing, RevOps, Enablement, and Data/Engineering
- Manage project timelines, risks, dependencies, and stakeholder communication
- Potentially manage junior GTM Engineers and/or third-party partners

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 4–8+ years in GTM systems, RevOps/Sales Ops/Marketing Ops, growth operations, automation engineering, or similar
- Demonstrated ability to build automations and integrations across a GTM tech stack
- Solid hands-on experience with Salesforce (data model, workflow automation, QA, hygiene)
- Experience building and operationalizing Clay workflows (or equivalent enrichment/orchestration tools)
- Excellent technical fluency: APIs/webhooks, data flows, automation logic, troubleshooting, systems thinking
- Sound project management skills: planning, prioritization, stakeholder alignment, execution
- Ability to communicate technical topics clearly to non-technical audiences

### Nice-to-Have
- Experience with Gong, 6sense, Vector, and modern intent/data tooling ecosystems
- Experience building scoring models, signal engines, and lifecycle automation
- Familiarity with data warehouse/analytics concepts and GTM data modeling for reporting
- Prior experience managing junior team members and/or vendors/consultancies

## Key Concepts

- **Composite Scoring Model**: Algorithmic approach that combines multiple signal types (fit, intent, engagement, timing) into a single "heat score" rather than relying on any single signal — avoids spam-like outreach triggered by weak individual signals.
- **Signal Engine**: System that ingests buying signals from multiple sources (6sense intent, Vector, web activity, engagement data) and orchestrates automated actions when signals converge above confidence thresholds.
- **Speed-to-Lead**: Time elapsed between a prospect signaling interest and a sales rep taking action — a key metric this role optimizes through automated routing and prioritization.
