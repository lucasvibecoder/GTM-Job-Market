## Summary
Lokalise is an AI-powered translation and localization platform (founded 2017, 25M+ users worldwide, 60+ integrations) helping product teams launch multilingual products 10x faster at 80% lower cost. The GTM Engineer joins Revenue Operations to build AI-powered workflows that replace static playbooks with "always-on" enablement — agentic automations embedded directly into revenue systems across Sales, AM, and CS. Remote role; must reside in UK, Latvia, or Spain.

## Responsibilities
- Design and deploy agentic workflows that automate prep, CRM updates, summaries, and admin tasks across Sales, AM, and CS
- Architect AI-native automations that surface next-best actions and trigger timely follow-ups
- Build signal detection systems monitoring usage shifts, renewal risks, and expansion opportunities
- Route AI-generated drafts (emails, decks, ROI views) to reps in real-time
- Integrate and evolve the GTM stack, connecting LLM platforms (Dust) with CRM and execution tools
- Develop core systems powering opportunity management, forecasting, and sales handoffs
- Construct enablement signal pipelines extracting data from Salesforce, Gong, and product usage
- Replace static playbooks with always-on enablement embedded in reps' daily workflows
- Drive and measure operational impact (prep time saved, signal-to-action speed, workflow adoption)

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 5+ years in GTM systems, revenue operations, or sales engineering/architecture
- Expertise orchestrating modern GTM stack (Clay, n8n, Make, Salesforce)
- Experience building AI-native workflows or autonomous agents (Dust, Relevance AI, OpenAI APIs)
- Ability to translate messy human problems from Sales/CS into clean system designs
- Autonomous prototype shipping in fast-paced, high-growth environments

### Nice-to-Have
- SQL or data warehouse experience for surfacing customer usage or intent signals

## Key Concepts
- **Always-on enablement**: Embedding AI-generated guidance (account context, next-best actions, competitive insights) directly into rep workflows, replacing static training playbooks with real-time, contextual support.
- **Signal detection systems**: Automated monitoring of product usage shifts, renewal risks, and expansion triggers that route AI-generated drafts (emails, decks, ROI views) to reps in real-time.
