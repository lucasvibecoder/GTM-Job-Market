## Summary

Engine transforms business travel with a platform combining corporate travel, charge card, and spend management — 20K+ companies, 1M+ travelers, billions in annual bookings. Cash flow positive, backed by Telescope Partners, Blackstone, and Permira. This Founding GTM Engineer builds the Growth Engineering function from the ground up — creating systems, signals, and automations for outbound and net-new customer acquisition. Includes hiring and mentoring as the team grows. Remote.

## Responsibilities

- Build and own systems that aggregate and transform signals powering outbound and growth efforts
- Design and ship automations and internal tools to improve account identification, prioritization, and engagement
- Partner with Sales, Marketing, Product, and Data to turn business needs into scalable technical solutions
- Lead the foundation of Growth Engineering at Engine, including hiring and mentoring
- Experiment quickly, learn from results, and evolve successful ideas into durable platforms

## Requirements

### Must-Have

- Experience building and maintaining production software (growth, product, marketing, or sales-adjacent)
- Strong proficiency in Python; Node.js is a plus
- Experience integrating GTM systems (Salesforce, Outreach, data/automation platforms)
- Comfort building internal tools, automations, and data-driven workflows
- Prior people leadership experience or readiness to step into team-lead role
- Builder mindset with bias toward action, ownership, and measurable impact

### Nice-to-Have

- None listed

## Key Concepts

- **Growth Engineering**: A dedicated engineering function that builds the technical infrastructure powering go-to-market — signal aggregation, internal tools, automation. Different from GTM Engineering in that it's a formal engineering team with production code responsibilities, not a RevOps/operations role.
- **Signal Aggregation**: Collecting and transforming data from multiple sources (product usage, intent data, market signals) into a unified view that powers outbound targeting. The core technical challenge this role solves.
