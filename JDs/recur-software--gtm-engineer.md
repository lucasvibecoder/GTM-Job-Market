## Summary
Recur Software is a private equity platform for the AI era — acquires mission-critical vertical SaaS companies ($1-10M ARR) and transforms them using AI. Raised $50M+ backed by Reef Pass, Prosus Ventures, Accel. The GTM Engineer owns the technical infrastructure behind how Recur sources acquisitions and how portfolio companies find customers. Unique multiplier: you build repeatable GTM playbooks deployed across multiple companies.

## Responsibilities
- Build and maintain enrichment and prospecting pipelines in Clay — from raw company lists to qualified, personalized outreach
- Design and optimize cold email campaigns in Smartlead (sequences, deliverability, A/B testing, reply handling)
- Create automations connecting tools: Clay, Salesforce, Claude Code, etc.
- Develop repeatable GTM playbooks deployable across Recur's portfolio companies
- Run experiments across messaging, channels, and sales processes — test fast, measure, kill what doesn't work
- Analyze outbound performance data and find bottlenecks slowing pipeline conversion
- Help portfolio companies stand up their own outbound, inbound, and lead generation infrastructure
- Work with founders to identify new channels, verticals, and approaches for deal sourcing

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 2-5 years in GTM engineering, revenue operations, or growth at B2B SaaS
- Hands-on experience with Clay, Smartlead, Apollo, HubSpot, Salesforce — built real pipelines, not just watched demos
- Comfortable writing lightweight automations (n8n, Zapier, Make, or basic Python scripting) and using AI tools like Claude Code
- Deep understanding of cold email best practices: deliverability, domain health, personalization at scale
- Data-oriented: thinks in enrichment steps, match rates, and conversion funnels
- Experimenter at heart — tests ten things and learns rather than plans one thing perfectly
- Resourceful and self-directed

### Nice-to-Have
- None explicitly listed

## Key Concepts
- **Enrichment pipeline**: A multi-step data workflow that takes raw company/contact lists and progressively adds information (firmographics, technographics, intent signals) until leads are qualified and personalized for outreach.
- **Portfolio GTM playbook**: A repeatable go-to-market system designed to be deployed across multiple companies. The unique challenge of building one engine that works across different verticals and customer profiles.
