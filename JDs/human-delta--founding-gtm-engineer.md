## Summary
Human Delta is the trust layer between enterprise data and AI tooling — backed by Susa Ventures, Afore Capital, Kleiner Perkins Scout Fund, and others. They find what's broken in data, governance, and workflows before AI deployment. The Founding GTM Engineer is the first GTM hire, owning the full go-to-market engine from scratch — outbound, inbound, data infrastructure, and AI-powered workflows. Reports directly to the founding team.

## Responsibilities
- Own the full go-to-market engine from the ground up
- Build, automate, and scale every part of the pipeline — outbound, inbound, data infrastructure, and AI-powered workflows
- Architect the systems that drive revenue
- Use AI to move faster than teams 10x the size

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- Technical and obsessed with using AI to move faster
- Fluent in the modern GTM toolchain — Clay, AI agents, automation platforms, web scraping
- Prior startup, founding, or early-stage experience
- Background in operations, customer success, consulting, or product strategy

### Nice-to-Have
- None explicitly listed

## Key Concepts
- **Trust layer for AI deployment**: The product category Human Delta operates in — validating that enterprise data, governance, and workflows are sound before organizations deploy AI tools. A growing need as AI adoption accelerates.
- **Founding GTM Engineer**: The first go-to-market hire responsible for building the entire revenue engine from zero. Higher ownership and ambiguity than a typical GTM Engineer role — requires both strategic thinking and hands-on execution.
