## Summary

Divisions Maintenance Group (DMG) provides facility maintenance services to retail chains and distribution/fulfillment centers. This GTM Engineer sits on the Business Intelligence team within Revenue Strategy & Planning — at the intersection of data engineering, analytics, and GTM operations. Builds data pipelines, dashboards, and AI-assisted workflows for Sales, RevOps, and Finance. **Title note**: LinkedIn URL slug is "Revenue Analytics Engineer" while the JD title is "GTM Engineer" — functionally this is a **data/analytics engineer with GTM focus**, not a traditional outbound or systems GTM Engineer role.

## Responsibilities

### GTM Analytics & Engineering
- Build and maintain data pipelines, transformations, and analytical models supporting Sales, Marketing, and Revenue Operations
- Design and deliver dashboards, KPIs, and self-service analytics for GTM leaders and frontline teams
- Ensure data quality, consistency, and documentation across BI and GTM data assets
- Support pipeline health, performance tracking, forecasting inputs, and funnel analysis
- Analyze historical deal and pricing data to identify patterns, risks, and opportunities informing pricing strategy
- Support pricing workflows by improving data completeness, validation checks, and standardized inputs

### GTM Workflow Enablement & Automation
- Identify high-friction GTM workflows and improve them using data, low-code automation, and AI-assisted tooling
- Use AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude) to accelerate analysis, reporting, drafting GTM artifacts, and prototyping automation
- Leverage low-code automation tools (Zapier or similar) to streamline repetitive GTM workflows
- Build repeatable, governed processes rather than one-off analyses
- Document workflows, logic, and outcomes in Confluence
- Instrument outputs so impact can be measured (time saved, adoption, performance lift)

### Cross-Functional Partnership
- Translate business questions into clear technical solutions
- Communicate insights and tradeoffs to non-technical stakeholders

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Data Engineering, Analytics, or related technical field
- Strong SQL skills and experience with structured datasets
- Working knowledge of Python for data transformation and automation
- Familiarity with data modeling, ETL/ELT concepts, and modern analytics workflows
- Experience building dashboards or reports in Power BI (or similar)
- Strong communication skills and comfort working with GTM stakeholders
- Comfort using AI tools to accelerate coding, analysis, and problem-solving
- Salesforce exposure (admin, reporting, or analytics)
- Proactive, ownership-oriented mindset

### Nice-to-Have
- None explicitly stated

## Key Concepts

- **Revenue Analytics Engineering**: A hybrid of data engineering and business analytics specifically scoped to the revenue lifecycle — building the data models, pipelines, and dashboards that GTM leaders use to make decisions. Distinct from traditional BI in that it's embedded within a revenue org, not a central analytics team.
- **AI-Assisted Workflow Enablement**: Using AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude) not to build AI products but to accelerate the analyst's own work — drafting summaries, prototyping automations, speeding up analysis. A lighter-touch AI integration than agentic engineering.
