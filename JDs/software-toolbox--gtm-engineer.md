## Summary
Software Toolbox is a full-stack industrial AI company delivering connectivity software, data platforms, and AI agents (JonJon.ai) for manufacturing and process industries. HQ in Charlotte, NC but hiring US-wide. The GTM Engineer builds and operates the outbound and lifecycle email marketing engine — segmented campaigns, lead routing/scoring, conversion instrumentation, and weekly experimentation cadence.

## Responsibilities
- Build segmented email programs and campaigns (ICP-based), A/B test messaging, manage deliverability, and report funnel impact
- Own lead routing, scoring, and handoff (marketing to SDR/AE), plus meeting booking workflows
- Stand up conversion instrumentation: landing pages, forms, UTMs, attribution, dashboards
- Run weekly experimentation cadence: subject lines, sequences, CTAs, landing pages, nurture leads
- Lead consistent adjustments to build and improve customer connection
- Own email marketing end-to-end, collaborating with Marketing, ECommerce, and Sales teams

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 3-6+ years in lifecycle/demand gen and/or marketing ops with hands-on campaign execution
- Strong ops chops: CRM + marketing automation + data hygiene + reporting (HubSpot experience)
- Proven ability to move pipeline metrics (meetings set, conversion rate, CAC payback signals)
- Excellent writing with an engineering mindset (hypothesis, test, measure, iterate)
- Strong work ethic

### Nice-to-Have
- Experience marketing to industrial/OT personas (Ops/Engineering/IT) and technical products
- Familiarity with account-based motions (ABM-lite), personalization at scale, and intent signals
- Basic technical literacy: APIs/webhooks, enrichment, SQL or data tooling comfort

## Key Concepts
- **Lifecycle email marketing engine**: A system of segmented, automated email programs that guide prospects through their journey from awareness to conversion, with ongoing experimentation.
- **Conversion instrumentation**: The measurement infrastructure — UTMs, attribution models, landing page analytics, dashboards — that connects marketing activity to pipeline outcomes.
