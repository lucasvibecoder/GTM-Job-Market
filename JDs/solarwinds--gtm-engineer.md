## Summary
SolarWinds is a publicly traded IT management company (Observability, Service Management, Database products) undergoing a cloud transformation. The GTM Engineer sits on the GTM Technology team within Marketing Strategy & Operations, owning the full lifecycle of AI agents and automation solutions — from designing prompt chains and context packs to governing agent deployment and measuring ROI across the demand generation stack.

## Responsibilities
- Design, develop, and deploy AI agents and ML orchestration patterns (prompts, chains, context packs) for GTM functions
- Build and maintain Zapier Canvas automations and high-impact GTM integrations
- Develop retrieval integrations (Glean/Genie, transcripts, product telemetry) for agent grounding
- Implement Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) approval workflows for critical processes
- Build reusable automation modules, templates, and workflow standards for enterprise-wide adoption
- Establish governance frameworks for AI agent deployment, behavior management, and compliance
- Monitor system health and integration reliability across Salesforce, Marketo, Outreach, Qualified, 6sense, Slack
- Define and optimize KPIs for agent task accuracy, resolution speed, and business impact
- Translate marketing and sales programs into technical specs with emphasis on programmatic execution and email nurture design
- Serve as internal AI evangelist, developing documentation, knowledge bases, and training playbooks
- Align AI deployments with Demand Generation, IT, Security, and Sales priorities

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 3-5 years in B2B marketing, Revenue Operations, or Sales Strategy
- Deep understanding of lead funnels, ABM principles, and demand generation metrics
- AI prompt engineering, agent alignment, and failure mode management
- Comfort with structured and unstructured datasets; SQL proficiency
- Proficiency with Salesforce, marketing automation, Zapier, Make, Retool, Relevance AI
- Familiarity with agent platforms (Glean Agents, Google Agentspace)

### Nice-to-Have
- Python or JavaScript foundational knowledge
- Experience with LLMs, agent architectures, or orchestration frameworks (LangChain, CrewAI)
- REST APIs and third-party system integration
- No-code/low-code platform background
- MCP framework implementation between GTM tools

## Key Concepts
- **Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) workflows**: Automation patterns where AI handles routine steps but routes critical decisions to a human for approval, balancing speed with governance and compliance.
- **Context packs**: Curated bundles of real-time business context (call transcripts, product telemetry, CRM data) fed to AI agents so their outputs are grounded in accurate, current information.
- **MCP (Model Context Protocol)**: A standard for connecting AI models to external tools and data sources, enabling agents to take actions across the GTM stack without custom integrations for each tool.
