## Summary
Snowflake (public company) is hiring a GTM Systems Engineer to revolutionize marketing operations using AI and automation. The role builds the scalable technical backbone that accelerates customer acquisition and expansion across the GTM org. Partners with Marketing, Sales, and Data teams to translate strategic vision into high-performing, measurable systems.

## Responsibilities
- Streamline and optimize marketing processes including campaign creation and audience segmentation
- Partner with Marketing, Marketing Intelligence, and Sales Operations to scope AI-driven solutions
- Integrate AI APIs, MCP Servers, and automation tools to build agents and workflows
- Architect, build, and deploy AI prototypes and scalable automated workflows (LLMs, AI APIs)
- Create and maintain technical documentation and training materials

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 6+ years in Marketing Ops or Revenue Ops within high-growth SaaS
- Expert-level Salesforce and Marketo
- Hands-on with data orchestration and automation (Clay, Workato)
- Track record applying AI or advanced automation to business problems (LLMs, RAG, prompt engineering)
- Python or JavaScript/TypeScript for automation scripts and API requests
- Exceptional executive communication
- Builder and practical innovator mindset
- Highly analytical and process-driven

### Nice-to-Have
- SQL
- BI visualization (Tableau, Looker, Power BI)

## Key Concepts
- **MCP Servers**: Model Context Protocol servers — a way to connect AI models to external tools and data. Cutting-edge requirement showing Snowflake is building AI-native GTM infrastructure.
- **AI agents in marketing ops**: Using LLMs to automate campaign creation, segmentation, and GTM workflows. This role is building the future of marketing operations.
