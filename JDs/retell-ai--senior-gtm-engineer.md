## Summary

Retell AI is a YC-backed voice AI startup reimagining the call center — $36M ARR with just 20 people, up from $5M at start of 2025. Building a CX platform where entire contact centers run on AI "workers" (frontline agents, QA analysts, managers). This Senior GTM Engineer owns the systems powering the go-to-market motion: lead flow, enrichment, routing, automation, reporting. **Hands-on builder role** — write code, ship integrations, create infrastructure. $190-230K base + equity, in-office in Redwood City with relocation provided.

## Responsibilities

- Design and implement end-to-end GTM workflows across lead capture, enrichment, routing, and outreach
- Build and maintain integrations using Python, SQL, or JavaScript to connect internal systems and external tools
- Own and optimize the GTM stack: Clay, Salesforce, enrichment APIs (Apollo, Clearbit), automation layers (n8n, Gumloop, Zapier)
- Ensure data quality and consistency across the funnel, from top-of-funnel acquisition through closed-won
- Create internal tools, scripts, and dashboards that improve sales team efficiency and visibility
- Continuously test, iterate, and ship improvements that measurably impact pipeline generation and conversion

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 3+ years in a GTM Engineer, Sales Engineer, or Technical RevOps role
- Comfortable coding in Python, SQL, or JavaScript — has built real integrations or automation systems
- Deep understanding of the modern GTM stack and how tools connect across the funnel
- Thinks beyond tooling — understands why data matters and how it influences sales outcomes
- Has shipped workflows, tools, or systems that materially changed how a sales team operates
- Enjoys owning ambiguous problems and turning them into scalable, automated solutions

### Nice-to-Have
- None explicitly stated

## Key Concepts

- **Voice AI Contact Center**: Retell's core product — AI agents that handle sales, support, and logistics calls that previously required human agents. The GTM Engineer markets a product that itself automates go-to-market conversations, creating an interesting meta-loop.
- **Enrichment Waterfall**: Using multiple enrichment APIs (Apollo, Clearbit, etc.) in sequence — if the first source doesn't return data, fall through to the next. This role builds and maintains these cascading enrichment pipelines.
