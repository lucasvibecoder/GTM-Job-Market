## Summary
Parabola is an AI workflow builder for operations and finance teams — automates messy data transformation from PDFs, emails, and spreadsheets (customers include Brooklinen, SKIMS, Flexport). Backed by OpenView, Matrix Partners, Thrive Capital. The GTM Engineer owns end-to-end GTM Ops projects powering the revenue engine — data quality, marketing attribution, source-of-truth models, revenue recognition, and scaled outbound tooling. Works closely with Marketing, Sales, and Operations leadership.

## Responsibilities
- Own end-to-end implementation of GTM Ops projects across the GTM stack — build, ship, iterate
- Maintain data quality, system hygiene, and reliable workflows across the wider tech stack
- Enable marketing team to communicate at scale while maintaining domain health, enrichment best practices
- Improve marketing attribution (first-touch + last-touch), including tracking standards and reporting
- Build and improve "source of truth" models capturing prospect activity through the funnel (lead → opp → customer)
- Support revenue recognition for closed-won deals (process, data flow, reporting, exports)
- Manage tools outside the CRM to enable scaled outbound/marketing (email + LinkedIn) compliantly

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- GTM Ops experience at a high-growth B2B SaaS company
- Hands-on builder — comfortable configuring, integrating, and debugging end-to-end
- Strong grasp of GTM funnels + lifecycle stages (and the "why" behind process + reporting decisions)
- Execution-oriented — ship high-quality work with clear direction
- Operational rigor — attention to detail, documentation, data/privacy/compliance
- Bias towards AI tools and coding solutions for smoother CRM data interaction
- In office in NYC or SF ~3-4 days a week

### Nice-to-Have
- Attribution methodologies + lifecycle reporting best practices
- Activity tracking / prospect engagement model design
- Basic SQL and/or data warehouse concepts
- Familiarity with current GTM stack: Salesforce, HubSpot, Outreach, Cargo, HeyReach, Attention, Apollo, Instantly

## Key Concepts
- **Source of truth models**: Centralized data models that capture and surface all prospect activity through the funnel (lead → opportunity → customer). Eliminates conflicting data across systems so teams trust a single version of pipeline reality.
- **Marketing attribution (first-touch + last-touch)**: Tracking which marketing touchpoints initiated a prospect's journey (first-touch) and which drove the final conversion (last-touch). The reporting infrastructure this role builds and improves.
