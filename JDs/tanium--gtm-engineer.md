## Summary
Tanium is a Forbes Cloud 100 endpoint management and security platform powering Autonomous IT for major enterprises. The GTM Engineer sits in the GTM Automation team, designing and building AI-powered automation and signal-based workflows across Marketing and Sales systems. Cross-functional builder role that owns GTM automation in platforms like Qualified, 6sense, Marketo, and Clari — with heavy emphasis on governance and partnership with IT/Sales Systems.

## Responsibilities
- Partner with GTM leadership to shape GTM automation and AI strategy
- Identify high-impact automation opportunities across Marketing and Sales
- Contribute to architectural discussions around AI-assisted and agentic workflows
- Design and build automation and AI-powered solutions including signal-based workflows acting on buyer intent, engagement, and lifecycle events
- Build automated engagement and outbound workflows including enrichment, qualification, routing, and Sales handoff
- Leverage AI for contextual personalization at scale across GTM motions
- Drive end-to-end implementation of GTM automation in platforms owned by GTM (Qualified, 6sense, Marketo, People.ai, Clari, Traction Complete)
- Collaborate with Sales Systems, Enterprise Architecture, and IT to translate GTM needs into scalable solutions
- Serve as day-to-day administrator for GTM automation tools
- Drive adoption and usage of owned GTM tools through enablement and documentation
- Partner with Sales Systems, Enterprise Architecture, IT, and Security for production deployment
- Define and track success metrics tied to business outcomes with BI and Analytics team
- Ensure automation workflows meet standards for data quality, auditability, privacy, and compliance

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 5+ years in GTM Operations, RevOps, Sales/Marketing Systems, or Automation Engineering
- Proven experience designing and building automation workflows across GTM systems
- Hands-on experience with AI-powered technologies, agents, or autonomous GTM systems
- Excellent communication skills translating between business and technical stakeholders
- Strong technical fluency including Salesforce, APIs, integrations, and workflow orchestration tools
- Strong analytical, organizational, and project management skills

### Nice-to-Have
- SQL and data analysis for debugging and validation
- Experience in environments where IT and business stakeholders own a mix of production systems
- Familiarity with Marketo, Qualified, enrichment platforms, Workato, analytics tools
- Experience building internal tools or lightweight UIs to unify data across systems
- Experience in a global B2B SaaS or enterprise technology environment
- Familiarity with advanced segmentation strategies, lifecycle marketing, and personalization frameworks

## Key Concepts
- **Signal-based workflows**: Automation triggered by buyer intent signals, engagement events, and lifecycle changes rather than static lists or manual triggers. Core motion this role builds.
- **GTM Automation governance**: Balancing speed of deployment with compliance, security, and data quality standards — critical in an enterprise environment where IT, Security, and business teams co-own production systems.
