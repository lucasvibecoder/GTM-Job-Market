## Summary

Tapcheck is an on-demand pay fintech platform (earned wage access, no cost to employers). This GTM Engineer — titled "GTM AI Engineer" in the JD body but "GTM Engineer" on LinkedIn — is the AI architect for the full revenue org, reporting to the VP of Revenue Operations. Builds AI agents, churn prediction models, automated QBR decks, and intelligent workflows across BDRs, Marketing, Sales, Channel, and Customer Success. Heavy Python/SQL, LLM/prompt engineering, and production AI system requirements. $160-190K base + bonus/equity.

> **Title note:** LinkedIn lists "GTM Engineer"; JD body uses "GTM AI Engineer." Both reflect the role — it's a GTM Engineer whose primary function is building AI-powered systems.

## Responsibilities

- Design and deploy AI-powered revenue automation — auto-enrich leads, generate personalized outreach, predict deal outcomes, flag accounts requiring intervention
- Create AI agents that research accounts, synthesize competitive intelligence, and generate talking points before calls
- Surface deal risks, recommend next steps, and help reps prioritize pipeline
- Develop AI-driven attribution insights, campaign optimization, lead scoring models, and partner routing logic
- Build churn prediction models, expansion opportunity identification, and automated QBR deck generation with real-time account data
- Extend automation into proactive customer health monitoring and intervention triggers
- Integrate AI capabilities into Salesforce, Gong, HubSpot, and broader tech stack
- Ensure solutions are production-ready, scalable, and adopted by end users

## Requirements

### Must-Have

- 3-5 years building AI/ML solutions or intelligent automation in B2B SaaS, fintech, or revenue operations context
- Strong proficiency in Python, SQL, and modern AI/ML frameworks
- Experience with LLMs (OpenAI, Claude), prompt engineering, and building AI agents
- Familiarity with workflow automation tools (Clay, n8n, Zapier) and data pipelines
- Deep familiarity with Salesforce and modern revenue tech stack (Gong, HubSpot, Outreach, ZoomInfo)
- Revenue acumen — understand lead flow, deal progression, customer expansion
- Comfortable with APIs, webhooks, and building integrations connecting disparate systems
- Data-driven measurement — baseline processes, track time savings, build automation ROI case
- Communication — translate complex technical concepts for non-technical stakeholders

### Nice-to-Have

- None listed

## Key Concepts

- **AI Agent Productivity Multiplier**: Building AI systems that don't replace reps but augment them — auto-generating account research, competitive intelligence, and talking points before calls so every conversation starts informed. The goal is hours of prep work compressed into seconds.
- **Churn Prediction Models**: ML models that analyze product usage, engagement signals, and account health data to predict which customers are at risk of churning before it happens — enabling proactive intervention rather than reactive firefighting.
