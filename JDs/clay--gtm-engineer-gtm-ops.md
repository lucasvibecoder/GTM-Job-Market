## Summary

Clay ($5B valuation, $100M+ revenue, Sequoia/CapitalG/First Round-backed) is the GTM platform used by Anthropic, Notion, Google, and Ramp. This GTM Engineer (GTM Ops) focuses on the heart of the funnel: pipeline management, opportunity workflows, deal acceleration, and post-sale handoffs. Builds the systems that move deals forward — from first meeting to renewal. Designs AI-native automations for next-best actions, timely follow-ups, and deal velocity.

> **Title note:** Listed as "GTM Engineer (GTM Ops)" on the listing. LinkedIn URL slug reads "gtm-operations." Both capture the RevOps-focused nature of this role — it's GTM Engineering applied to mid/bottom-funnel operations rather than top-of-funnel outbound.

## Responsibilities

- Build and maintain systems powering opportunity management, sales handoffs, forecasting, and post-sale workflows
- Design AI-native automations that surface next-best actions, trigger timely follow-ups, and improve deal velocity
- Connect GTM tools into one cohesive stack and evolve that stack as the company scales
- Work side-by-side with Sales and Customer teams to translate business needs into technical solutions
- Balance long-term scalability with short-term speed in a fast-growing environment

## Requirements

### Must-Have

- 4+ years in GTM systems, revenue operations, or sales architecture at a fast-moving company
- Built or owned systems supporting sales pipelines, post-sale processes, and customer lifecycle ops
- Enjoy working closely with non-technical teams — turn messy problems into clean system design
- Strong sense of curiosity, tinkering, and hacking
- Strong grasp of the modern GTM stack and SQL
- Excitement about AI making sales and customer workflows smarter and more automated

### Nice-to-Have

- None listed

## Key Concepts

- **Deal Velocity**: The speed at which opportunities move through the sales pipeline from first meeting to close. This role builds the systems and automations that reduce friction and accelerate progression at each stage.
- **AI-Native Automations**: Workflows built with AI at the core (not bolted on) — surfacing next-best actions, triggering context-aware follow-ups, and personalizing deal progression based on signals rather than static rules.
