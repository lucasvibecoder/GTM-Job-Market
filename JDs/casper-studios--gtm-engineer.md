## Summary
Casper Studios is a bootstrapped, profitable AI services firm (team from LinkedIn, Bain, Microsoft) partnering with enterprises, startups, and creators to build AI-driven products. Also builds own products including a Chrome extension (20k+ users) and voice-based AI tools. The GTM Engineer takes ownership of all go-to-market systems and automations — centralizing scattered workflows into a coherent infrastructure using Attio (current CRM) and Claude Code. Key goal: give time back to partners and senior leaders by automating proposal generation, pipeline visibility, outreach, and reporting.

## Responsibilities
- Take ownership of Casper's go-to-market systems and automations
- Manage and extend CRM infrastructure (currently Attio)
- Build proposal generation systems that automatically assemble client proposals from past work and templates
- Create pipeline visibility and review systems for leadership
- Build account and network mapping across relationships and partners
- Design automated outreach workflows across platforms such as LinkedIn
- Create content systems that help the team publish insights and case studies consistently
- Build internal automation that reduces manual work across business development and operations

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- Experience in revenue operations, growth engineering, GTM engineering, sales operations, or automation engineering
- Comfortable building automations using APIs, workflow tools, or scripting environments
- Commercial instinct — understand how sales teams operate, how pipeline/relationships/timing affect deals
- Comfortable working with CRMs and revenue systems

### Nice-to-Have
- Experience with Attio, HubSpot, or Salesforce
- Experience using Claude Code to automate workflows

## Key Concepts
- **GTM infrastructure centralization**: Consolidating scattered automations and workflows (currently maintained by founders and engineers) into a coherent, scalable system that can grow with the company.
- **Proposal generation system**: An automated workflow that assembles client proposals from templates and past work, reducing the manual burden on senior leaders and partners.
