## Summary

Nooks ($70M+ raised, Kleiner Perkins-backed) builds the AI Sales Assistant Platform (ASAP) used by HubSpot, Rippling, and hundreds more. Grown ARR 4x then 3x, targeting 3x again. This is their first GTM Engineer — a technical co-pilot for the Sales Development org. Owns setup, optimization, and enablement within the Nooks platform, translating product releases into scalable SDR workflows and playbooks for internal and external customers. Reports to Head of Sales Development. SF hybrid 3x/week.

> **Title note:** Listed as "GTM Engineer (Hybrid)" on Clay, but LinkedIn URL slug reads "gtm-sales-development-engineer." Both titles capture the role — it's a GTM Engineer focused specifically on sales development enablement.

## Responsibilities

- Partner with Engineering and Product to stay ahead of new features, translating into actionable SDR enablement
- Set up new features, products, and playbooks within the Nooks platform for sales team efficiency
- Build best-in-class processes for external customers
- Use analytics and data to report on improved efficiencies and impact to pipeline generation
- Provide ongoing product feedback and ideas to product/engineering team
- Partner with sales enablement on playbooks and content for all connecting GTM technology
- Identify and resolve process bottlenecks, data issues, and inconsistencies in process, systems, and strategy

## Requirements

### Must-Have

- 2+ years of relevant sales development experience selling into sales leaders
- Experience prospecting into B2B SaaS Mid-Market / Enterprise companies
- Ability to thrive in a rapidly changing early-stage startup
- Manage multiple projects and timelines across departments
- Strong analytic skills — leverage data to determine prioritization
- Deep familiarity with Nooks, HubSpot, ZoomInfo, and Sales Nav
- Hybrid — travel to SF office 3x/week

### Nice-to-Have

- None listed

## Key Concepts

- **AI Sales Assistant Platform (ASAP)**: Nooks' platform that automates sales busywork — parallel dialing, AI note-taking, research — so reps focus on human conversations. This role enables SDR teams to maximize the platform's capabilities.
- **Pipeline Velocity**: The speed at which leads move through the sales funnel from first touch to closed deal. This role builds systems and playbooks to accelerate it through better tooling and process optimization.
