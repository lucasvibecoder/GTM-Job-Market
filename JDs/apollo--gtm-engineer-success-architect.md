## Summary
Apollo.io is the leading GTM platform for revenue teams ($250M raised, $1.6B valuation, Series D, 500K+ companies). The GTM Engineer (Success Architect) is a customer-facing role — part outbound campaign strategist, part AI workflow orchestrator, part trusted advisor. You design, deploy, and optimize Apollo usage for high-value customers, building AI-native inbound and outbound engines that generate pipeline at scale. Post-sale focused: adoption, expansion, and credit consumption growth.

## Responsibilities
- Design and execute adoption and expansion plays across the post-sale journey, leveraging AI, product telemetry, and signal-based triggers
- Own key growth outcomes — GRR, NRR, credit consumption uplift, and seat expansion
- Deliver playbook-driven interventions that drive measurable impact on customer accounts
- Assess and analyze accounts, delivering robust Apollo Platform configurations
- Demonstrate advanced GTM strategy: signal-based ICP logic, AI-generated messaging + advanced prompting, campaign architecture
- Partner with clients to refine their GTM strategies and align with pipeline and revenue objectives
- Stay ahead of GTM tech trends, continuously refining the stack

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 4+ years in Sales Ops, RevOps, Growth, Account Management, or Sales Development leadership
- Data fluency — turn complex data sets into actionable revenue strategies (ICP, TAM, SAM)
- CRMs, sales automation tools, and AI platforms mastery
- Process optimization — spot inefficiencies and turn chaotic workflows into smooth operations
- Customer obsessed — build lasting client relationships
- Scale GTM strategies in a fast-paced, results-driven environment

### Nice-to-Have
- None explicitly listed

## Key Concepts
- **Success Architect**: A customer-facing GTM Engineer who builds and optimizes the GTM infrastructure for clients rather than internally. Blends consulting, campaign strategy, and platform configuration into a post-sale growth role.
- **Signal-based ICP logic**: Using behavioral and firmographic signals to dynamically define and refine ideal customer profiles, rather than static list-based targeting. A core capability this role teaches Apollo's customers to leverage.
