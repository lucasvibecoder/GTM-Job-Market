## Summary

Cube is a cloud-based FP&A platform backed by Battery Ventures and Mayfield, helping Finance teams plan without sacrificing spreadsheets. This GTM Engineer owns and scales outbound pipeline generation with secondary focus on improving throughput across GTM roles and funnel stages. Sits at the intersection of Sales, RevOps, Marketing, and Finance. Hybrid NYC (Tue-Thu).

## Responsibilities

- Build and maintain end-to-end outbound pipeline systems from ICP definition through enrichment, sequencing, and attribution
- Create account and contact prioritization models using firmographic, technographic, intent, and behavioral signals
- Automate routing, territories, and book-of-business management to support scale
- Eliminate manual work from SDR and AE workflows through automation and internal tooling
- Improve funnel throughput by reducing time to first touch, time in stage, and handoff friction
- Identify and resolve bottlenecks using data, tooling, and systems design
- Implement AI-driven workflows for outbound personalization, research, prioritization, and opportunity prep
- Own administration, configuration, and optimization of core GTM tools including Salesforce
- Define source-of-truth metrics and build dashboards for pipeline creation, velocity, and productivity

## Requirements

### Must-Have

- 3-7+ years in GTM Engineering, RevOps, Sales Ops, Growth, or related role
- Proven experience building outbound pipeline systems at scale
- Strong GTM tooling experience with Salesforce as core
- SQL, Python, or JavaScript
- Deep understanding of SDR, AE, and full-funnel dynamics
- Operator mindset with strong bias toward automation and measurable impact

### Nice-to-Have

- None listed

## Key Concepts

- **FP&A (Financial Planning & Analysis)**: The finance function responsible for budgeting, forecasting, and strategic financial planning. Cube's core product — a platform that lets Finance teams do this work in spreadsheets connected to a cloud backend.
- **Funnel Throughput**: The rate at which leads, opportunities, and deals flow through each stage of the sales process. This role's secondary mandate — measuring and improving conversion rates, stage duration, and handoff efficiency across SDR, AE, and CS.
