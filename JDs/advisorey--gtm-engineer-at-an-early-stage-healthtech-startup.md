## Summary
Agency posting by advisorey on behalf of an unnamed early-stage AI HealthTech startup. The GTM Engineer owns outbound workflows (Outreach/SalesLoft), HubSpot optimization, and Clay enrichment pipelines to power the revenue engine. Hands-on role focused on tooling, data, and process with direct pipeline impact. Contact: Chessa Eskandanian-Yee (chessa@advisorey.com).

## Responsibilities
- Build and maintain outbound workflows and sequences in Outreach or SalesLoft
- Own and optimize the HubSpot instance — lead lifecycle, scoring, routing, and reporting
- Build enrichment pipelines using Clay to automate prospecting research at scale
- Partner with Sales and Marketing to identify funnel gaps and build solutions
- Manage integrations across the GTM stack and maintain clean data flow between systems
- Document processes and help the team adopt new tooling effectively

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 2+ years in GTM engineering, sales ops, or revenue operations
- Solid hands-on HubSpot experience (workflows, properties, pipelines, reporting)
- Experience building in Outreach or SalesLoft (sequences, templates, governance)
- Familiarity with Clay or enrichment/data tools for prospect research and list building
- Comfortable connecting tools via APIs or Zapier-style integrations
- Strong communicator who can work cross-functionally with non-technical teammates

### Nice-to-Have
- Experience in healthtech, B2B SaaS, or selling into enterprise

## Key Concepts
- **Enrichment Pipeline**: An automated workflow (typically in Clay) that takes a raw prospect list and layers on firmographic, technographic, and contact data from multiple providers — enabling personalized outreach at scale.
- **Lead Lifecycle Management**: The system of stages, scoring rules, and routing logic in HubSpot that determines how a lead progresses from first touch to sales handoff.
