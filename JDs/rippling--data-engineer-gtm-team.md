## Summary

Rippling ($1.4B+ raised, Kleiner Perkins/Founders Fund/Sequoia-backed) gives businesses one place to run HR, IT, and Finance. This Data Engineer on the GTM Team designs and implements backend services, AI integrations, and data pipelines powering the sales automation stack — account/lead enrichment, recommendation engines, AI-powered CRM workflows, and multi-LLM orchestration. Works with AI/ML engineers, data scientists, and sales partners. Full-stack data engineering role.

> **Title note:** Listed as "Data Engineer - GTM Team" in the JD. LinkedIn URL slug reads "senior-data-engineer-gtm-team" (includes "Senior"). Both titles relevant — the seniority level skews senior. Marked `gtm_adjacent` as this is a data engineering role, not a GTM Engineer variant.

## Responsibilities

- Design and implement scalable backend systems powering AI/ML-driven recommendation, ranking, and personalization workflows
- Build and maintain multi-LLM applications using OpenAI, Claude, and custom Databricks models in real-time workflows
- Develop and optimize data pipelines for model training, enrichment, and scoring using Databricks, Snowflake, and Kafka
- Collaborate on AI/ML pipeline development — training and deploying XGBoost, classification, and recommender models
- Design and maintain scalable data pipelines unifying CRM, product, marketing, billing, and internal databases
- Build internal tools and APIs to support feature generation and GTM evaluation frameworks
- Partner with Growth Engineering and GTM stakeholders to translate growth initiatives into scalable AI and data systems

## Requirements

### Must-Have

- 3+ years professional software engineering (backend, data, or AI-focused)

- Experience building production-grade services or data-intensive applications
- Familiarity with AI/ML systems (model training, inference APIs, recommendation engines)
- Experience with data platforms (Databricks, Snowflake, Kafka)
- Experience working with GTM teams (sales, marketing, RevOps, Sales Ops)

## Key Concepts

- **Multi-LLM Orchestration**: Coordinating multiple large language models (OpenAI, Claude, custom models) within a single workflow — routing different tasks to different models based on capability, cost, and latency. This role builds the infrastructure that makes this work at scale.
- **Recommendation Engine**: A system that suggests actions or prioritizations based on patterns in data — in this context, recommending which accounts to target, which leads to prioritize, or which outreach to send. Built on XGBoost, matrix factorization, and similar ML models.
