## Summary
DoorLoop is property management software (offices in Miami, NYC, Tel Aviv) moving upmarket to enterprise. The GTM Engineer owns the full rebuild of a legacy Salesforce environment into a flexible, multi-funnel platform — complex routing, attribution, AI-powered workflows, and scalable automation across Marketing, Sales, and RevOps. Architecture-level builder role, not data engineering.

## Responsibilities
- Own Salesforce architecture and GTM system design (objects, fields, relationships, flows, validation logic, routing, integrations)
- Rebuild and simplify a heavily customized legacy Salesforce environment for enterprise GTM execution
- Design, build, and maintain GTM automations and workflows, including AI-powered workflows and decisioning
- Implement flexible, segment-aware routing frameworks with deterministic logic and AI-assisted decisioning
- Design GTM system changes with an experimentation mindset (hypotheses, safe rollout, pre-/post-change measurement)
- Partner with Marketing and RevOps on lifecycle management, lead/account scoring, attribution, and segmentation
- Support account-based GTM strategies through account hierarchies, enrichment, scoring, and targeting logic
- Collaborate with Data Engineering and Analytics to ensure Salesforce feeds Snowflake and Tableau cleanly
- Leverage webhooks, n8n, AI services, and code-based logic to extend automation beyond Salesforce
- Integrate web analytics and product data (PostHog) for lifecycle logic, attribution, routing, and experimentation
- Ensure GTM system reliability and data quality
- Document system architecture, workflows, automation decisions, and experiment outcomes
- Participate in RevOps intake and prioritization, balancing rebuilds with ongoing GTM needs

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 3-6+ years in GTM Engineering, Revenue Operations, or Sales Operations at high-growth SaaS
- Deep hands-on Salesforce architecture experience (complex object relationships, Flows, validation rules)
- Systems-thinking mindset with experience rebuilding or refactoring legacy GTM systems
- Salesforce integration with marketing automation (HubSpot), web analytics (PostHog), and other GTM tooling
- Strong understanding of GTM funnels — routing, lifecycle stages, scoring, attribution
- AI-powered workflows, decisioning, or automation within GTM systems
- Comfort with webhooks, workflow automation tools, AI services, and code-like logic
- Balance precision and speed — build systems that last
- Push back on requests that introduce unnecessary complexity or tech debt
- Cross-functional collaboration and clear communication
- High ownership mentality with a strong bar for reliability and measurable impact

### Nice-to-Have
- Apex or programmatic Salesforce experience
- Workflow orchestration tools (n8n)
- Operationalizing AI models or agents in production GTM workflows
- Enrichment and ABM tooling (ZoomInfo, Clearbit, Clay)
- Understanding how CRM and product data feed data warehouses and BI tools

## Key Concepts
- **Legacy Salesforce refactoring**: Transforming a heavily customized, single-funnel Salesforce instance into a flexible multi-funnel platform. The core challenge of this role — rebuilding without creating new tech debt.
- **Segment-aware routing**: Lead and account routing that adapts based on segment (e.g., SMB vs. enterprise) rather than one-size-fits-all rules. Critical as DoorLoop moves upmarket to enterprise GTM execution.
