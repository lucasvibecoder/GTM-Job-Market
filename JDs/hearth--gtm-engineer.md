## Summary

Hearth builds revenue-critical software and AI agents for home improvement contractors (roofers, HVAC techs, builders), helping 15,000+ paying customers win more jobs and manage homeowner relationships. This is the **founding GTM Engineer** — the first hire in a new Automation & AI GTM pod, tasked with transforming reactive, ticket-driven revenue operations into an AI-first GTM engineering organization. Reports alongside GTM teams and product, with a mandate to make every Marketing, CS, and Sales team member more productive through intelligent automation.

## Responsibilities

- Partner with product to build retention revenue systems that scale successful human interventions driving key leading indicators for customer outcomes
- Solve critical marketing ops bottlenecks and enable high-velocity marketing automation through HubSpot, seamlessly leveraging data from HubSpot, Salesforce, and Redshift
- Audit and rebuild the outbound motion end-to-end — lead sourcing, scoring, and distribution — to drive a 2–5x increase in ARR/Rep/Day
- Seed the internal AI Go Team working with the AI PM to optimize systems and drive AI revenue goals as founding member
- Improve data quality and deduplication in partnership with the data team, building resilient, production-grade automations across Clay, Salesforce, and HubSpot

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- Product mindset — roadmap thinking, prioritize by business impact, proactively identify opportunities
- End-to-end shipping — from problem identification through production deployment to impact measurement, no handoffs
- First principles thinking — deconstruct problems and reinvent processes rather than optimize existing solutions
- AI/LLM fluency — built real workflows with Claude, GPT; prompt engineering, API integration, agentic solutions
- Advanced GTM ops automation (Clay, n8n, Zapier or custom-built equivalents)
- Salesforce depth — flows, automation rules, SFDC data model
- HubSpot and marketing automation stack ownership
- Startup velocity — ships fast, iterates, comfortable with ambiguity

### Nice-to-Have
- Python/SQL skills for data manipulation and custom integrations
- B2B SaaS, transactional sales teams, home services, or financial services experience
- Track record creating actionable GTM engineering content on Substack, LinkedIn, or X

## Key Concepts

- **ARR/Rep/Day**: Revenue productivity metric measuring annual recurring revenue generated per sales rep per day — the target KPI for the outbound engine rebuild (2–5x increase).
- **AI GTM Pod**: A cross-functional team structure pairing a GTM Engineer with an AI PM to systematically identify, build, and deploy AI-powered revenue workflows across the GTM org.
- **Production-Grade Automation**: Automations built with data validation, error handling, and deduplication that run reliably at scale — distinguished from one-off scripts or fragile prototypes.
