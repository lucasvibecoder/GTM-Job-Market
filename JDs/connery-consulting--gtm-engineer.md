## Summary
Connery Consulting is recruiting for an undisclosed AI-powered SaaS company helping global brands improve product quality through real-time customer feedback insights. The GTM Engineer joins the Revenue organization at the intersection of Sales, Marketing, and RevOps — improving operational efficiency and implementing AI-driven tools to enhance GTM performance. Reports close to the CRO. Onsite SF (4 days/week).

## Responsibilities
- Serve as trusted operational partner to Sales, Marketing, and Revenue Operations teams
- Support day-to-day GTM operations while identifying opportunities to streamline workflows with automation and AI tools
- Partner with executive leadership (CRO) to evaluate and implement AI-enabled solutions for sales effectiveness
- Identify friction points in lead management, forecasting, prospecting, and pipeline visibility — drive process improvements
- Lead or contribute to cross-functional initiatives to reduce manual effort and increase revenue team efficiency
- Ensure consistent execution of pipeline tracking, territory planning, lead routing, and compensation support
- Support tool adoption and new processes in partnership with Sales Enablement, including documentation and training

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 3-5 years in Revenue Operations or Sales Operations in B2B SaaS
- Strong CRM knowledge (Salesforce) and at least one sales engagement or intelligence platform (Gong, Clay, Momentum)
- AI-powered sales technologies experience or familiarity
- Naturally curious, proactive, comfortable experimenting with emerging tools
- Translate business challenges into operational or systems-level solutions
- Hands-on builder in execution-focused environments
- Experience working directly with Sales teams (SDRs, AEs, frontline managers)
- Passion for simplifying complex GTM processes

### Nice-to-Have
- None explicitly listed

## Key Concepts
- **AI-enabled sales effectiveness**: Evaluating and implementing AI tools that enhance how sales teams prospect, forecast, and manage pipeline — not building AI, but selecting and deploying it for commercial impact. The strategic lens this role uses to assess new tools.
- **GTM operational efficiency**: Systematically identifying and removing friction from lead management, territory planning, pipeline tracking, and routing. The day-to-day operational improvement work that compounds into revenue team leverage.
