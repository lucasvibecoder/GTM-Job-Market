## Summary

Payabli is a next-generation payments infrastructure and monetization platform purpose-built for vertical software companies, enabling platforms to embed, monetize, and operationalize payments through a single developer-friendly API. Backed by QED Investors, Fika Ventures, TTV Capital, and Bling Capital. This GTM Engineer role builds and maintains the technical infrastructure powering the sales motion — from prospect identification and enrichment to outreach automation and pipeline analytics. Sits at the intersection of sales operations, data engineering, and growth automation. Works directly with the Head of Sales and cross-functional department heads.

## Responsibilities

### Sales Enablement & Automation
- Build and optimize automated workflows for prospect outreach using Amplemarket, Knode.ai, and other sales engagement platforms
- Integrate and configure AI-powered sales tools (Claude integrations, Amigo AI agent)
- Create scripts and automations for prospect data enrichment, lead prioritization, and personalized outreach at scale
- Implement and maintain integrations between CRM, sales engagement platforms, and GTM tools

### Revenue Operations
- Manage and optimize sales tech stack for seamless data flow between systems
- Build dashboards and reporting for pipeline health, conversion metrics, and team performance
- Maintain data hygiene across CRM and sales tools with validation rules and cleanup processes
- Design lead scoring models, territory assignments, and routing logic

### Prospect Research & ICP Refinement
- Develop systematic approaches to identify and qualify prospects matching ICP across target verticals
- Build tools and processes for competitive intelligence gathering and market research
- Create data-driven frameworks for account prioritization and territory planning
- Analyze closed-won and closed-lost deals to continuously refine targeting criteria

### Process Optimization & Experimentation
- Identify opportunities to eliminate manual work and accelerate the sales cycle
- Run experiments on outreach sequences, messaging variations, and channel optimization
- Document processes and create training materials for sales team

### Analytics & Insights
- Build custom reports and analyses to support sales strategy and forecasting
- Track GTM metrics including pipeline velocity, conversion rates, and tool ROI
- Provide data-driven recommendations on resource allocation and strategy

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 4+ years in revenue operations, sales operations, marketing operations, or growth engineering
- Strong technical skills including APIs, SQL, and scripting (Python, JavaScript)
- Hands-on experience with modern sales engagement platforms and CRM systems (Salesforce, HubSpot, Outreach, Apollo)
- Demonstrated ability to build automations, integrations, and workflows with measurable business impact
- Data analysis skills with ability to translate metrics into actionable insights
- Self-starter mentality with bias toward experimentation
- Project management and stakeholder communication skills

### Nice-to-Have
- AI-powered sales tools and LLM integration experience
- B2B SaaS, fintech, or payments background
- Vertical SaaS business models and multi-vertical GTM strategies
- Data enrichment tools (ZoomInfo, Clearbit, Clay) and intent data platforms
- Modern data stack (Segment, Fivetran, dbt)
- Payment processing or embedded finance concepts

## Key Concepts

- **Embedded Payments Infrastructure**: Payabli's core product — a single API that lets vertical SaaS platforms natively integrate payment acceptance, issuance, and operations into their own product, making payments a revenue stream rather than a third-party handoff.
- **Amigo AI Agent**: AI-powered sales assistant tool used at Payabli alongside Claude integrations to enhance team productivity through automated prospect research and engagement workflows.
