## Summary
Giga is a voice AI startup ($61M Series A, customers include DoorDash) building real-time AI agents for enterprise customer experience across voice, chat, and email. The GTM Engineer owns the full revenue engine — tech stack architecture, outbound pipeline generation, data integrity, and AI-powered automation. High-autonomy role building from scratch at a company whose own GTM should match the precision they deliver to customers.

## Responsibilities
- Architect the GTM tech stack with seamless integrations, centralizing data in CRM and eventually data warehouse (Salesforce, Gong, Clay, Claude)
- Identify repetitive work across the GTM org and replace it with intelligent automation using APIs, webhooks, and AI agents
- Build and maintain data flows, enrichment logic, and sync architecture for accuracy and deduplication
- Partner with leadership to translate GTM strategy into automated playbooks, dynamic scoring models, and signal-based workflows
- Build the outbound engine from scratch — enrichment logic, intent triggers, AI-personalized touchpoints, and human review checkpoints wired to qualified meetings booked

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 3-5+ years in Growth, GTM Engineer, or Revenue Operations (open to technical backgrounds that have worked with GTM teams)
- Builder and systems thinker — sees manual processes and immediately thinks about the API call or AI agent that replaces them
- Comfortable with APIs, webhooks, and data integrations
- Python, JavaScript, or SQL in a professional context
- GTM funnel fluency — understands what actually converts pipeline

### Nice-to-Have
- Background at a fast-moving B2B SaaS / AI startup or agency
- Hands-on experience with CRMs and Clay, bonus for building data warehouses
- Experience designing enrichment waterfalls and managing complex data sourcing across multiple providers
- Experience centralizing selling signals from multiple data sources into a single location
- Built or deployed AI agents for GTM use cases — from ideation to realization
- Automated an outbound or inbound motion end-to-end with measurable pipeline created

## Key Concepts
- **AI-native GTM stack**: A tech stack designed from the ground up around AI capabilities rather than bolting AI onto legacy tools. Giga explicitly calls for this architecture approach.
- **Signal-based pipeline generation**: Building outbound engines triggered by intent signals rather than static lists — enrichment logic, intent triggers, and AI-personalized touchpoints feeding qualified meetings.
