## Summary

SparrowBid is a live travel marketplace connected to 2.4M hotels worldwide, using gamified auctions to help travelers unlock better deals. Product is live with early revenue. This GTM Engineer role is about building scrappy growth experiments with **zero marketing budget** — referral loops, affiliate structures, tracking frameworks, landing flows, and automation without paid tools. **Equity-only compensation until next financing round.** **Note:** LinkedIn lists this as "GTM Engineer" while the Gusher posting uses "GTM Growth Engineer."

## Responsibilities

- Build scrappy growth experiments with zero marketing budget
- Create and launch referral loops and affiliate structures
- Design tracking frameworks and landing page flows
- Wire integrations and ship experiments quickly
- Map new customer acquisition routes
- Work directly in tools to set up tracking and automation
- Collaborate closely with founders

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- Systems thinking and ability to design growth mechanics
- Comfortable working directly in tools and setting up tracking
- Ability to wire integrations and ship experiments quickly
- Creativity and speed over budget and polish

### Nice-to-Have
- Travel industry experience

## Key Concepts

- **Gamified Auctions**: SparrowBid's core mechanic — travelers bid on hotel rooms in a live auction format to unlock prices below standard booking rates, creating engagement and urgency.
- **Zero-Budget Growth Engineering**: Building acquisition systems (referral loops, affiliate structures, organic flows) entirely through technical creativity rather than paid advertising spend — the defining constraint of this role.
