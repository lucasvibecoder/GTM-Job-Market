## Summary

Redwood Software builds the first SaaS-based composable automation platform specifically built for ERP — orchestrating, managing, and monitoring workflows across any application, service, or server. This GTM Engineer role leads AI-driven tools, automations, and processes across the **marketing department** specifically. Functions as an internal consultant mapping marketing workflows into automatable steps using a combination of AI, automation platforms (Make, Workato, n8n), and point marketing AI tools (Tofu, Regie.ai, RevSure). Integrates the MarTech stack with the AI tech stack.

## Responsibilities

- Map marketing workflows and identify opportunities for automation (content creation, segmentation, reporting, QA)
- Serve as internal consultant to campaign engineers, content creators, and demand-gen leads
- Train and support marketing teams on AI best practices and responsible adoption
- Measure impact/ROI of AI tools; continuously monitor and optimize automation performance
- Evaluate build-vs-buy decisions (automation in Make.com vs. dedicated AI marketing tools like Tofu, Regie.ai)
- Build and maintain automations across systems using Make, n8n, Workato
- Design API-driven integrations between AI platforms and existing MarTech stack with attribution in mind
- Explore scalable API processes to automate bulk updates across email, forms, landing pages, and programs
- Develop prompt libraries, fine-tuned models, and reusable automation templates
- Ensure all AI-driven processes adhere to data privacy, compliance, and brand standards

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 5+ years in marketing operations, marketing technology, demand gen, or related fields with B2B GTM understanding
- Deep understanding of MarTech platforms (Salesforce, Marketo, Outreach, Mutiny)
- Marketing funnel and campaign attribution familiarity
- Experience with AI marketing software (Tofu, Regie.ai, RevSure)
- Strong grasp of generative AI (GPT, Claude, Gemini), predictive modeling, and workflow automation platforms (Make, Zapier, Workato)
- Python and JavaScript proficiency for automation scripts, data transformations, and API integrations
- API design skills (REST, GraphQL)
- SQL, JSON, and ETL pipeline familiarity
- Experience building workflows in Make, Airflow, or similar
- Strong project management and cross-functional collaboration
- Data privacy and compliance awareness

### Nice-to-Have
- Predictive modeling experience
- AI agent design and maintenance
- Fine-tuned model development
- Prompt engineering

## Key Concepts

- **Composable Automation Platform**: Modular automation architecture where individual workflow components can be assembled and reconfigured — Redwood's core product approach to ERP automation, relevant context for understanding the company's DNA.
- **Prompt Libraries**: Curated collections of tested, optimized AI prompts organized by use case (content generation, segmentation, reporting) that standardize AI output quality across the marketing team.
- **Build-vs-Buy Evaluation**: Decision framework this role owns — determining when to build custom automations in platforms like Make vs. purchasing specialized AI marketing tools like Tofu or Regie.ai.
