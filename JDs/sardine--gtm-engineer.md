## Summary
Sardine is a fraud prevention and AML compliance platform ($145M raised, investors include a16z, Visa, Experian, Google Ventures) used by 300+ banks, retailers, and fintechs. As the first GTM Engineer, you own the entire commercial infrastructure — systems architecture, workflow automation, data pipelines, and lifecycle engineering across the full lead-to-cash journey. Remote-first, high-impact role treating the internal GTM stack with production-grade rigor.

## Responsibilities
- Own the technical roadmap for the GTM tech stack (CRM, Marketing Automation, CPQ), ensuring all tools integrate into a cohesive, scalable ecosystem
- Identify manual bottlenecks across the lead-to-cash lifecycle and engineer automated solutions to reduce human-in-the-loop friction
- Design and maintain data pipelines between disparate GTM tools, ensuring a single source of truth for revenue reporting
- Partner with Product and Engineering to operationalize new features and pricing models within internal systems
- Build infrastructure for the entire customer journey — automated lead routing, scoring, programmatic renewal, and expansion workflows
- Act as primary administrator for core GTM platforms, treating configuration as code with managed permissions and technical debt
- Build logic for advanced performance dashboards translating raw system data into actionable insights on sales velocity, conversion health, and platform adoption

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 4+ years in technical Revenue Operations, GTM Engineering, or Sales Systems with emphasis on systems architecture and workflow automation
- Proven track record building complex business logic and treating internal operations as a scalable product
- Advanced experience with APIs, webhooks, and middleware (Zapier, Clay, Workato, Tray) to sync data across the revenue stack
- Deep technical expertise in Salesforce and GTM tools (HubSpot, Unify, Clay, n8n)
- Strong ability to design relational data models and map complex business processes into clean automated system logic
- Builder mentality — enjoys turning messy manual processes into scalable automated workflows
- Ability to translate non-technical requirements from GTM team into technical specifications

### Nice-to-Have
- None explicitly listed

## Key Concepts
- **Lead-to-cash lifecycle**: The full customer journey from initial lead capture through closed-won deal and revenue recognition. This role automates every stage of that flow.
- **Configuration as code**: Treating GTM platform setup (permissions, workflows, routing rules) with the same rigor as software — version-controlled, testable, and deployable across environments.
