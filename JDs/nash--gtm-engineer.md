## Summary

Nash builds logistics infrastructure powering delivery for Walmart, 7-Eleven, Urban Outfitters, Shopify, and Toast — a last-mile orchestration platform tackling a $500B industry. Founded in 2021, YC and a16z backed. This GTM Engineer owns the entire growth infrastructure: outbound engines, inbound routing, nurture tracks, CRO, demand capture (SEO, paid, social), and full-funnel reporting across Enterprise and Mid-Market segments. Extremely broad scope at 7+ YOE — closer to a GTM systems architect than a typical outbound builder.

## Responsibilities

- Build automated outbound engines scaling targeted outreach across ICPs and personas (Enterprise + Mid-Market)
- Turn signals (site activity, content consumption, intent) into automated actions
- Own nurture tracks across early interest, evaluation, rollout, and expansion
- Lead CRO across key touchpoints using structured experiments
- Own demand capture: SEO, paid search, targeted social, retargeting
- Build inbound routing, scoring, and follow-up systems
- Create ADR-ready systems: cadences, messaging, triggers, follow-up logic
- Build partner-driven demand loops
- Build full-funnel reporting exposing friction and opportunity
- Test new channels and double down on what drives pipeline

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 7+ years in growth marketing, growth PM, marketing engineering, revenue engineering, or GTM systems roles
- Proven ability to build outbound, inbound, nurture, and CRO programs end-to-end
- Comfortable with Clay, Apollo, HubSpot, Customer.io, Webflow, and adjacent tools
- Fluency in automation, scoring, segmentation, and full-funnel analysis
- Experience supporting ADR/SDR workflows
- High ownership and pragmatic execution
- Comfortable experimenting with small budgets and iterating quickly

### Nice-to-Have
- Logistics, commerce infrastructure, or AI exposure

## Key Concepts

- **Last-mile orchestration**: Coordinating the final delivery leg from warehouse/store to customer across multiple courier services. Nash's core product — unifying checkout, courier management, and post-purchase experience into one platform rather than custom API integrations per provider.
- **ADR-ready systems**: Pre-built cadences, messaging templates, triggers, and follow-up logic that Account Development Reps can activate immediately. The GTM Engineer builds the infrastructure so ADRs execute plays rather than building them from scratch.
