## Summary

Wrapbook is a production payroll and accounting platform for the entertainment industry, backed by a16z and WndrCo ($130M raised, 250+ people). This GTM Engineer (Pre-Sale) sits within Revenue Operations and builds the systems that power demand generation and conversion — turning real GTM motions into durable, production-ready Salesforce workflows. Heavy Salesforce engineering (Flows, custom objects, Apex), partners with a dedicated GTM Systems Architect and a corresponding Post-Sale GTM Engineer to maintain end-to-end lifecycle integrity.

## Responsibilities

- Design and maintain pre-sale GTM workflows in Salesforce — routing, scoring, qualification, handoffs, and deal support
- Implement lead management and enrichment workflows in Salesforce (lifecycle state, enrichment timing, routing impact, data quality checks)
- Translate GTM processes and motions into scalable, inspectable workflows with Marketing and Sales Operations
- Configure Salesforce using Flows, custom objects, validation rules, reports, dashboards, and light development
- Partner with GTM Systems Architect on system design implementation and execution feedback
- Partner with SFDC Systems Engineer on complex development, integrations, and platform-level changes
- Implement automation and AI-assisted workflows with guardrails and human-in-the-loop controls
- Establish monitoring and validation mechanisms for high-impact GTM workflows
- Ensure Salesforce configurations remain secure, auditable, and reliable at scale
- Partner with GTM Engineer (Post-Sale) for clean handoffs, shared data models, and end-to-end lifecycle integrity

## Requirements

### Must-Have

- 4-6+ years in Salesforce administration, business systems, RevOps, or GTM operations engineering
- Strong hands-on Salesforce configuration (Flows, custom objects, reports, dashboards)
- Salesforce Administrator certification strongly preferred
- Working familiarity with Apex, APIs, and integrations
- Experience supporting Marketing and Sales teams in B2B SaaS
- Strong understanding of demand generation, pipeline creation, and pre-sale GTM workflows
- Experience designing workflows that are reliable, observable, and easy to operate at scale
- Systems thinking and bias toward replacing manual work with scalable processes

### Nice-to-Have

- Experience in fintech, payroll, or regulated environments

## Key Concepts

- **Pre-Sale/Post-Sale GTM Split**: Wrapbook structures its GTM Engineering as two paired roles — one owns demand generation through deal close, the other owns onboarding through delivery. They share data models and handoff protocols, ensuring lifecycle integrity without one person owning too broad a scope.
- **Production-Ready Salesforce Workflows**: Treating Salesforce automation with engineering rigor — durable, failure-resilient, monitored, and auditable. Not just "it works" but "it works at scale, fails gracefully, and is observable."
