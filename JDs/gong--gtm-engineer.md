## Summary

Gong (5,000+ customers, Revenue AI Operating System) is hiring a GTM Engineer to build customer-facing intelligent workflows that create an agentic customer journey. Uses Workato, Retool, LLMs, and Gong to design, test, and deploy scalable automations that elevate the customer experience. Partners across Outcomes, Customer Success, RevOps, and Product. Chicago-based, remote-friendly.

> **Title note:** Listed as "GTM Engineer" in the JD body, but LinkedIn URL slug reads "systems-engineer-cx-automation." Both titles capture the role — it's a systems/automation engineer focused on customer-facing GTM workflows.

## Responsibilities

- Act as technical expert for building and maintaining automation projects serving customers
- Design and implement agentic workflows using Workato, Retool, LLMs, and Gong — scalable, repeatable, actionable automations
- Build and fine-tune AI infrastructure — integrations with GTM systems, data retrieval, rapid scaling
- Serve as technical expert for integrating and testing pre-release and new product functionality
- Successful technical builds may become prototypes for future core product features
- Partner cross-functionally to validate technical requirements, test prototypes, ensure seamless deployment

## Requirements

### Must-Have

- 3+ years hands-on experience in Business Systems Integration, Sales Engineering, Solutions Engineering, or similar technical GTM role
- Automation expertise — proven track record building and deploying automations with Workato, n8n, or Retool
- Passion for technology — curious, eager to test and learn through experimentation
- Systems architecture — understand data movement across complex systems, design for integrity and performance

### Nice-to-Have

- None listed

## Key Concepts

- **Agentic Workflows**: AI-driven automation sequences that act autonomously on behalf of the business — delivering timely information, resources, and calls-to-action to customers without manual intervention. Goes beyond simple automation into intelligent, context-aware actions.
- **Revenue AI Operating System**: Gong's platform that unifies customer interaction data (calls, emails, deals) with AI to provide insights, automate workflows, and guide revenue teams. This role builds the automation layer on top of that system.
