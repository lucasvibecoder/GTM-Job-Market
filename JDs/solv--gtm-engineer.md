## Summary

Solv (solvhealth.com) is a healthcare tech company making healthcare accessible, transparent, and effortless for consumers, partnering with forward-thinking providers. This is their **first GTM & Revenue Engineer** — owns the technical infrastructure powering how Solv closes and retains customers across the full funnel (first touch through renewal and expansion). Reports to Senior Director of Marketing & Strategy, measured by pipeline generated, revenue influenced, and operational leverage. **Title note**: LinkedIn lists as "GTM Engineer"; JD body uses "first GTM & Revenue Engineer" — both titles in play. **Notably Claude-native**: the role explicitly requires Claude and Claude Code experience, and their GTM stack "runs on Claude" with connectors into HubSpot, Glean, Granola, Slack, and Drive. No Clay mentioned.

## Responsibilities

### GTM Infrastructure & Automation
- Design and deploy agentic workflows that automate outbound prospecting, lead enrichment, account research, and follow-up sequences.
- Design and scale the GTM tech stack (runs on Claude, connected to HubSpot, Drive, Glean, Granola, Slack).
- Identify manual, high-friction GTM processes and replace with scalable, AI-native solutions.
- Instrument everything: build dashboards and reporting for pipeline health, conversion trends, and campaign attribution.

### Outbound Pipeline Engine
- Build account prioritization models (propensity scoring, ICP fit, intent signals) to focus outbound effort.
- Architect multi-channel outbound sequences combining personalized copy, enriched signals, and coordinated timing.
- Extract signal from Solv's proprietary industry data to generate narratives for thought leadership and ABM.
- Own the feedback loop: analyze what's working, kill what isn't, iterate fast.

### GTM Enablement Infrastructure
- Build tools giving Sales reps real-time access to account intelligence, competitive positioning, and ROI benchmarks.
- Create scalable, templatized assets (pitch frameworks, ROI models, battlecards) reps can customize and deploy.
- Translate product releases into deployable sales plays.
- Build workflows supporting retention and expansion with Customer Success (renewal prep, churn signals, CSM intelligence).
- Ship internal web apps (deal calculators, account health dashboards, ROI snapshots).

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 3–6 years in a GTM, sales ops, revenue ops, or marketing engineering role at a B2B SaaS company.
- Hands-on experience architecting AI-powered GTM systems with Claude.
- Comfortable owning a technical project end-to-end — scoping, shipping, iterating.
- Builder mindset — more energized by shipping a working automation than writing a strategy doc.
- AI-native — Claude, GPT, Perplexity, and agentic frameworks are already core to how you work.
- Claude Code-comfortable — can build and deploy real tools independently without traditional coding background.
- CRM-literate — worked inside Salesforce or HubSpot at the data model level.
- GTM-fluent — understands outbound, deal execution, retention, and what data matters.
- High-agency — scopes own problems, moves without perfect information, ships fast.

### Nice-to-Have
- None explicitly stated.

## Key Concepts

- **Claude-Native GTM Stack**: Solv runs its GTM infrastructure on Claude (Anthropic's AI model) rather than traditional automation platforms like Clay or n8n — using Claude's connectors to integrate with HubSpot, Glean, Granola, Slack, and Drive. A signal that some companies are building GTM workflows directly on LLM APIs rather than dedicated GTM tools.
- **Propensity Scoring**: Statistical models that predict how likely a target account is to convert, used here to prioritize outbound effort on accounts with the highest probability of becoming customers.
- **Internal Web Apps as GTM Tools**: Building custom web applications (deal calculators, account health dashboards, ROI snapshots) that reduce the sales team's dependency on ad-hoc requests — a more engineering-forward approach than configuring off-the-shelf tools.
