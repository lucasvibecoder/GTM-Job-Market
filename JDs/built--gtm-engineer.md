## Summary

Built is the AI-powered platform transforming how real estate is financed, developed, and managed — a comprehensive operating system for the $1.5T construction/real estate industry. Series D stage with investment from Citi, powering 86,000 active projects valued at $300B+ across 350+ lenders and 80,000+ borrowers. This GTM Engineer role designs, operates, and continuously improves the systems and campaigns driving lender pipeline and revenue. Not just building GTM infrastructure — actively running it day to day. Owns campaign execution in HubSpot, automation workflows via Clay/AirOps/n8n, and GTM performance reporting. High-ownership, hands-on role defining how GTM is executed at Built.

## Responsibilities

### Campaign Execution & GTM Operations
- Own end-to-end campaign execution for the lender business in HubSpot (email, lifecycle, nurture, paid ads, routing, lead management)
- Build, launch, measure, and optimize campaigns based on performance and pipeline impact
- Own day-to-day operation and optimization of the GTM tech stack (HubSpot, Clay, AirOps, Lemlist)
- Build and maintain lead scoring models, routing logic, lifecycle stages, and SLAs
- Ensure data quality, attribution accuracy, and system reliability across GTM systems
- Partner with Marketing, Sales, and Product Marketing to operationalize GTM strategy

### Automation and AI Workflows
- Design, build, and maintain automated workflows using n8n, Lemlist, Clay, and AirOps
- Build AI-powered enrichment, outbound, and intent-detection pipelines
- Integrate GTM tools and data sources via APIs and automation platforms
- Replace manual processes with scalable, reliable workflows
- Rapidly prototype, test, and iterate on GTM experiments

### Analytics, Enablement & Operating Models
- Own marketing attribution, pipeline analytics, and GTM performance reporting
- Build and maintain dashboards and automated reporting pipelines (Salesforce, HubSpot, Sheets, Looker)
- Create playbooks, SOPs, and documentation for GTM systems, campaigns, and workflows
- Train and enable Marketing and Revenue teams on tools, processes, and best practices
- Establish guardrails and operating models for adoption, consistency, and trust
- Act as internal owner for "how we run Marketing GTM"

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 5+ years in Marketing Ops, Revenue Ops, or Go-To-Market / Growth Engineering
- Hands-on experience executing and optimizing campaigns end-to-end in HubSpot
- Strong experience with GTM automation and enrichment tools (Clay, AirOps, n8n)
- Salesforce proficiency (reports, dashboards, data structure)
- Strong API and integration experience across GTM systems
- Analytical fluency (SQL, advanced spreadsheets, or both)
- Strong systems thinking and ownership mindset
- Comfort operating without a fully defined playbook

### Nice-to-Have
- ABM platforms (6sense, Demandbase, or similar)
- Python or JavaScript for custom scripts and integrations
- AI/LLM tools for GTM workflows or data processing
- B2B SaaS experience in fintech or construction tech

## Key Concepts

- **Construction Draw Management**: Built's origin product — managing the process of releasing loan funds in stages as construction milestones are completed, replacing manual inspection and approval workflows with a digital platform.
- **Lender GTM Engine**: The specific go-to-market motion this role owns — targeting and converting the 350+ financial institutions that use Built to manage construction and real estate lending, distinct from the borrower/contractor side of the marketplace.
