## Summary
Monograph is a firm performance management platform for architecture and engineering practices. The Marketing Operations Manager owns systems, processes, and analytics powering demand generation and revenue pipeline. Reports to VP of Operations, partners closely with Marketing team and collaborates with Revenue Ops and Sales. Strategic, hands-on role blending systems thinking with execution.

## Responsibilities
- Partner with Sales and Marketing leadership to design, implement, optimize full lead-to-revenue funnel
- Own marketing performance data: dashboards, campaign analysis, pipeline and revenue trends
- Manage and improve tech stack (HubSpot, Salesforce, LinkedIn, Clay, AI tools) ensuring data integrity
- Partner with Revenue Ops and Demand Gen to optimize lead flow, campaign attribution, full funnel reporting
- Build automations, lead scoring models, and workflows for speed-to-lead and conversion
- Lead cross-departmental projects improving GTM efficiency, pipeline hygiene, lifecycle management
- Champion reporting, tracking, and communication best practices between Marketing and Sales

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 5+ years in Marketing Ops, Revenue Ops, or Growth Ops (SaaS)
- Hands-on Salesforce CRM and HubSpot experience
- Other marketing tools (Gong, Clay, Dreamdata, Webflow)
- SQL proficiency for data analysis and validation
- GTM strategy alignment with Sales partner experience
- Dashboard, workflow, and forecasting model building/optimization
- Analytical skills, data-driven insight → action
- Cross-functional communication and project management

### Nice-to-Have
- JavaScript experience

## Key Concepts
- **Lead-to-revenue funnel optimization**: Designing and measuring the full path from first touch to closed revenue. This role owns the instrumentation and optimization of that entire journey.
- **Marketing performance as product**: Treating dashboards, workflows, and scoring models as products that need design, iteration, and stakeholder buy-in.
