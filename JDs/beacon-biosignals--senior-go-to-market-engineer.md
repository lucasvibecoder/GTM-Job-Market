## Summary
Beacon Biosignals is a precision medicine platform for the brain — FDA-cleared EEG headband with AI algorithms, ~100K patient database, and cloud-native analytics for clinical trials. The Senior GTM Engineer is the first full-time Revenue Operations hire, reporting to the CBO with end-to-end ownership of Beacon's GTM systems across Marketing, Business Development, Program Management, Account Management, and Clinical Operations. Remote with quarterly travel to Boston/NYC.

## Responsibilities
- Be the primary steward of the GTM tech stack (CRM, engagement, analytics) — architect, document, and evolve how tools fit together
- Design, prototype, and scale automation and AI-enabled workflows across demand, pipeline acceleration, expansion, and retention motions
- Build dashboards, attribution frameworks, executive reporting, and pipeline analytics
- Create data models (scoring, propensity) and translate them into operational workflows
- Serve as bridge between commercial teams and technology — help non-technical stakeholders adopt and extend GTM systems
- Partner with BD, Finance, and Legal to support deal tracking, contracts, PO/invoicing, revenue recognition, renewals, and billing flows
- Embed commercial systems into program management and clinical trial delivery (contract tracking, handoff workflows, reporting)
- Continuously scan for bottlenecks — own diagnostic sprints, build pilots, iterate on workflows

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 5+ years in RevOps, Growth Ops, or Business Operations in a tech company
- Strong CRM expertise (HubSpot, Salesforce) — dashboards, workflow automation, integrations, data integrity
- SQL, Python or scripting / API integration skills
- Data modeling, interpretation, dashboarding, attribution, forecasting
- Strong project management across multiple stakeholders and competing priorities
- Translate complex logic for business stakeholders clearly
- Entrepreneurial mindset with bias toward simplicity, self-service, and scalability

### Nice-to-Have
- Healthcare, clinical trials, diagnostics, or regulated environment experience
- AI/LLMs and prompt engineering applied to commercial operations workflows
- Enterprise high-value sales cycle experience

## Key Concepts
- **Signal-based triggers and proactive play sequencing**: Automated workflows that fire when specific buying or engagement signals are detected, triggering pre-built outreach or internal action sequences. The AI-enabled automation layer this role designs across demand and pipeline motions.
- **Clinical-commercial system embedding**: Integrating GTM systems into clinical trial delivery — contract tracking, handoff workflows between commercial and clinical teams, reporting flows. Unique to healthcare/biotech GTM where the sales cycle extends into complex delivery operations.
