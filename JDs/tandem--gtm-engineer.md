## Summary

**Tandem** is rethinking how new therapies reach market — an AI-first platform that supports thousands of new patients daily, spreads via doctor word-of-mouth, and partners with the largest biopharma companies. Backed by Thrive Capital, General Catalyst, and Accel. This **GTM Engineer** builds the data infrastructure, integrations, and automation powering sales, marketing, and account ops. Write production code, build internal tools, embed AI into workflows, and run experiments on activation flows. Explicitly framed as founding the GTM engineering function.

## Responsibilities

- Build infrastructure that powers network growth — core GTM systems (HubSpot, Monday), AI agents, data integrations, and lightweight workflows
- Develop technical solutions that remove friction across the go-to-market motion, including self-serve flows, activation tooling, and data instrumentation
- Work closely with product and operations to identify bottlenecks and build tools that unlock faster, higher-quality adoption
- Embed AI tooling into GTM workflows and evaluate new tools and integrations to accelerate outbound, GTM intelligence, and follow-up automation
- Design and run experiments on onboarding and activation flows to optimize conversion and reduce friction
- Lay the foundation for a go-to-market engineering function over time

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- Strong programming skills and general Computer Science knowledge
- Familiarity with GTM automation tooling (Clay, HubSpot, n8n)
- Experience driving GTM engineering projects end-to-end in fast-paced, client-driven environments
- Strong product instincts — knows what's valuable, what's shippable, and how to translate between the two
- Comfort working with go-to-market and ops teams to solve real user problems
- Track record of moving quickly, finding shortcuts, and going to unreasonable lengths to deliver

### Nice-to-Have
- None listed

## Key Concepts

- **Two-Sided Network Effects**: A platform dynamic where each additional user on one side (e.g., doctors) makes the platform more valuable to the other side (e.g., biopharma companies), creating compounding growth that's hard to replicate.
- **Activation Tooling**: Internal tools and flows designed to get new users to their first meaningful action faster — reducing time-to-value and improving conversion from signup to active use.
- **GTM Intelligence**: Using data signals, enrichment, and AI to surface which accounts are most likely to convert, what messaging resonates, and when to engage — replacing gut-feel outbound with data-driven targeting.
