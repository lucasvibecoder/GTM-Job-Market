## Summary

Jungle Scout is the leading all-in-one platform for selling on Amazon, supporting $50B+ in annual Amazon revenue. This GTM Engineer sits at the intersection of Revenue Operations, Data, Marketing, and Sales — with a core focus on training, evaluating, and improving AI-driven analytical models through structured output reviews, feedback loops, and quality control. Heavy data engineering role: advanced SQL, data warehousing, ETL, model validation, BI tools. Chicago-based, hybrid 3 days.

## Responsibilities

- Design and implement structured output review processes to validate model and analytics accuracy
- Perform systematic quality audits on segmentation models, forecasting outputs, attribution models, and AI-driven insights
- Develop feedback loops to retrain and refine models based on performance data and business outcomes
- Establish quality benchmarks and monitoring dashboards for model performance
- Partner with Data and Product teams to improve training datasets and reduce bias, drift, and inconsistencies
- Write and optimize advanced SQL queries to support modeling, reporting, and validation workflows
- Manage and maintain relational databases and data warehouse structures
- Design scalable data models that support GTM analytics and operational systems
- Build and maintain data transformations and QA checks across pipelines
- Improve system interoperability across CRM, marketing automation, billing, and product analytics tools
- Support territory planning, lead scoring, segmentation, and pipeline forecasting
- Build reporting frameworks that drive strategic decision-making

## Requirements

### Must-Have

- Advanced SQL proficiency (complex joins, window functions, aggregations, query optimization)
- Strong understanding of relational databases and data warehousing concepts
- Experience with data modeling and ETL processes
- Familiarity with CRM and marketing automation platforms (Salesforce, HubSpot, Marketo)
- Experience working with BI tools (Looker, Tableau, Sigma)
- Exposure to model validation, performance monitoring, or AI/ML quality frameworks
- Deep understanding of SaaS go-to-market processes across Marketing, Sales, and Customer Success
- Strong understanding of SaaS metrics (ARR, CAC, LTV, churn, conversion rates, pipeline coverage)
- Strong attention to detail and commitment to data integrity

### Nice-to-Have

- Experience in B2B SaaS or ecommerce
- Python or scripting experience for automation and data validation
- Experience working with product usage data
- Familiarity with experimentation frameworks (A/B testing, cohort analysis)

## Key Concepts

- **Model Drift**: When a predictive model's performance degrades over time because the data patterns it was trained on have changed. This role monitors for drift in segmentation, forecasting, and attribution models, and builds feedback loops to retrain them.
- **ETL (Extract, Transform, Load)**: The process of pulling data from multiple sources, cleaning/transforming it, and loading it into a data warehouse for analysis. This role designs and maintains these pipelines across GTM systems.
