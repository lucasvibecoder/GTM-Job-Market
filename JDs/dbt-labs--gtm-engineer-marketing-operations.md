## Summary
dbt Labs is the pioneer of analytics engineering, powering data transformations for 90,000+ teams weekly with $100M+ ARR. The GTM Engineer, Marketing Operations owns a 15+ tool marketing stack and builds AI-powered agents and workflows (LLMs, MCP Servers) to accelerate customer acquisition across the entire GTM org. High-seniority role (6+ YOE) that bridges Marketing, Sales, and Data teams with a heavy automation and AI mandate.

## Responsibilities
- Streamline and optimize marketing processes including campaign creation and audience segmentation
- Partner with Marketing, Marketing Intelligence, and Sales Operations to scope AI-driven solutions and technology adoption
- Integrate AI APIs, MCP Servers, and automation tools to build agents and workflows for Marketing's GTM motions
- Own AI utilization strategy to drive optimization, productivity, and cost savings
- Architect, build, and deploy AI prototypes and scalable automated workflows integrating LLMs and AI APIs
- Create and maintain technical documentation and training materials for operational best practices
- Own and manage a 15+ Marketing tooling suite including integrations, optimizations, and access management
- Maintain a forward-looking tooling roadmap with vendor management and administration
- Partner with SDR and Sales leaders on lead funnel management, routing, processing, SLA management, and scoring frameworks

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 6+ years in Marketing Ops or Revenue Ops in high-growth SaaS
- Expert-level Salesforce and HubSpot experience
- Hands-on with data orchestration and automation platforms (Clay, Workato)
- Experience owning a multi-tool mar tech stack with vendor evaluation and management
- Proven track record applying AI or advanced automation to solve complex business problems (LLMs, RAG, prompt engineering)
- Exceptional communication and executive presence for technical and non-technical stakeholders
- Builder and practical innovator mindset
- Highly analytical and process-driven
- Proficiency in SQL or BI visualization tools (Tableau, Looker, Power BI, Omni)
- Experience with lead management infrastructure (routing logic, scoring models, SLA frameworks)

### Nice-to-Have
- Proficiency in Python or JavaScript/TypeScript for automation scripts and API requests

## Key Concepts
- **MCP Servers**: Model Context Protocol servers — standardized interfaces that let AI models connect to external tools and data sources. dbt wants this role to integrate MCP into marketing workflows.
- **Analytics Engineering**: The discipline of transforming raw data into clean, reliable datasets for analysis. dbt Labs created this category and their platform is the industry standard.
- **Data Control Plane**: dbt's framework for governing data transformations at scale — ensuring data quality, lineage, and access control across the enterprise.
