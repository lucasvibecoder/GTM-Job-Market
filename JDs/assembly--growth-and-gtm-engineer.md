Assembly (assembly.com) is hiring its first Growth & GTM Engineer to work at the intersection of engineering, product, and marketing — reporting directly to the CEO. In-office in NYC, $139K-$180K. Company product not described in the JD; company_type classified as vertical-saas based on available context and may need verification.

- Code and optimize onboarding flows, referrals, viral loops, and templates
