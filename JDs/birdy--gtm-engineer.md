## Summary

Birdy (iqbirdy.com) designs, builds, and operates revenue systems for B2B startups from Seed to Series A — not a lead gen agency, but a full GTM infrastructure shop. This GTM Engineer will architect and implement outbound and RevOps systems from scratch to help founders find PMF and scale. Core work: Clay enrichment workflows, Instantly deliverability infrastructure, CRM architecture (HubSpot/Salesforce), and Zapier automation. **Opportunity to grow into Head of GTM Engineering.**

## Responsibilities

- Architect Clay workflows: design multi-source enrichment and automated data loops to find hyper-specific intent signals
- Build outbound infrastructure: set up and optimize technical deliverability (SPF, DKIM, DMARC) and domain clusters using Instantly
- Engineer CRM systems: own lifecycle design and lead routing in HubSpot or Salesforce to eliminate manual friction
- Drive signal velocity: use Zapier automation and funnel math to move founders from manual grind to a predictable engine

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- Clay power user — master of API enrichments and conditional logic
- Technical deliverability expert — maintain primary inbox placement at scale
- CRM architect — strong experience setting up HubSpot or Salesforce from scratch
- Systems thinker — views GTM as a technical product, not a sales task
- Independent operator — moves from strategy to execution without hand-holding

### Nice-to-Have
- None explicitly stated

## Key Concepts

- **Signal Velocity**: The speed at which buying signals are detected, enriched, and routed to action — Birdy frames this as the core metric for converting manual founder-led sales into a predictable engine.
- **Domain Clustering**: Running outbound email from multiple related domains (rather than a single domain) to distribute sending volume, protect primary domain reputation, and maintain deliverability at scale.
