## Summary
Iru is an AI-powered security and IT platform ($100M raised at $850M valuation) backed by General Catalyst and Tiger Global, serving companies like Notion, Cursor, and Replit. The Senior GTM Engineer is a technical builder role reporting to the Head of GTM Operations — designing and shipping pipeline generation engines, scoring systems, internal tools, and AI-powered revenue programs across HubSpot, Clay, Apollo, and n8n. Explicitly not a systems admin or campaign execution role.

## Responsibilities
- Build net-new GTM capabilities across HubSpot, Clay, Apollo, Orum, n8n using APIs, webhooks, and custom code
- Design and launch pipeline generation programs driven by signals, enrichment, timing, intent, and AI-based decisioning
- Build scoring, routing, prioritization, and handoff systems across leads, accounts, opportunities, partners, and customer lifecycle signals
- Create internal tools and lightweight applications for AEs, SDRs, SEs, CSMs, and GTM leaders
- Develop AI-powered workflows for research, personalization, meeting prep, follow-up, account planning, and expansion identification
- Connect product, usage, security, compliance, and CRM data into GTM workflows
- Build advanced models and logic for segmentation, lifecycle management, territory design, capacity planning, and revenue prioritization
- Identify high-friction manual parts of the revenue engine and replace with scalable systems
- Own technical architecture, QA, monitoring, documentation, and long-term maintainability
- Advise on build-versus-buy decisions with a bias toward durable solutions

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 5+ years in a technical GTM role (GTM Engineering, Technical RevOps, Growth Engineering, Forward Deployed Engineering, or similar) in B2B SaaS
- Strong Python and SQL skills for scripting, data work, API calls, and internal tool building
- Strong hands-on experience with HubSpot or comparable CRM and automation ecosystem
- Experience building production workflows with Clay or comparable enrichment and orchestration tooling
- Experience building custom integrations via APIs, webhooks, and workflow orchestration platforms
- Familiarity with LLM APIs and AI workflow design for research, classification, personalization, and structured outputs
- Track record of shipping automations, tools, or applications that materially improved GTM execution
- Strong commercial judgment and user empathy
- High standards for reliability, documentation, data governance, and security

### Nice-to-Have
- None explicitly listed

## Key Concepts
- **Pipeline generation engine**: A fully automated or semi-autonomous system that identifies target accounts, applies fit and timing logic, enriches contacts, prioritizes outreach, and routes opportunities — the flagship project this role would own.
- **Signal-based decisioning**: Using buyer intent, engagement events, product usage, and lifecycle data to trigger and prioritize GTM actions rather than relying on static lists or manual review.
- **Forward Deployed Engineering**: A technical role embedded within a business function (not core engineering), building tools and systems tailored to revenue team needs — how Iru frames this position.
