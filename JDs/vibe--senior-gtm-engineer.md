## Summary
Vibe.co is a CTV advertising platform aiming to 2.5x revenue in 2026. The Senior GTM Engineer sits at the intersection of engineering, growth, marketing, and revenue operations — designing, building, and shipping internal products and automations that make GTM teams dramatically more efficient. Deeply technical with outsized business impact.

## Responsibilities
- Build end-to-end GTM systems that support acquisition, activation, and retention
- Partner with Sales, RevOps, Growth, and Marketing to translate pain points into technical solutions
- Own data logic and workflows for scoring, routing, segmentation, reporting, and automation
- Prototype quickly and iterate with stakeholders to validate what moves KPIs
- Design internal tools and processes for sales team efficiency
- Improve data quality, definitions, and reliability across GTM stack
- Ship solutions quickly, measure outcomes, refine based on performance
- Expand automation capabilities into outbound

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 3+ years technical experience in engineering, analytics engineering, growth engineering, or technical RevOps
- Strong SQL proficiency, comfortable in modern data warehouse
- Workflow automation (n8n, Zapier, Make)
- Daily AI tool usage (Claude, ChatGPT, Cursor, Claude Code)
- Builder mindset — shipped internal tools, automated processes, built scaled systems
- Modern GTM systems understanding (CRM logic, scoring, workflows)
- High velocity and autonomy
- Product mindset — impact over architecture
- Clear communication, translates business needs to technical designs

### Nice-to-Have
- Fast-paced, high-growth environment experience

## Key Concepts
- **AI-native development workflow**: Daily use of Claude Code, Cursor, ChatGPT as development accelerators. This role expects AI fluency as table-stakes.
- **Internal product engineering**: Building internal tools and automations AS products — with user research (stakeholder needs), iteration, and performance measurement.
