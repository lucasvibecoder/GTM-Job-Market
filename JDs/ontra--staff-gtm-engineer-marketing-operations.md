## Summary

Ontra builds AI-powered solutions for private markets, automating contract workflows, compliance, and entity management for 1,000+ global GPs and investment banks. This Staff GTM Engineer reports to the Director of Marketing Operations and owns the design and build of AI-powered GTM workflows — systematizing outreach and enrichment across the funnel, developing AI models/agents/prompts, and running data-driven experiments to fuel acquisition and growth.

## Responsibilities

- Design and build AI-powered GTM workflows that scale acquisition, activation, and pipeline — replacing manual processes with durable, reusable systems
- Partner with Sales, Marketing, RevOps, and Product teams to identify GTM inefficiencies and translate business needs into scalable technical solutions
- Build and maintain reliable integrations between AI platforms and core GTM systems, ensuring clean data flow and operational continuity
- Design, run, and analyze GTM experiments (outbound, routing, enrichment) to refine workflows and inform scaling decisions
- Develop, test, and refine AI models, agents, and prompts for accuracy and reliability in real-world GTM use cases
- Implement data validation, error handling, monitoring, and logging for stable, observable AI-powered workflows at scale
- Document workflows, integrations, and patterns to enable GTM teams to move faster with reusable tools

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 8+ years in GTM Engineering, Marketing Ops Engineering, RevOps Engineering, or similar AI/automation-focused roles
- APIs, webhooks, and SaaS integrations experience
- Scripting or programming (JavaScript/TypeScript or Python) for custom logic and transformations
- Hands-on ownership of GTM platforms (Salesforce, Marketo) at a system/integration level
- Workflow automation with Clay, Zapier/Make, or similar enrichment and automation platforms
- Practical AI/LLM-powered solution implementation (ChatGPT API or similar) within GTM workflows
- GTM experiment design and data-driven measurement with SQL
- B2B SaaS cross-functional experience (Sales, Marketing, RevOps, Product, Data)

### Nice-to-Have
- No explicit nice-to-haves listed

## Key Concepts

- **System Reliability & Observability**: Engineering practices (validation, error handling, monitoring, logging) that ensure automated workflows are stable, debuggable, and trustworthy at scale — borrowed from software engineering and applied to GTM systems.
- **AI Model & Prompt Development**: The iterative process of building, testing, and refining AI models and prompt templates to improve output quality and consistency in production GTM workflows.
- **GTM Enablement**: Reducing manual work for revenue teams by providing documented, scalable, reusable automation tools and patterns they can self-serve.
