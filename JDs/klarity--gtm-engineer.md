## Summary
Klarity is an AI-native company operationalizing AI internally and delivering it to customers. The GTM Engineer is the foundational hire — building the revenue operating system from scratch. Owns Salesforce architecture, pipeline workflows, forecasting systems, deal acceleration tooling, and post-sale lifecycle. Operates as both product manager and engineer for the GTM org, working directly with executives and GTM leadership. High visibility, high leverage, genuine ownership.

## Responsibilities
- Build and maintain systems powering revenue execution: opportunity management, pipeline workflows, forecasting infrastructure, deal desk handoffs, post-sale workflows
- Own Salesforce architecture and development — data models, automation, validation rules, triggers, integrations
- Own internal GTM "products" end-to-end: problem discovery, system design, build, rollout, adoption, iteration
- Partner with Sales, Marketing, Customer Success, and GTM Operations leadership
- Build AI-forward automations — next-best actions, timely follow-ups, routing, enrichment, prioritization
- Experiment with AI agents, workflows, and lightweight tooling
- Integrate and orchestrate tools across Marketing, Sales, CS, and Finance into a cohesive GTM operating system
- Build and maintain reliable data pipelines for data quality, reporting trust, and operational consistency
- Support programmatic GTM motions: outbound, enrichment, account-based workflows, lifecycle automation
- Define, instrument, and own key GTM system metrics (deal velocity, conversion by stage, forecast accuracy, rep productivity)

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 4+ years experience building end-to-end systems
- Strong Salesforce experience (admin + development) — data models, automation, validation rules, triggers, integrations
- Strong SQL skills and experience with data modeling, ETL/ELT
- Integrate and orchestrate systems using no-code tools (Zapier, Make, n8n, Clay), data platforms (dbt, Airflow, Fivetran), or custom APIs/scripting
- Work with non-technical stakeholders and translate ambiguous business problems into technical solutions
- Builder mindset — curiosity, ownership, ship quickly and iterate
- Genuine excitement about AI in GTM workflows

### Nice-to-Have
- None explicitly listed

## Key Concepts
- **Revenue operating system**: The complete technical infrastructure that powers how a company sells — from pipeline workflows and forecasting to deal desk handoffs and post-sale lifecycle. What this role builds from the ground up, treating GTM systems as internal products.
- **GTM product management**: Treating internal GTM systems like products — with problem discovery, design, build, rollout, adoption tracking, and iteration cycles. The operating model this role uses, blending engineering execution with product thinking.
