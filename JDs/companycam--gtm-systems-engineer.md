## Summary
CompanyCam builds a photo documentation and productivity app for contractors across commercial and home services industries. The GTM Systems Engineer designs and scales automation infrastructure across sales, marketing, and customer success — blending CRM expertise, AI-powered workflow development, and data fluency. This is a builder role: architect scalable systems, not just maintain them.

## Responsibilities
- Design and implement automation improving GTM team efficiency and effectiveness
- Build AI-powered workflows, prompt frameworks, and automation logic using modern LLM platforms and orchestration tools
- Develop intelligent routing, territory assignment, and operational workflows within CRM and automation platforms
- Test, measure, and optimize automation performance for measurable business impact
- Administer and enhance the Salesforce ecosystem with advanced automation, flows, and custom functionality
- Support integrations across the GTM tech stack including marketing automation, enrichment platforms, CPQ, and contract management tools
- Design scalable workflows for lead management, opportunity management, and customer lifecycle processes
- Reduce manual work across marketing, sales, and customer success through system design and automation
- Partner with cross-functional teams to translate business requirements into scalable technical solutions
- Prototype and evaluate new tools, AI platforms, and automation technologies
- Maintain system documentation, governance standards, and data integrity across the GTM tech ecosystem
- Build reporting frameworks and dashboards for funnel performance, automation effectiveness, and system adoption

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 4+ years in GTM Systems, Revenue Operations, Sales Operations, Marketing Operations, or similar
- Strong hands-on Salesforce (or similar CRM) administration and optimization
- Experience designing automation workflows across multiple GTM systems
- Technical fluency with APIs, integrations, and system orchestration tools
- Experience building or experimenting with AI-powered workflows using LLM APIs (OpenAI, Anthropic)
- Analytical and problem-solving skills; ability to translate business needs into technical solutions
- Cross-functional partnership experience with sales, marketing, customer success, and operations
- Bachelor's degree in CS, IS, Engineering, Business, or related field

### Nice-to-Have
- None explicitly listed

## Key Concepts
- **AI-powered GTM automation**: Using LLM APIs and orchestration tools to build intelligent workflows that replace manual processes across the revenue org — the core technical capability this role requires.
- **GTM tech stack architecture**: Designing the interconnected system of CRM, marketing automation, enrichment, CPQ, and contract management tools that powers the go-to-market organization.
