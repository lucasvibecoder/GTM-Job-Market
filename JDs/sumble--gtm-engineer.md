## Summary

Sumble is an account intelligence and signals platform that maps every account, person, and job post globally to uncover insights other data vendors can't provide — for example, identifying every US company running a Databricks data migration and pinpointing the exact team leading it. Backed by a $38.5M Series A (Coatue, Canaan), team of 20+ engineers from Google, Meta, Snowflake, and Rippling. Scaled to millions in revenue **without a marketing department** — now building the growth infrastructure to match the product. This GTM Engineer role is code-heavy ("you'll for sure be writing code"), building on a monorepo, creating automations that route Sumble's own signal data into outbound sequences, dashboards, internal tooling, and growth experiments. Building this function from scratch.

## Responsibilities

- Build the GTM stack: own selection, implementation, and integration of marketing and sales tools (CRM, enrichment, sequencing, attribution, analytics)
- Build signal-to-pipeline automations using Sumble's own data (e.g., target account posts matching buying signal → auto-enrich, score, and route to rep with context)
- Instrument end-to-end marketing attribution and build dashboards/reporting for marketing and sales teams
- Build campaign infrastructure: landing pages, lead capture, nurture sequences, A/B testing frameworks, personalization logic
- Prototype lightweight internal tools (scripts, dashboards, Slack bots) for the revenue team — competitive intel alerts, account research auto-briefs powered by Sumble data
- Own CRM and marketing data integrity: enrichment pipelines, deduplication logic, lifecycle stage definitions
- Run growth experiments across channels: outbound sequences, landing page variants, lead-scoring models

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- Some prior experience in GTM engineering, growth engineering, marketing ops, or revenue ops at a B2B SaaS company
- Hands-on experience with CRM platforms (Salesforce or HubSpot) and marketing automation tools
- Experience building integrations, automations, or internal tools using APIs and workflow platforms (Zapier, Make, n8n, Tray, or custom scripts)
- US time zone based

### Nice-to-Have
- Strong opinions on tools like Clay, Apollo, HubSpot, Salesforce
- Modern GTM/RevOps knowledge

## Key Concepts

- **Signal-to-Pipeline Automation**: Core mechanic of this role — when Sumble's data detects a buying signal (e.g., a job post indicating a technology initiative), the system automatically enriches the account, scores it, and routes it to the right rep with full context, eliminating manual research.
- **Buying Signal from Job Posts**: Sumble's unique data advantage — analyzing job postings globally to infer what technology projects companies are running, which serves as a leading indicator of purchase intent for the products those projects require.
