## Summary
Crux is a capital markets platform for clean energy financing ($77M raised from a16z, Lowercarbon Capital, profitable and growing). The GTM Engineer builds and scales technical infrastructure across all internal operations — the intersection of engineering and scaling businesses. Owns tech stack implementation, custom integrations, automation workflows, and AI/ML optimization.

## Responsibilities
- Build automation infrastructure using Zapier, n8n, Make, and custom API integrations
- Implement AI-powered tools for cross-functional business solutions
- Design custom integrations: APIs, webhooks, data connectors between tools (HubSpot, Omni, InCycle) and internal systems
- Partner cross-functionally to align marketing infrastructure with product development and growth strategies

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- Full-stack growth technologist: front-end tracking and analytics to back-end data processing and automation
- API and integration expert: hands-on building integrations between marketing tools, CRMs, internal systems (REST APIs, webhooks, data transformation)
- AI/automation enthusiasm: implemented AI-powered tools for lead scoring, content optimization, process automation
- Business-minded builder: conversion funnels, attribution models, technical infrastructure → revenue impact
- Startup builder: fast-paced environments, scalable solutions, feedback iteration, ownership of critical infrastructure
- Analytical thinker: data-driven validation, identifies metrics that matter
- Problem solver: operates with ownership in unclear swim lanes, scopes fuzzy requests
