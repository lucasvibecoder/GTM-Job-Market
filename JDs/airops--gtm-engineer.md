## Summary
AirOps is the first end-to-end content engineering platform for AI-era marketing (backed by Greylock, Unusual Ventures). The GTM Engineer is their first hire for this role — owns the complete outbound motion from signal identification and data enrichment to personalized messaging and multi-channel campaign execution. Works across Growth, Sales, and Sales Development teams.

## Responsibilities
- Manage and optimize complete outbound tech stack: Clay workflows, Smartlead campaigns, enrichment tools
- Ensure deliverability, data quality, and system performance
- Build sophisticated Clay tables that identify buying signals, enrich prospect data, trigger personalized outreach at scale
- Design and launch outbound campaigns with personalized messaging based on account-specific triggers
- Partner with Sales and Growth to design, execute, and analyze experiments
- Continuously assess new tools and AI-driven solutions for conversion improvement

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 2+ years in Sales Ops, Revenue Ops, Marketing Ops, Analytics, BI, or similar technical GTM role
- Clay proficiency: complex multi-step workflows combining enrichment, signal detection, personalized outreach at scale
- Outbound email tools (Smartlead, Instantly) — deliverability, warm-up, campaign optimization
- Technical mindset: APIs, webhooks, data integrations, automation tools
- Systems thinking: sees tool connections, bottlenecks, scalable processes
- Data-driven approach to measuring impact and iterating
- Ownership mentality: built things from scratch
- AI tool curiosity

### Nice-to-Have
- Former founder or early-stage startup experience
- Familiarity with Leadfeeder, Unify, Warmly, Amplemarket, Apollo, Trigify, Fibbler, Dripify, LinkedIn Sales Navigator
- Internal tools or custom integrations experience
- Light coding (Python, SQL, JavaScript)

## Key Concepts
- **Signal-driven outbound**: Building Clay tables that detect buying signals and auto-trigger personalized outreach. Goes beyond static list building.
- **Content engineering platform**: AirOps helps marketers create content that earns AI citations. The GTM Engineer powers the pipeline that sells this platform.
