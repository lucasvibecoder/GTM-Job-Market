## Summary
Beautiful.ai is an AI-powered presentation platform used by companies like SAP, HP, and Rakuten. The GTM Engineer sits at the intersection of growth, marketing, sales, and automation — actively generating qualified pipeline (not just routing leads). Role spans outbound campaign execution, enrichment workflows, lead routing, and dashboard reporting across a broad tech stack.

## Responsibilities
- Manage and operate the GTM tool stack
- Build and automate workflows
- Build pipeline through Smart Campaigns
- Launch and support outbound campaigns (email, LinkedIn, sequencing)
- Build segmented target lists using Apollo, Common Room, Clay, and other enrichment tools
- Create workflows that automate personalization at scale
- Partner with marketing to run experiments across inbound and outbound channels
- Help create campaign messaging and content (email copy, landing page inputs, enablement materials)
- General pipeline reporting and analysis
- Build lead routing, enrichment, and lifecycle workflows in Salesforce and HubSpot
- Use Zapier, n8n, and Clay to automate prospect research and outbound personalization
- Implement AI-powered scoring and segmentation models
- Reduce manual SDR work through smart automation
- Monitor campaign performance (conversion rates, speed-to-lead, reply rates)
- Build dashboards tracking pipeline generation by source and segment
- Identify drop-offs in the funnel and run experiments to fix them

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 1-3 years in Growth, RevOps, Sales Ops, Marketing Ops, or a technical GTM role
- Background in marketing or sales with understanding of how pipeline gets built
- Comfortable writing campaign copy and thinking about messaging
- Strong in Salesforce (flows, reporting, objects)
- Working knowledge of SQL
- Hands-on with automation tools (Zapier, n8n, Make)
- Curious about AI for efficiency and personalization

### Nice-to-Have
- None explicitly listed

## Key Concepts
- **Smart Campaigns**: Automated outbound sequences that combine enrichment data, segmentation logic, and personalization to generate pipeline without manual SDR effort.
- **Speed-to-lead**: The time between a prospect expressing interest and a sales rep making contact. A key metric this role optimizes through automation.
