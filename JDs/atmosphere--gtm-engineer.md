## Summary
Atmosphere is a streaming TV platform for commercial venues (60,000+ bars, restaurants, gyms, airports) with 150M+ monthly viewers. The GTM Engineer designs and builds the systems, automations, and AI-powered workflows that power venue acquisition at scale — cold email outreach, onboarding automation, A/B testing, and dashboards. Code-heavy builder role (Python, SQL, JS) reporting to SVP of Platform Sales & Marketing.

## Responsibilities
- Engineer scalable growth solutions — integrations, automations, and tools for venue acquisition, onboarding, and customer support
- Leverage AI coding agents and modern development tools to rapidly prototype and deploy GTM experiments
- Analyze complex data sets to identify growth opportunities and build dashboards
- Build and scale cold email systems for venue outreach globally
- Design and implement A/B tests, attribution models, and performance tracking systems
- Build custom tools and workflows that enhance team productivity and campaign effectiveness
- Create technical solutions for global market expansion, venue onboarding automation, and business retention
- Collaborate across departments to translate business requirements into technical solutions
- Drive data-driven experimentation to optimize venue acquisition funnels and reduce onboarding costs

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- Bachelor's degree or equivalent in Engineering, CS, Data, or related technical field
- 2-4 years in engineering, data analysis, or technical roles with Python, SQL, JavaScript proficiency
- Sales ops, marketing ops, revenue ops, or growth experience with measurable results
- Comfortable using AI coding assistants and modern development tools
- Strong understanding of digital marketing, lead generation, attribution, and conversion optimization
- Experience integrating CRM, marketing automation, analytics, and GTM tools (Salesforce, HubSpot, Segment)
- Complex dataset analysis, reporting, and translating insights into strategy
- Self-starter who identifies and executes independently
- Cross-functional communication of technical concepts

### Nice-to-Have
- Streaming media, AdTech, or B2B SaaS platform experience
- Cold email platforms (Instantly), lead enrichment tools (Clay), CRM systems (Salesforce, HubSpot)
- Product marketing or technical product management background
- Modern web technologies and APIs
- Building internal tools or customer-facing applications
- Machine learning or predictive analytics

## Key Concepts
- **Venue acquisition funnel**: The pipeline from identifying a potential commercial location to getting Atmosphere's platform installed and activated. This role optimizes every stage through automation and experimentation.
- **AI coding agents**: Using AI-assisted development tools (like Claude Code, Cursor, etc.) to rapidly build and iterate on GTM solutions. Explicitly called out as a core working method, not just a nice-to-have.
