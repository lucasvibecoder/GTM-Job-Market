## Summary
Agency posting from William and William Recruiting for an undisclosed Sequoia-backed AI company. The role owns the full outbound infrastructure — stack decisions, ICP models, enrichment logic, BDR enablement — reporting directly to the Head of Growth. Based in NYC or SF, 5 days in-office. The client has strong Fortune 500 inbound but needs a repeatable outbound motion built from scratch.

## Responsibilities
- Design and build the outbound system from current foundation up: prospecting pipelines, enrichment workflows, automation logic
- Equip BDR team with tooling and playbooks that make them faster
- Build ICP and scoring models so outbound effort lands on accounts that can close
- Own the GTM tech stack: evaluate, implement, cut what isn't working
- Track every stage of the outbound funnel and drive improvement through iteration
- Work directly with Growth, Sales, and Product to keep outbound calibrated to market signals

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 3-7 years in GTM engineering, RevOps, or closely adjacent function
- Hands-on experience with Clay or equivalent enrichment tooling
- Comfortable writing SQL or automation scripts without asking engineering for help
- Based in NYC or SF, in-office five days a week
- B2B startup experience (Series B+), seen a mature outbound system from the inside
- AI-native in how you work — have built personal projects that demonstrate it
- Opinionated about the stack and can explain tool choices for specific problems

### Nice-to-Have
- None explicitly listed

## Key Concepts
- **ICP scoring model**: A data-driven framework that ranks accounts by likelihood to buy, ensuring outbound effort focuses on highest-value targets rather than spray-and-pray.
- **Outbound infrastructure**: The full technical system — from data enrichment to sequencing to lead handoff — that powers repeatable, scalable pipeline generation.
