## Summary

Proof is the world's first identity-assured transaction management platform, developed by the team behind Notarize — pioneering the first online real estate closing, mortgage closing, auto sale, and will since 2015. This GTM Engineer role sits on the Revenue Operations team to design, build, and operate the systems that generate, prioritize, and activate pipeline. Hands-on builder role with **clear weekly output expectations** (2 Clay playbooks/week, 35% lead-to-meeting conversion) and direct pipeline contribution ownership. Reports to Sr. Manager of Revenue Operations. **Note:** Salary range ($100K-$140K) is described as "total cash compensation" including base pay and commissions — marked as OTE.

## Responsibilities

- Design and build end-to-end workflows for prospect research, enrichment, prioritization, sequencing, and follow-up
- Create and maintain Clay playbooks leveraging ICP definitions, buying signals, intent data, past engagement, and closed-lost history
- Use signals from customer data, conversations, and activity to inform personalization and account prioritization at scale
- Partner with Revenue Marketing to translate campaign themes and positioning into outbound automations aligned to GTM priorities
- Continuously refine workflows based on performance data
- Stay current on emerging AI, automation, and GTM technology offerings
- Help shape what an AI-first GTM motion looks like in practice

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- Current or former AE, SDR, BDR, or outbound-focused seller with deep prospecting understanding
- 2+ years building multi-step workflows in Clay, Zapier, Make, or similar
- Conditional logic, loops, and data mapping experience
- Motivation to redesign GTM work using AI, automation, and system thinking
- Ability to experiment, measure results, and iterate quickly
- Strong system thinking across tools
- Technical fluency with Clay, AI models, enrichment logic, and automation tooling
- SQL and query building for segmentation, filtering, and signal creation
- API and webhook configuration proficiency
- ICP modeling, buyer personas, and account prioritization logic
- Prompt creation for prospect research and personalization at scale

### Nice-to-Have
- Growth mindset to evolve from executing GTM motions to designing systems

## Key Concepts

- **Clay Playbook**: A reusable, multi-step Clay workflow template combining data enrichment, scoring, and outreach triggers — this role is measured on deploying a minimum of two new playbooks per week.
- **Closed-Lost History**: Using data from deals that didn't close to inform future targeting — identifying patterns in why prospects churned from pipeline to avoid repeating mistakes and potentially reactivate dormant opportunities.
