## Summary

Finout is a cloud cost management platform for engineering and FinOps teams ($85M raised, 100+ people, customers include Wiz, Lyft, NYT, SiriusXM). This GTM Engineer architects the "unified decision engine" for revenue operations — building data pipelines across Clay/HubSpot/Salesforce, deploying autonomous AI agents for lead research and outbound personalization, and bridging the gap between Growth's experimentation velocity and Ops' governance requirements. Requires 2+ years building AI agents/LLM workflows. Hybrid in Midtown Manhattan (3 days/week).

## Responsibilities

- Architect complex data pipelines across the GTM stack (Clay, HubSpot, Salesforce) — ensuring data intelligently flows to inform decisions
- Design and implement autonomous AI agents using Python/LLMs for lead research, outbound personalization, and signal processing
- Engineer the decisioning layer that determines whether a prospect gets a personalized email, an ad, or a human call
- Serve as technical translator between Growth team's high-velocity experiments and Ops team's governance requirements
- Build API connectors and scripts that enable speed without breaking data hygiene
- Manage advanced enrichment waterfalls in Clay to surface high-intent signals and keep CRM data actionable

## Requirements

### Must-Have

- 2+ years of experience in AI implementation (specifically building agents/LLM workflows)
- Python proficiency and building API connectors or scripts to move data between platforms
- Deep familiarity with Clay, HubSpot, Salesforce, and Zapier
- Builder mentality — solving "impossible" data problems and building systems from the ground up
- Ability to balance high-speed experimentation with data hygiene and governance

### Nice-to-Have

- None listed

## Key Concepts

- **Decisioning Layer**: The automated logic that determines what action a prospect receives (personalized email, ad, or human call) based on signals, scores, and enrichment data — replacing manual rep judgment with scalable, data-driven routing.
- **Enrichment Waterfall**: A cascading sequence of data enrichment sources (typically in Clay) where each layer fills gaps left by the previous one — ensuring maximum coverage and signal density before a lead enters the CRM or outbound sequence.
