## Summary

DualEntry (founded 2024, $100M+ raised from Lightspeed/Khosla/Google Ventures) builds an AI-native ERP that lets accounting teams automate manual data entry — making the one-person finance team a reality. This GTM Engineer builds one of the best-in-world AI outbound engines from the ground up. Owns and optimizes the full GTM/RevOps setup across HubSpot and outbound tools. In-office NYC. Intense, high-ownership role.

## Responsibilities

- Own and continuously improve GTM and RevOps setup across HubSpot and outbound tools
- Build and iterate on cold email and LinkedIn sequences (copy, targeting, sequencing)
- Set up and optimize outbound workflows using Smartlead, HeyReach, and Clay
- Write, test, and refine outbound copy for email and LinkedIn with focus on booking meetings
- Define and maintain lead tracking, routing logic, and lifecycle stages in HubSpot across inbound, outbound, and LinkedIn
- Identify where leads drop off (forms, ads, partial bookings) and implement automated follow-ups
- Maintain clean, enriched data using Apollo, FullEnrich, RB2B, and Nooks

## Requirements

### Must-Have

- 2+ years in GTM Engineer, RevOps, Sales Ops, or Marketing Ops
- Strong hands-on HubSpot experience (workflows, lifecycle stages, routing, reporting)
- Proven experience building and managing outbound sequences end-to-end
- Confidence writing, testing, and optimizing outbound copy
- Practical experience with Clay, Smartlead, HeyReach, Apollo, FullEnrich, RB2B, Demandbase, or similar
- Strong understanding of lead tracking, attribution, and follow-up workflows
- High attention to detail and data quality mindset

### Nice-to-Have

- Experience in fintech, accounting, finance, or developer tools

## Key Concepts

- **AI-Native ERP**: Enterprise Resource Planning software built from the ground up with AI at the core — automating data entry, reconciliation, and reporting that legacy ERPs (built in the 1990s) still require manually. DualEntry's core product disrupting a $220B market.
- **Outbound Copy Optimization**: The practice of systematically writing, A/B testing, and refining cold email and LinkedIn messaging to maximize reply rates and meeting bookings. A core hands-on responsibility in this role, not delegated to marketing.
