## Summary
Suger is a Series A revenue platform ($Y Combinator, Craft Ventures, Intel Capital) helping B2B companies sell through cloud marketplaces (AWS, Azure, GCP). 200+ customers including Snowflake and Glean. The GTM Engineer is the technical backbone of the growth team — building, connecting, and automating tools that turn data into pipeline actions. This is a code-heavy, engineering-focused role.

## Responsibilities
- Own and operate the GTM automation engine: scrape, integrate, transform data into real-time actions across email, SDR outreach, web engagement
- Manage a technical GTM backlog against OKR-driven roadmap
- Build outbound triggers from high-intent signals through CRM and outreach tools
- Leverage OpenAI and third-party APIs for personalized content and automation at scale
- Write queries and build dashboards to uncover growth opportunities
- Develop internal tools and scripts (Python, SQL, JavaScript)
- Maintain and extend GTM integrations (Salesforce, Apollo, Segment, Clearbit)

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 2-5 years in data engineering, growth engineering, or applied data science
- Fluent in Python and SQL
- Bonus: JavaScript, APIs, scraping tools (Puppeteer, Playwright)
- OpenAI API experience for content automation or enrichment
- Obsessed with speed: ideas to prototypes quickly
- Understanding of GTM motions and pipeline generation
- Thrives in zero-to-one startup environments

### Nice-to-Have
- RevOps, martech, or sales tech experience
- Cloud marketplace ecosystems (AWS, Azure, GCP)

## Key Concepts
- **GTM automation engine**: A system that scrapes, integrates, and transforms data into automated actions across email, SDR, and web channels. Beyond campaigns — this is infrastructure.
- **Cloud marketplace GTM**: Selling through AWS/Azure/GCP marketplaces is a specific B2B motion. Understanding this ecosystem is a differentiator.
