## Summary
GlossGenius is a fintech/SMB platform powering 100,000+ small businesses with booking, marketing, analytics, and payment processing. The GTM Engineer architects AI-powered sales technology — building LLM workflows for lead enrichment and personalization, piping product usage signals from Snowflake/BigQuery into actionable sales triggers, and creating internal tools and Slack bots using Python and APIs. Reports to Senior Manager, Revenue Operations.

## Responsibilities
- Design and deploy LLM-powered solutions for lead enrichment, automated personalization, and real-time sales coaching
- Pipe high-intent product usage signals from Snowflake/BigQuery into actionable sales triggers
- Build and maintain custom integrations between CRM, sales engagement platforms, and internal tools using Python and APIs
- Develop code-based lead routing logic to match leads with reps at moments of high intent
- Create internal apps or Slack bots for instant access to customer health scores and upsell opportunities

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 4+ years in a technical role — Forward Deployed Engineering, Solutions Architecture, or Technical RevOps
- Advanced Python and SQL skills; comfortable scripting and interfacing with REST APIs
- Hands-on experience with OpenAI API, LangChain, or similar frameworks
- Prompt engineering for high-quality, structured output
- Deep understanding of where in the sales funnel reps lose time, with a track record of building solutions

### Nice-to-Have
- Salesforce Advanced Admin/Apex knowledge
- Node.js
- Snowflake and Fivetran
- Outreach/SalesLoft, Gong, ZoomInfo, Clearbit

## Key Concepts
- **Product-led sales signals**: High-intent behavioral data from product usage (feature adoption, usage spikes) piped from a data warehouse into CRM as actionable triggers for the sales team.
- **Real-time sales coaching**: AI-generated contextual guidance — like automated battlecards triggered by competitor mentions in Gong — delivered to reps during or immediately after conversations.
