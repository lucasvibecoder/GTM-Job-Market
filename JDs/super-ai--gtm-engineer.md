## Summary

Super.AI builds an Intelligent Document Processing (IDP) platform combining LLMs, OCR, and human-in-the-loop systems for enterprises in shipping, logistics, financial services, and insurance. This GTM Engineer owns the full go-to-market motion — from ICP definition and segmentation through positioning, outbound execution, and pipeline analytics. Acts as the connective tissue between Product, Sales, and Marketing. Denver-based, hybrid.

> **Title note:** Listed as "GTM Engineer" on LinkedIn, but the JD body describes the role as "GTM Engineer Leader." Both labels are relevant — the scope is strategic and cross-functional beyond a typical individual contributor GTM Engineer.

## Responsibilities

- Own and evolve GTM strategy across ICP definition, segmentation, messaging, and channel prioritization
- Translate product capabilities into sharp, differentiated positioning, value propositions, and sales narratives
- Build and maintain account segmentation frameworks using firmographics, technographics, intent data, and behavioral signals
- Design and execute outbound and event-driven GTM plays in collaboration with Sales
- Partner with Sales to enable better discovery, demos, and deal progression through tighter messaging
- Work hands-on with GTM tooling (CRM, enrichment, intent, automation) to improve speed, accuracy, and scale
- Analyze pipeline and funnel data to identify what's working and where to double down
- Own all GTM metrics reporting — historical, present, and future
- Act as connective tissue between Product, Sales, and Marketing to keep feedback loops tight

## Requirements

### Must-Have

- 3-6+ years in GTM, growth, product marketing, sales strategy, or revenue operations at B2B SaaS
- Strong understanding of enterprise buying motions, especially in operations, finance, or document-heavy workflows
- Hands-on experience with CRM systems (HubSpot preferred), enrichment tools, and GTM analytics
- Strong GTM Metrics Reporting skills and experience
- Ability to synthesize messy inputs into crisp narratives
- Strong written and verbal communication with bias toward clarity and simplicity
- High ownership mindset, good judgment, comfort operating with limited direction
- Experience working closely with Sales teams on real deals
- Must be based in Denver, CO area (hybrid)

### Nice-to-Have

- None listed

## Key Concepts

- **Intelligent Document Processing (IDP)**: AI-powered technology that automates extraction, classification, and processing of data from complex documents — combining LLMs, OCR, and human review. Super.AI's core product, serving enterprises that handle high volumes of document-centric workflows.
- **Account Segmentation Framework**: A structured system for categorizing target accounts using firmographic data (company size, industry), technographic data (tech stack), intent signals (buying behavior), and behavioral signals (engagement). Determines which accounts to prioritize for outbound.
