## Summary
Orchestry is a SaaS company in the Microsoft 365 ecosystem providing governance, lifecycle management, and automation for digital workplaces. The GTM Engineer (Sales) designs, builds, and executes systems powering outbound and pipeline generation — sits at the intersection of Sales, Marketing, and Systems. Works alongside Head of Sales and AEs. **Note:** Compensation is listed as OTE $110K-$140K CAD (Clay metadata says USD, but JD body specifies CAD). Remote-first despite San Francisco listing.

## Responsibilities
- Build and launch multi-channel outbound campaigns across email, LinkedIn, and other formats using Instantly, HeyReach, Clay, and Claude Code
- Partner with Sales to identify ICP segments, priority accounts, and messaging strategies
- Continuously test subject lines, sequencing logic, personalization depth, and channel mix
- Manage outbound deliverability fundamentals (domain setup, inbox rotation, sender reputation)
- Design and maintain scalable enrichment and targeting workflows in Clay (waterfall enrichment, integrations, routing logic, structured table architecture)
- Own data flow into HubSpot (contact enrichment, lifecycle stage management, segmentation, campaign attribution)
- Integrate and optimize tooling across the GTM stack to eliminate manual work
- Leverage AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, GPT workflows) to automate research, personalization, and repetitive tasks
- Build high-fit account and contact lists aligned to ICP using structured targeting and enrichment workflows
- Improve CRM hygiene and data reliability
- Develop repeatable "list → enrich → sequence → measure → optimize" workflows
- Track campaign performance and identify bottlenecks
- Document processes and playbooks for team-wide scalability

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 4+ years in growth, outbound, RevOps, GTM, sales operations, or analytical role in SaaS
- Strong hands-on expertise with modern outbound tools (Clay, Claude Code, Instantly, HeyReach, Apollo)
- Deep understanding of modern outbound infrastructure (sequencing, deliverability, inbox strategy, LinkedIn automation)
- Experience working in HubSpot with segmentation, lifecycle stages, and reporting
- Strong written communication for compelling outbound messaging
- Highly organized and execution-focused with ability to run multiple campaigns simultaneously
- Strong ownership mindset, thrives in ambiguity and operates independently

### Nice-to-Have
- Experience targeting IT, Security, or Microsoft 365 personas
- Familiarity with Microsoft 365 (SharePoint, Teams, Entra ID, Purview, Power Platform) and governance/admin landscape
- Early-stage or high-growth B2B SaaS experience
- Customer-facing or sales experience
- Light scripting, APIs, or automation tools (Zapier, Make, n8n)
- AI tools to accelerate GTM workflows

## Key Concepts
- **Waterfall enrichment**: Running a lead through multiple data providers in sequence — if Provider A doesn't return a result, try Provider B, then C. Maximizes coverage while controlling cost. Core Clay workflow pattern.
- **Outbound deliverability infrastructure**: The technical foundation that determines whether outbound emails actually reach inboxes — domain setup, inbox rotation, warm-up, sender reputation management. Without this, campaigns fail regardless of messaging quality.
