## Summary

RemoteHunter (recruiting firm) is hiring on behalf of an unnamed identity verification and risk management company in fintech. The client has verified hundreds of millions of identities via real-time APIs, initially in financial services, and was first to implement eCBSV technology (profile strongly suggests Socure). The GTM Engineer owns the technical infrastructure supporting revenue teams — building systems, workflows, and AI-driven tools for targeted outreach to banks, fintechs, and financial institutions. Described as a "pioneering position with high visibility" offering ownership over scalable solutions that directly impact growth.

## Responsibilities

- Design and maintain tools and processes that enhance the GTM function.
- Integrate and build a cohesive GTM technology stack.
- Identify and implement automation across the sales funnel.
- Develop tools to discover and present revenue opportunities.
- Collaborate with GTM teams including Sales, Partner Success, and Marketing.
- Translate business needs into scalable technical solutions.
- Analyze and improve GTM processes and analytics proactively.

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 4+ years in GTM systems, revenue operations, or software/product engineering.
- Practical experience with CRMs, revenue forecasting, and internal tools for non-technical users.
- Proficiency in SQL.
- Skilled in AI tooling and large language models (LLMs).
- Experience building AI agents.
- Ability to rapidly prototype and deploy solutions.
- Proven experience working with Sales, Partner Success, and Marketing teams.
- Strong ownership and adaptability in early-stage projects.

### Nice-to-Have
- Background in fintech, fraud, identity, or regulated B2B.
- Salesforce experience.
- Fluency in Python and/or JavaScript.
- Clay Sales Intelligence experience.

## Key Concepts

- **eCBSV (Electronic Consent Based Social Security Number Verification)**: A system allowing financial institutions to verify SSNs electronically with consent — the client company was the first to implement this, indicating deep identity verification infrastructure.
- **GTM Technology Stack Ownership**: Full responsibility for selecting, integrating, and maintaining the set of tools that power go-to-market operations — from CRM to AI agents to automation workflows.
