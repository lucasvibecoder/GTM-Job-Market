## Summary
Lightfield is an AI-native CRM (backed by Greylock, Lightspeed, Coatue) that assembles itself from email, calendar, and meetings. Previously built Tome (25M+ users). The GTM Engineer designs go-to-market systems, processes, and tech stack from the ground up with Lightfield at the core. Reports directly to Head of GTM. Mission-critical role shaping both the GTM engine and the product itself.

## Responsibilities
- Build and evolve GTM automation engine for campaign performance, pipeline generation, trial conversion, customer referrals
- Design and implement GTM tech stack — evaluate and configure new technology
- Own warm outbound strategy: signal-based campaigns using Clay and Unify
- Architect and implement personalized customer journeys across the full lifecycle
- Build growth instrumentation to track GTM performance
- Identify and design automated workflows for internal operations (custom GPTs, social listening agents, alerting)
- Dogfood Lightfield as primary GTM system — turn needs into product requirements and work with engineering

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- Track record at fast-paced early-stage company
- Passion for AI and GTM tech — early adopter, tinkerer, hacker
- Exceptional cross-functional collaboration
- Technical proficiency: APIs, webhooks, automation (Zapier, n8n), data (SQL, events, tracking)

### Nice-to-Have
- Clay experience

## Key Concepts
- **Warm outbound**: Signal-based campaigns that engage prospects showing buying signals — warmer than pure cold outbound but still proactive. Using Clay and Unify to detect and act on signals.
- **Product-shaping GTM role**: Using the company's own product (Lightfield CRM) as the primary GTM system and feeding user needs directly into product development. GTM Engineer as product stakeholder.
