## Summary

**Mumba Technologies** is a technology services firm. Despite the "GTM Marketing Engineer" title, this is a **marketing manager role** — owns campaign planning/execution, events, webinars, content, and demand gen across digital and outbound channels. No engineering, automation building, or code involved. Works directly with company leadership to translate business goals into marketing programs. Title is GTM-adjacent at best.

## Responsibilities

- Translate business objectives into clear marketing plans and campaigns
- Own campaign execution across digital, outbound, inbound, and event channels
- Prioritize initiatives based on ROI, impact, and available resources
- Build and manage a marketing calendar (campaigns, webinars, events)
- Define KPIs tied to pipeline, revenue, and growth
- Track performance across channels (email, paid, organic, events, webinars)
- Plan and execute integrated campaigns (email, LinkedIn, ads, content)
- Partner with sales to support pipeline creation and account-based efforts
- Manage CRM and marketing automation workflows (HubSpot, Salesforce)
- Own end-to-end webinar execution (topics, speakers, promotion, follow-up)
- Plan and manage conference participation (booths, speaking sessions, outreach)
- Develop messaging aligned to customer pain points and solutions
- Oversee creation of campaign assets (emails, landing pages, decks, one-pagers)

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 3-10+ years of experience in B2B marketing
- Strong execution skills — able to plan and do
- Data-driven mindset with comfort in dashboards, metrics, and attribution
- Experience running campaigns, webinars, and/or events
- Strong communication skills and comfort working with leadership
- Ability to operate independently and own outcomes

### Nice-to-Have
- Experience in SaaS, technology services, or B2B software
- Familiarity with Clay, HubSpot, or similar CRM/automation tools
- Experience supporting sales and pipeline-driven growth
- Conference and webinar management experience
- Account-based marketing (ABM) exposure

## Key Concepts

- **Account-Based Marketing (ABM)**: A strategy that focuses marketing resources on a defined set of target accounts rather than broad audience campaigns — aligning sales and marketing around specific companies with personalized messaging and outreach.
- **Marketing Attribution**: Tracking which marketing touchpoints (ads, emails, events, content) contribute to pipeline and revenue, used to measure ROI and allocate budget to the highest-performing channels.
