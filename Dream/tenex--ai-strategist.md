## Summary
Tenex positions itself as "Chief AI Officer for mid-market and enterprise companies" — a full-stack strategic technology partner that identifies highest-ROI AI opportunities, sets transformation strategy, and executes through technology and change management. The AI Strategist is the voice of the client — translating goals into reality by uncovering bottlenecks, aligning stakeholders, and executing AI-driven implementation plans.

## Responsibilities
- Lead AI Strategy Audits: 2-4 week diagnostics to map workflows, quantify pain points, surface data assets, prioritize highest-ROI AI use-cases with phased roadmap
- Orchestrate Product-Strategy Sprints: customer interviews, data capture, rapid prototyping, implementation planning
- Pair with Forward-Deployed Engineers to stand up automated/AI-driven workflows using SaaS, low-code platforms, or internal tools
- Design and run training workshops for Change Management and Enablement — turn demos into daily habits
- Stakeholder Management: engage C-suite, line-of-business owners, and end-users
- Thought Leadership: publish playbooks, speak at industry events, position Tenex as "McKinsey for AI"

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- Communication skills of a top strategic seller (confident orator, active listener, consultative relationship-builder)
- High IQ + even higher motivation
- Highly organized, detail-oriented, proactive problem-solver
- Comfortable exploring new technologies, managing multiple projects in fast-paced startup
- Translate executive-level problems → structured AI solutions with measurable ROI
- Bridge business strategy and technical execution (execs + engineers)
- Execution-oriented: ambiguity → outcomes → repeatable playbooks

### Nice-to-Have
- Management consulting experience
- Early-stage startup experience
- Client engagement management
- Engineering team management
