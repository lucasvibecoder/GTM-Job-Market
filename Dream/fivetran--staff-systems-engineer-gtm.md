## Summary

Fivetran ($5.6B valuation) makes data access as simple as electricity — automated data integration into warehouses with no engineering required. This Staff Systems Engineer, GTM owns the Salesforce CRM ecosystem for a rapidly scaling global business. Deep Salesforce engineering: Apex, LWC, CPQ, enterprise integrations, team leadership. Requires Salesforce Developer I & II certifications. Hybrid 2 days/week, Denver or Oakland.

> **Title note:** Listed as "Staff Systems Engineer, GTM" — a senior Salesforce engineering title, not a GTM Engineer variant. Marked `gtm_adjacent`. The "GTM" qualifier indicates this engineer serves the go-to-market organization specifically.

## Responsibilities

- Define and drive technical strategy for Salesforce CRM and related systems aligned with business objectives
- Lead design and implementation of scalable, secure solutions integrating with enterprise systems
- Spearhead process automation and data integration strategic initiatives
- Mentor and develop a team of engineers, promote best practices for coding, design, and architecture
- Address high-impact, complex technical challenges for reliability, performance, and scalability
- Establish and enforce governance frameworks for compliance and data privacy
- Own the Salesforce ecosystem fully — enhancements, optimizations, primary point of contact for critical issues

## Key Concepts

- **IPaaS (Integration Platform as a Service)**: Cloud-based middleware that connects enterprise applications through pre-built connectors and APIs — enabling data to flow between Salesforce and other business systems without custom point-to-point integrations. A core architectural pattern for this role.
- **Lightning Web Components (LWC)**: Salesforce's modern UI framework for building custom components using standard web technologies (HTML, JavaScript). Replaces older Visualforce pages with faster, more maintainable interfaces.
