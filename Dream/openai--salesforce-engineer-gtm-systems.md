## Summary

OpenAI's Enterprise Platform team enables the GTM org (Sales, Solutions, CS, Support, Marketing, Partnerships) to deploy AI products across customer businesses. This Salesforce Engineer role partners with Financial Engineering, Finance Platform Technology, and Order Management to build order-to-cash solutions — order-capture objects, fulfillment triggers, tax/billing integrations, SKU governance — all in Salesforce. **This is a deep Salesforce engineering role, not a GTM Engineer variant**, but it sits squarely inside OpenAI's GTM systems org and informs what "GTM infrastructure at scale" looks like at an AI leader.

## Responsibilities

- Build order-to-cash solutions in Salesforce: order-capture objects, fulfillment triggers, financial approval workflows, and entitlement logic from opportunity close through invoicing and revenue recognition
- Engineer integrations connecting Salesforce to ERP, tax engines, billing platforms, CLM, and e-signature tools across the revenue lifecycle
- Implement scoped technical outcomes: translate requirements into production-ready flows, validation rules, Platform Events, and Apex code
- Own QA for new builds, resolve production issues, and maintain documentation aligned with Copado-driven change management, SOX/SOC2 controls, and deployment best practices
- Partner cross-functionally with FinEng, Fin Systems, Order Management, Sales Ops, Billing, and Legal Ops on SKUs, pricebooks, and workflow compliance

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- Advanced Salesforce development (Apex, async patterns, APIs) and data model expertise across opportunity, quote, order, and billing domains
- Proven experience designing robust integrations using REST, Platform Events, and data sync patterns between Salesforce and external systems
- QTC and finance-system experience: CPQ, order-management objects, product catalog governance, consumption- vs subscription-based pricing, tax/billing integrations, ERP data reconciliation
- Execution and quality focus: build, test, and deploy scoped solutions in a regulated, change-controlled environment with audit-ready documentation
- Cross-functional communication skills partnering with finance and operations teams at scale

### Nice-to-Have
- Salesforce certifications (Platform Developer I/II, Application Architect)
- Prior experience in AI-driven or high-growth SaaS businesses

## Key Concepts

- **Order-to-Cash (O2C)**: The end-to-end business process from order capture through invoicing and revenue recognition. This role owns the Salesforce automation layer that keeps O2C flowing cleanly.
- **Platform Events**: Salesforce's event-driven messaging architecture for real-time integrations between Salesforce and external systems — used here to sync order/billing data across ERP and tax engines.
- **Copado**: A Salesforce-native DevOps platform for CI/CD, change management, and deployment automation — the change-control backbone for this role's builds.
