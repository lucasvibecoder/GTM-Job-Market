## Summary
Every is a subscription media company at the cutting edge of AI. The Growth Engineer owns end-to-end execution of growth optimizations across the conversion funnel — building and shipping data-driven experiments that drive subscriber acquisition, activation, monetization, and retention. Full-stack role working cross-functionally with product GMs, editorial, and design.

## Responsibilities
- Design and run A/B tests to improve sign-ups, ecosystem activation, subscription conversion, and retention
- Build 2-4+ experiments per week across the conversion funnel
- Create the experimentation framework: A/B testing, analytics instrumentation, experiment tracking
- Build systems for targeting, segmentation, and attribution
- Take projects from hypothesis to shipped code to analyzed results
- Work cross-functionally with product GMs, Editorial, Consulting, Product Marketing, Platform, and Support

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 2+ years shipping user-facing features that moved meaningful product or business metrics
- Growth engineering, conversion optimization, or product engineering at startup/high-growth
- Tech-stack agnostic: front-end, back-end, and mobile capable
- Already using AI coding tools to accelerate work
- Data-driven approach blending speed with experimental rigor

### Nice-to-Have
- None explicitly listed

### Application Requirement
Describe an experiment you'd design and ship in your first two weeks: hypothesis, target metric, technical implementation, measurement plan.
