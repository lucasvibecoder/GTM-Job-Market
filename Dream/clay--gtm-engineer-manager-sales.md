## Summary

Clay is the GTM platform powering how companies like Anthropic, Notion, Google, and Ramp go to market — $100M+ revenue, $5B valuation, Series C backed by Sequoia, CapitalG, and First Round. This GTM Engineer Manager (Sales) role leads and scales a team of GTMEs who combine technical expertise with consultative selling to help customers unlock Clay's full potential. Not just managing — architecting how Clay shows up in the most complex, high-stakes GTM motions and coaching GTMEs to become trusted advisors. **Note:** Clay data listed this as "Sales Manager (GTME)" and LinkedIn URL slug shows "gtm-engineer-manager-sales" — the JD body uses "GTM Engineer Manager (Sales)."

## Responsibilities

- Build and develop a high-performing team: recruit, onboard, and coach GTMEs who balance technical depth with consultative selling
- Design scalable enablement systems and foster a culture of curiosity and impact
- Own and scale the GTME playbook: design frameworks, discovery methodologies, demo structures, and qualification criteria
- Balance rigor with flexibility — know when to enforce the playbook and when to empower creativity
- Bridge teams and influence product: partner with Sales leadership, Product, and Customer Success
- Synthesize patterns from complex deals into actionable product feedback
- Be the voice of the field, ensuring the team has enablement and tools to win

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 4+ years leading GTMEs, AEs, Solutions Engineers, Sales Engineers, or similar customer-facing teams
- Proven deal execution experience in Mid-Market/Enterprise B2B sales cycles
- Ability to navigate technical objections and win enterprise deals
- Coaching ability — staying close to deals while building scalable structure

### Nice-to-Have
- Passion for Clay and GTM automation
- Experience redefining how enterprises build their GTM engine

## Key Concepts

- **Negative Maintenance**: One of Clay's operating principles — designing systems and processes that require less upkeep over time rather than more, a philosophy this role applies to building scalable GTME playbooks.
- **Consultative Technical Selling**: The art of combining deep product expertise with strategic advisory skills — not just demo'ing features but transforming how companies think about their entire GTM motion, which is the core coaching mandate of this role.
