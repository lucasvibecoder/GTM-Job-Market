## Summary

Wiz is the fastest-growing cloud security startup, protecting 50%+ of the Fortune 100 and scanning 230 billion files daily. This Lead GTM Systems Engineer is the technical architect and escalation point for the entire Post-Sales and Marketplace ecosystem — owning Planhat (CS), Kantata (PSA), and Tackle.io (marketplace), all integrated into Salesforce Core. You build the automated workflows that let Services and Success teams scale at enterprise level, with data accuracy for Board-level reporting. 8+ YOE, lead-level role with team mentorship responsibilities.

## Responsibilities

- Serve as primary technical owner for the Salesforce-to-Planhat integration — design and maintain data syncs for health scores, usage data, and customer data
- Mentor junior admins and drive operational maturity (SOPs, deployment workflows, technical debt management)
- Partner with cross-functional leadership to translate business requirements into technical roadmaps via Agile delivery
- Design and maintain configurations within Kantata and Tackle.io; establish automation standards bridging Sales and Post-Sales
- Provide expert technical support to Global Customer Success and RevOps teams
- Act as technical advisor for new GTM tooling evaluations, assessing fit into existing architecture
- Establish proactive monitoring for sync errors while ensuring stack security, compliance, and optimization

## Requirements

### Must-Have

- 8+ years as GTM Systems Engineer or Business Systems Administrator in high-growth SaaS, with 2+ years in a lead or manager role
- PSA and CS platform expertise — custom integrations, data mappings, health score logic tied to Salesforce (Planhat and Kantata highly preferred)
- Deep Salesforce Core architecture knowledge including Quote-to-Cash workflows; CPQ or Revenue Cloud (RCA) hands-on experience highly preferred
- API and connectivity skills (REST, SOAP) for custom integrations beyond native connectors
- Automation mindset — fully automated solutions for manual GTM processes, especially Closed Won → Customer Onboarding
- Complex data sync diagnosis across multi-directional platforms maintaining data integrity for Finance and Legal
- Security-first mindset ensuring GTM system architecture remains secure and compliant at scale

## Key Concepts

- **Professional Services Automation (PSA)**: Software platforms (like Kantata) that manage the delivery side of services businesses — resource allocation, project management, time tracking, and billing. In GTM context, PSA bridges the gap between closing a deal and delivering the service, preventing revenue leakage.
- **Quote-to-Cash (QTC)**: The end-to-end business process from product configuration and pricing (CPQ) through contract execution to revenue recognition. Salesforce CPQ and Revenue Cloud automate this pipeline, and this role ensures the data thread continues seamlessly into post-sales systems.
- **Health Score Logic**: Quantitative models in Customer Success platforms (Planhat) that combine usage data, engagement signals, and account metadata to predict churn risk and expansion opportunity — requires accurate bidirectional data sync with Salesforce.
